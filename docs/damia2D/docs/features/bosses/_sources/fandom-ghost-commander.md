The Ghost Commander **leads the troop of Knights that, when living, guarded the newborn Princess Louvia of Mille Seseau**. Now, although **dead, he and his troop still clinge to the world on the Phantom Ship**. The Ghost Commander is **encountered alongside four Ghost Knights**.

## Story

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

When Dart and his group examine the Phantom Ship **after the collision with the Queen Fury**, they decide to examine the ship to unveil its mysteries. They witness a tragedy from the past, as it appears like **the entire crew and the newborn Princess Louvia on board were killed by the Black Monster**.

After Dart and his companions **hear the story of the ship from the captain's soul**, the room on the **Main Deck of the ship** opens, where they are attacked by the Ghost Commander and Ghost Knights, who **take them for the Black Monster**. When **Shana tells the restless Commander after the fight that there is no more Black Monster and that they should not suffer anymore**, he **fades away after a cryptic line, making Shana wonder whether there is something she doesn't know about yet**.

> "You... you are... Thank god. You made it. **Princess Louvia...**" — Ghost Commander

## Battle

While the Ghost Commander or a Ghost Knight is alive on the battlefield, the others will **resurrect after a short while**. **To win the fight, you have to take down the last one before another resuscitates**. Their turns continue **"invisibly" when they are dead**; if one remains on the field, each dead one will arise to fight again, as their **"next turn"**.

These bosses are **much easier if you buy Trans Light and/or Dancing Ray, both available at the Queen Fury's Item store**, due to the fact that **Attack Items are very strong, and Light is the counter to their Element, Darkness**. **Meru and/or Shana will do the most damage with attack items since they have the highest magic attack**. **Shana's Star Children will also do significant damage**.

If you are finding the fight difficult a recommended strategy is to **damage every enemy until the indicator above their head is red then hit them all at once or in quick succession**. If done correctly you will eliminate them together or sequentially.

The attacks are listed below:

- **Attack** : Slashes a party member with its sword.
- **Fading Skull** : The Ghost Commander **has souls forming into him and launches a skull at one party member**, inducing **fear which lowers defense and attack**.
- **Absorb** : The Commander **shoots lights at a single party member dealing moderate damage, also healing himself by the same amount**.
- **Sharing Pain** : The Commander will **stab himself and show a lighter ghost of himself traveling over to one party member and inflicting damage**.
