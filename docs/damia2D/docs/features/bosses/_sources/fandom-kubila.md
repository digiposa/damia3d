## Story

After failing to protect the **Signet Sphere of Aglis**, Dart and his group venture through **Zenebatos, the ancient law city of the Winglies**, and the **legal maze of its bureaucracy**. They arrive at the **Great Court**, where its **Signet Sphere is located**, in order to protect it from Zieg. However, **Nomos, the supreme arbitrator of Zenebatos, is controlled by Zieg, sentences the party to death and summons the three winged executioners Vector, Kubila, and Selebus**.

## Battle

### Moveset and abilities

Out of the three executioners, **Kubila is the one that does most magic damage and notably has a "Can't Combat" spell**.

- **Sickle**: He **moves over to a party member and strikes twice**.
- **Gremlin Summon**: He uses his scythe and **cuts three lines in front of him before he moves away**. The **frame falls down and he summons a monster resembling a lizard that shoots bubbles at all party members**.
- **Philosopher Spell**: He **pulls out his book and casts a random spell that hits either one ally or the whole party**.
- **Judgement of Evil**: He **only uses this move when he or one of the other executioners is about to get killed**. He **calls upon a rock from the skies on to one party member that instantly kills him/her** (Can't Combat).

### Strategy

Due to his Can't Combat spell, it is **recommended that he would be killed first or second** and also have some **Angel's Prayer in hand** for that occasion. He uses the spell, in addition to his normal turns, **when any of the Dark Judges is killed, including himself, on a random party member**. While this attack's effect can be **countered with the Talisman or similar equipment**, since it **targets randomly, all three party members have to be wearing such an item**, in lieu of other benefits, to be completely safe. Because of this, **killing Kubila last will ensure that at least one party member receives no XP for the fight**.
