## Hooded Man

The **Hooded Man is a veiled figure in collaboration with Sandora**.

### Seles raid

As the **raid on Seles comes to a close**, they inform the **Great Commander that it is Emperor Doel's command to take one of the survivors, Shana, into custody**. When the commander asks about her, he is told that **it is not his concern**.

### Hellena Prison confrontation

When the Hooded Man **returns to Hellena Prison**, he is confronted by the **head warden Fruegel**. He tells the warden that the **world's future rests on the survivor they took into custody**. Fruegel is **dissatisfied with the lack of specifics**. The Hooded Man says **nothing more needs to be conveyed, and that those who don't understand should just follow orders**.

Fruegel grows impatient, **proclaiming that only His Majesty, Doel, can order him**. The Hooded Man is unfazed, reminding the warden with a **pointed sword that no harm may come to her or it'll cost more than his head; a message from Doel himself**. Fruegel turns away in frustration.

### Post-jailbreak Hellena

After the **jailbreak at Hellena**, Fruegel is surprised to see the **Hooded Man looking down at him from an upper platform**. He asks the head warden if **His Majesty Doel should be notified, implying Fruegel is on thin ice**. Fruegel insists that he will retrieve Shana. The man says that **otherwise he would be dead by now**. As Fruegel leaves the room, the Hooded Man says it **doesn't matter, that everything is going exactly according to his plan**.

### Indels Castle (Basil)

Later, Dart & company arrive at **Indels Castle**. He asks for advice about the **Black Monster and Shana's safety**, while Lavitz is there to update **King Albert and participate in the war meeting**. The **Hooded Man is present, hired by the king as an advisor for the war**.

Having been **seen in positions of influence for both sides of the war, his motives are unclear**.
