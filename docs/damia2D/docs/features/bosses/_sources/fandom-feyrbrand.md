**Feyrbrand the Green-Tusked Dragon** (緑牙竜フェルブランド, _Ryokugaryū Feruburando_, lit. "Green-Tusked Dragon Felbrand") is the **first of several vassal Dragons** that appear in The Legend of Dragoon. Besides its moniker of the "Green-Tusked Dragon", it is also known for its **poison**.

## Story

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

Feyrbrand was used by **Imperial Sandora** against the rivaling **Kingdom of Basil** in the Serdian War. As the control over the Dragon broke the balance of power, **Emperor Doel started to become more aggressive**. The Dragon was controlled by **Greham, a defector from Basil to Sandora**, who obtained the **Dragoon Spirit of the Jade Dragon**.

### First Encounter

Dart is in a forest when he hears a parade of horses galloping by. He heads up to confront two Sandora Soldiers. Before they are able to attack, **Feyrbrand appears**. Dart is chased deeper into the forest until he is **saved by Rose**. She explains that he was just attacked by a Dragon, but does not understand why they would need a Dragon to destroy the nearby village. Dart says that **the village is his hometown** and runs off to see what happened.

### Second Encounter

After obtaining the power of the Dragoon themselves, Dart and his company eventually find Feyrbrand in the **Nest of Dragon**. At the end of the underground nest they find **Greham, an old friend of Lavitz' father Servi Slambert**, who however betrayed Servi and the Kingdom of Basil and deflected to Sandora. They find out that it has been him who has been controlling the Dragon and so to stop him, they fight and overcome both Greham and Feyrbrand.

### Chapter 4: Moon & Fate

The Dragoons again encounter the **soul of Feyrbrand for the final time at the Death City Mayfil**. **Rose explains** that he may still be in this world **because he had too much pride as a Dragon for having been defeated by humans, unable to die and to find peace**. By **defeating his spirit one more time, his attachment to the world was severed and his soul was presumably freed**.

## Battle at Nest of Dragon

**Feyrbrand is of wind element and as such takes stronger damage from attacking items of earth element, i.e. Pellet and Meteor Fall.** However, attacking him with magical attacks and items is a **double-edged sword**, as his attack power will increase every time when hit with magic damage. Feyrbrand is able to deal significant physical damage with his regular attack, has a lot of HP and can disrupt the player by causing status ailments. **For these reasons, it may be recommendable to defeat Feyrbrand before Greham.**

Feyrbrand uses **three attacks** during the battle:

- **Tusk Attack**: Feyrbrand attacks a party member with his tusks.
- **Slime Shot**: Feyrbrand shoots damaging slime out of his abdomen. Each slime has a different color:
  - **Green slime** inflicts **Poison**
  - **White slime** inflicts **Stun**
  - **Blue slime** inflicts **Fear**
- **Attack Power Up**: Feyrbrand charges up energy to permanently raise its attack power. He **only uses it when he is hit with any magic based attack**.

## Trivia

- ⚠️ **If one looks into the game's script, there is an unused line by Rose at the beginning of the game when Dart is saved by her, in which she suggests that Feyrbrand is blind. This is the reason why Feyrbrand is unable to detect Dart and Rose hiding behind a simple rock.**
- The **Japanese kanji for Feyrbrand's title, Green-Tusked Dragon (緑牙竜, Ryokugaryū), hint at its connection with Imperial Sandora. The character for dragon (竜, ryū) can also be read as "ryō," meaning "imperial."**
