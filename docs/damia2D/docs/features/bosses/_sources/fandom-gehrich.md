Gehrich is the **leader of the Gehrich Gang, a group of bandits terrorizing Tiberoa during chapter 2 of the game**.

---

## Story

He was once **Haschel's student in the Rouge School of Martial Arts**, but was **kicked out twenty years ago for his bad attitude**. Following the trace of the Gehrich Gang to the Home of Gigantos, Dart and the party fight him **inside the main hall**. After the fight, **Princess Emille's uncharacteristically rude behavior** is explained by Gehrich's revelation: **The real princess has been kidnapped and replaced by an imposter**. He **dies shortly after and is entombed by Haschel**.

---

## Battle

He is fought alongside **Mappi**, who also is a significant threat due to his ability to **deal a Can't Combat attack**, so the **Talisman is recommendable**.

Gehrich's attacks are fairly strong. He **cannot use "Double Team" if Mappi is dead**, and **Mappi cannot use "Vanish", if Gehrich is dead**. The **Soul Headband**, which he occasionally drops at 25%, **gives Haschel 5 more DF and two more MAT**, and is **not purchasable until Deningrad**.

- **Swipe** : Gehrich slashes a party member with his knife.
- **Three Step Kick** : Gehrich kick's a party member three times, with the final kick slamming them to the ground.
- **Double Team** : Mappi and Gehrich team up on a party member.
