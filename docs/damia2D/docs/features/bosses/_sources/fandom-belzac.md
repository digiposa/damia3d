### Quote canon

> _"Children...I will realize the freedom you wished, and the future I promised to you. Children. Please watch us."_
>
> — Belzac

**Belzac was one of the original Dragoons during the Dragon Campaign**, wielding the **Dragoon Spirit of the Golden Dragon**.

## Story

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

When **Rose is stranded together with Dart in Lidiera**, she remembers the final battle of the Dragon Campaign. In her memories :

- Belzac **saved Shirley** from a **collapsing roof**, using his **herculean strength** to lift it up.
- However, a **Virage** spotted the two and **pierced Belzac with its claw**, fatally wounding him.
- Shirley, seeing this, promised him that his death would not be in vain, shooting an arrow at the Virage.
- At the same time, the Virage fired its laser beam, **presumably killing the two**.

### Chapter 4: Moon & Fate

When Dart, Rose and the others return to Vellweb to **free the souls of the four Dragoons that died in the Dragon Campaign** :

- Belzac first **mistakes Dart for Zieg**.
- Rose then reveals to Belzac that he is already dead.
- He remembers protecting Shirley from a Virage during the last battle of the Dragon Campaign.
- He goes into a **frenzy of disbelief and denial**, hearing about the death of Shirley.
- **Forces the group to fight**.

Upon his defeat, Belzac remarks that **death is not as sad as he imagined**, and **thanks Rose**.

> Belzac's soul is then freed → goes to **Mayfil**.

## Battle

### Dragoon Addition

Belzac is using the **same Dragoon Additions as Kongol**.

### Dragoon Magic

Belzac is able to use **all the magic Kongol can use** : **Meteor Strike, Grand Stream and Golden Dragon**.

### Strategy

- Like Kongol in his boss fight, he takes a **lot of magical damage** (low MDF makes him weak to magic).
- Dragoon Magic, Attacking Items and equipment that boost Magical Attack are **effective**.
- Like Kongol, his **physical attacks are strong** → **high physical defense is crucial** to withstand his attacks.

## Trivia

- According to **The Legend of Dragoon Official Guidebook**, Belzac was :
  - **Human**
  - **31 years old** at the time of the Dragon Campaign
  - **210 cm (6'11") tall** — géant
- The Guidebook reveals he was of the **royal family of Gloriano** (nation lore).
- **Contradiction canonique non-résolue sur sa mort** :
  - **Cutscene Disc 2** (Rose memory) : Virage pierces him while shielding Shirley + Virage laser kills both.
  - **Forbidden Land scene** (Rose) : "this must be the Virage that killed Belzac" — référence au **Scarred Super Virage**.
  - **Belzac himself (Vellweb)** : "[...] took the attack of Super Virage, with [his] own body...to be the shield for Shirley".
  - **MAIS** cutscene Disc 2 ne montre **pas** Super Virage — c'est **Kanzas qui fight Super Virage et se sacrifie** par self-destruct attack.
  - **Contradiction never resolved in-game**.
