**Imago** is a **three stage non-elemental boss fight** that takes place in the **Divine Tree right before the Dragoons fully reach the Moon That Never Sets**, that lays **within the heart of the Divine Tree**. It awakens after **Kongol's landing shakes it from its slumber** before it **crawls off-screen, only to later confront the team**.

Although itself **No Element**, Imago uses a **Light attack**.

It drops **Moon Serenade, Sun Rhapsody and Healing Rain** after battle.

⚠️ **Story canon ⭐** :

- Divine Tree boss fight pre-Moon That Never Sets (canon location)
- **Moon That Never Sets lays within heart of Divine Tree** canon (cohérent lore Disc 4)
- **Kongol's landing wakes Imago from slumber** canon — Giganto stomp wakes Caterpillar
- **"Crawls off-screen, later confronts team"** narrative canon

⚠️ **Imago Light-element attack canon confirmé** (despite Non-Elemental tag).

## Battle

All three forms have **high stats generally, but they vary somewhat**.

### Caterpillar

**Lower Magic Attack than the final form and the lowest Magic Defense**, otherwise the same as Imago.

### Pupa

**Lower Attack, higher Defense and lower Speed than either of the other two forms**.

### Imago

Imago has the **highest Magic Attack**, and is **tied for highest in every other stat**.

The Imago appears to be a **Harlequin/Jester humanoid looking entity** with **transparent luminescent red and yellow tinted wings that match the color scheme of the main body**. Imago has quite high Stats, and its main attacks, though few, can be painful if characters are not equipped properly against this boss.

⚠️ **Imago appearance canon MAJEUR ⭐** :

- **Harlequin/Jester humanoid** entity canon
- **Transparent luminescent red + yellow tinted wings** canon
- Wings match color scheme main body
- Has head antennas + regular hands (per Infight ability description)
- Pattern thematic "insect adult humanoid Harlequin jester canon" — distinctive design
- À refléter sprite design Damia : humanoid Harlequin transparent red/yellow wings

### Abilities (fandom canon names Imago) ⭐

- **Infight** — Its physical attack, consists of it hovering up towards a teammate, grabbing them by its head antennas and smacking them two or three times with its regular hands before jumping away
- **High Output Laser** — Is magic Light based, and consists of it blasting one of the teammates with a beam of energy
- **Smoke of Despair** — It hovers about, flapping its wings at a party member in hopes of damaging them with either **stun or poison, or inflicting dispirit** ⚠️
- **Triangle Death** — Is a **one-hit KO ability** that consists of the Imago forming a **blue triangle, capturing one of your party members in it, and slamming it into the ground** ⭐

⚠️ **Fandom canon names Imago ⭐ NEW MAJEUR** :
