**Doel** is the **Emperor of Imperial Sandora** : the **southern region of Serdio**. Shortly after **rising to power by killing his brother**, he led the **Serdian army to the Home of Giganto to defend the gigantos from humans**. Although he **failed to prevent the genocide**, he managed to **save the life of Kongol, a giganto child, and brought him back to Serdio** with him. His rule over Serdio was **broken when the northern region declared his nephew, Albert, to be next in line of succession over Doel, despite being only six at the time**. His wife **Katrina died during the war to unknown cause**.
