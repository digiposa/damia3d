## Appearance ⭐⭐⭐ MAJEUR NEW canon

The Divine Dragon bears the design most seen in **western-style dragons** : a **colossal, quadrupedal, winged reptile with elongated neck**. It flies across the sky on **seven wings** and a **long tail** and has a **cannon on its right shoulder** used to unleash massive magical power in an attack called the Divine Dragon Cannon.

After breaking its seal, the Divine Dragon's body remains **shackled**, with **broken chains hanging from its limbs**. The dragon's facial features are unorthodox: an array of **seven eyes**, with **six eyes located at the front of its upper jaw** while the **seventh, largest eye is located on what should be its chin**.

## Battle with the Divine Dragon (Mountain of Mortal Dragon Disc 3)

This battle is fought at the top of the **Mountain of Mortal Dragon**.

Despite being **drastically weakened by the Dragon Block Staff**, the Divine Dragon is still a formidable foe. As Dragoon forms are rendered **near-useless** by the Dragon Block Staff, the player has to fight it **almost exclusively in the regular forms** of the characters. Characters with **good magical defense are recommended**, as the Divine Dragon uses **devastating magical spells**. It is **strongly recommended to disable the Divine Dragon Ball and Divine Dragon Cannon first**, as these are extremely powerful and able to knock down the majority of the targeted characters' HP. Having **healing items** as well as **Mind Purifiers** is recommended as well, and **Bravery Amulets can be useful to prevent the Divine Dragon from inflicting Fear**.

As the effects of the Dragon Block Staff are **permanent in the battle**, Dragoons should be used for a **maximum of one round** and only for **supportive Dragoon Magic Spells** (e.g. **Albert's Rose Storm**, **Meru's Rainbow Breath** or **Miranda's healing spells**). Attacking as a Dragoon is near-useless and Dragoons are much more vulnerable than the regular forms of the characters. Should the player desire to use Dragoon forms in the battle, it is greatly recommended to use up all the SP before the battle with the Divine Dragon in random encounters, and to transform before the character accumulates 200 SP.

⚠️ **Strategy canon Divine Dragon ⭐⭐⭐ MAJEUR (fandom)** :

- **Dragon Block Staff permanent battle** canon — cannot disable
- **Dragoon forms near-useless** canon (0.4× dmg dealt + 4× dmg received per wiki)
- **High MDF recommended** (Dragon uses devastating magical spells)
- **Disable Divine Ball + Divine Cannon first** canon (Final Blow optional Boss Extras)
- ⭐⭐ **Bravery Amulet NEW item canon** anti-Fear accessory (vs Divine Dragon Pressure ability Fear)
- ⭐ **Mind Purifiers** + healing items recommended
- ⭐ **Dragoon usage limited 1 round** + supportive spells only canon :
