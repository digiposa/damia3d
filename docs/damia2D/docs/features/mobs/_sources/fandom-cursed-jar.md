### General Strategy

To consistently defeat Cursed Jar, it is **required to at least have a Magic Stone of Signet in the inventory**, although other Repeat Items such as **Speed Down** and **Speed Up** are useful as well. Cursed Jar should be attacked for 1 or a maximum of 2 damage, then the **Magic Stone of Signet should be used**. After 00PARTS has been blocked, the player should continue attacking. Cursed Jar has only 4 HP, but each character only attacks for 1 damage point.
