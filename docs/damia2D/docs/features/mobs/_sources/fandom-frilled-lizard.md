## Appearance

Its appearance is similar to that of a **blue human-sized velociraptor with reddish skin frills around the neck**. It has **two small clawed arms** but attacks **relying on its four mandibles**.

---

## Battle

Main article: Battle

Until it is reduced to **33 or less HP**, this creature deals medium damage, nothing beyond average at this point. Once reduced to **red health, it uses the spell item Pellet** and deals **slightly more than medium earth damage to a single target**. With low health and defence, it should require only one more hit to finish it off, so **do not Guard in response to Pellet**.

- **Headbutt** — Runs towards a single target and bites them dealing medium physical damage.
- **Pellet** — Deals medium to high earth damage upon a single target, depending on level and armor.
- **Escape** — This creature will have a chance to flee the fight once its health reaches critical.

Chance to evade, **with 120% A-AV**. ⚠️ (impossible mechanic)

## Drops

Frilled lizards can drop **two items, the Healing Potion and the spell item Meteor Fall**, both of which have the same uncommon drop rate of 8%. The potions can be helpful as always, even **meteor fall can be helpful for when you get to the Valley of Corrupted gravity**. They are not actually too annoying to get due to the frequency with which frilled lizard appears in combat, so without extra effort you should be able to get something just passing through the Barrens. Although as always, it varies from person to person and how lucky they are.
