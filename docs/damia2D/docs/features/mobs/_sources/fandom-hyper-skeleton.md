**Hyper Skeleton is a darkness-element based monster of which is located within The Death City, Mayfil**. It **can cast multiple Status Ailments**. It has a **rare chance to drop the Spear of Terror, a weapon available to buy much earlier in Deningrad**.

It **resembles a skeletal Centaur clad in heavy armor**.

---

## Battle

**Immune to erase such as Total Vanishing and Can't Combat effects, and all other Status Ailments as well**.

- **Galloping Strike**
- **Midnight Terror** — Casts **Fear** on one ally
- **Panic Bell** — Casts **Confuse** on one ally
- **Poison Needle** — Casts **Poison** on one ally
- **Stunning Hammer** — Casts **Stun** on one ally
