## Appearance

Guillotine is an **apparatus designed for beheading people**: this particular contraption has **wheels** and is **animated by dark magic or due to an evil spirit**. On the top of this object, a **heavy and large blade rests, prepared to fall upon its next victim**. This guillotine has **several spikes along each side and many on the uppermost rim**. **Several chains can be seen hanging off this structure**. A **horned skeleton-like torso is fused into the top of the guillotine and launches the blade onto its foes**.

## Battle

> Main article: Battle

This monsters stats are **mostly the highest in this location**. This monsters physical damage is **fairly potent**, yet once its health has dropped below half way, it will then **use Midnight Terror often**. This move will cast **Fear upon all targets simultaneously** and that can then affect the tide of battle even if it is alone since it will strike hard then. They are **weaker towards magical damage rather than physical damage** so try and use **Light-element spell items if available to get the elemental weakness bonus damage added**. Due to it not being a standard living monster and instead a **construct with an undead torso attached to it**, all status ailments are impossible to hit.

- **Guile Edge** — Rolls towards an individual target, smashing down upon them with its blade dealing high physical damage
- **Midnight Terror** — A given probability to inflict all targets with Fear upon hit

This monster is **immune to all Status Ailments**.

## Drops

Upon defeat, this monster may drop the **healing item Healing Breeze with a rare probability of 8%**. This healing item can be very useful since **Zenebatos opponents deal plenty enough damage and two can perform Can't Combat**. They **can be bought for 30 gold**. The **average time to obtain this is in roughly 15 minutes**.
