**Goblin** (**ゴブリン, Goburin**) is a **Fire based monster that is found in the Forest**. It has the **uncommonly low Speed of 40, with enemies averaging 60**.

---

## Appearance

The goblin is a **small humanoid enemy with green skin and red eyes**. The goblin **wears a brown tunic and carries a club made of bone**.

---

## Battle

In combat this enemy **focuses only on single targets**. It sometimes drops a **Detonate Rock** after battle.

- **Kick** : runs up to the target and **kicks them for minor damage**
- **Bone Club** : runs up and hits the target with its club, **dealing more damage than the Kick attack**
- **Rock Throw** : The goblin **chucks a rock at the target**
