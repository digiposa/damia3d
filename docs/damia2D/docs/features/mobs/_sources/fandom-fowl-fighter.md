Fowl Fighter appears in **Hellena Prison during the second visit**. This enemy is **only found in a couple of battles with the Hellena Warden**. This enemy is of the Fire element.

It has **high Magic Defense and is Fire, so the spells Dart has acquired since his last visit are not recommended**, especially since a **Boss battle is coming up**.

---

## Appearance

The Fowl Fighter is a **stronger recolored version of the Assassin Cock with Red and Black for its color**.

---

## Battle

This enemy appears with **Hellena Wardens**, on the **final ascent to Fruegel**.

- **Feet Sickle** — The Fowl Fighter runs up and slashes the target
- **Double Feet Sickle** — The Fowl Fighter runs up and slashes the target with both feet
- **Rooster Crow** — The Fowl Fighter crows causing a sound wave. **This attack is magical in nature and can cause Fear**
