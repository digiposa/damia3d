**Gnome** is an **Earth element creature only located within the Forbidden Land, Kadessa**.

---

## Appearance

Gnome is a **very short humanoid with darker skin and an orange pair of what look like overalls and a pointed hat**. He **wields a pile of straw; the ensemble might call to mind a scarecrow**. **Creatures here might be drawn towards the energy of the Dragon Block Staff**.

---

## Battle

These creatures deal **medium to high damage with either physical or magical damage**, and luckily are **slow to attack so you will almost undoubtedly go first**. They can **Stun a single target on hit with one of their spell items** which is slightly annoying, however, **it does no magic damage**. They shouldn't be much of a threat to players unless severely under leveled or equipped with old armors.

- **Straw Whip** : Walks towards a single target and **whips them with its bundle of straw**, dealing medium damage.
- **Pellet** : Deals **medium earth element damage** towards a single target. **Can cast at 50% (Amber) HP or higher**.
- **Stunning Hammer** : **Casts red beams towards a single target dealing no damage, however, inflicting Stun upon hit**.
