### Trivia canon

- **Their model is a recolor of the Fowl Fighter encountered in Hellena Prison.**
- ⚠️ **Given its name, the blades affixed to its feet, and its resemblance to the Fowl Fighter, this bird is possibly also trained by Sandora. If so, it is likely that Assassin Cocks and Fowl Fighters belong to the same species and are distinguished only by the type of training they have received.**
