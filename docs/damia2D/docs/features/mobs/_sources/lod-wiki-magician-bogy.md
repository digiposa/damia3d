Magician Bogy is a Minor Enemy. They are only found in limited number on the Phantom Ship formerly known as the Saint Louvia. After the ship crashes into the Queen Fury, a Bogy observes Dart and company searching the area. A small group of them toy with Dart, eventually engaging his party in battle. In total, four of them are encountered.
