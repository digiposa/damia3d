**Aqua King is a water-element based monster of which is located within The Magic City, Aglis.**

## Appearance

Aqua King appears as some sort of **merfolk, wielding a trident**. It is the **much more defensive cousin to Merman**. This humanoid has several fins sticking out from all over its body and gills all over. This monster's **eyes are yellow** and skin is a type of **red** with but two exceptions being its **underbelly and its fins which are both silver**.

## Battle

You should note immediately that this monster **cannot attack**; although it has a move called Stab, **it will never use that**. Instead it will only use both **Physical Attack Barrier** and **Magical Attack Barrier**. **It cannot have both active simultaneously**. So be prepared to combat with both physical and magical attacks, or wait for it to cast the other type of Barrier or the third ability it uses: **Power Up**. Power Up **increases DF and MDF and AT and MAT**.

An enemy that cannot attack sounds great, however, an added benefit to this opponent is that you can **grind your Additions on it once it enters a physical attack barrier which it does most often**. Although it takes patience and time, it could be worth it especially if you defeat any other enemies with it, after all even some such as **Spider Urchin could still attack back**. However, with their **70 Speed, expect them to be able to change damage immunities much more rapidly than Kongol and Albert can keep up with, without Bandit's Shoes and Bandit's Ring**.

### Abilities (fandom canon names)

- **Stab** — Actually, it **cannot attack**.
- **Physical Attack Barrier** — Becomes immune to all physical attacks for the duration of a turn.
- **Magical Attack Barrier** — Becomes immune to all magical attacks for the duration of a turn.
- **Power Up** — Increases both the physical and magical attack power as well as defence of itself for the duration of **three turns**.

Encounter rate: **Uncommon**

All battle formations of which you will encounter an Aqua King:

## Drop

Upon defeat, this monster may drop the healing item **Angel's Prayer with a rare probability of 8%**. This healing item is for obvious reasons very useful, although grinding to obtain it if you are need of it will only be counter-productive. **This item can be purchased for 30 gold**. The average amount of time to obtain this item is **roughly 15 minutes**. As always, this varies per person.
