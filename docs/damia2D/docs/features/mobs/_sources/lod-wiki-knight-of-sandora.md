### Counter Opportunities (0)

**Knight of Sandora is a Minor Enemy**. They are **first encountered briefly occupying Seles**, and are **later found patrolling the entrance and interior of the Black Castle in Kazas**. Like most of the fighting force wielded by Sandora, the **knighthood prefer one sided plate armor that protects their front facing limbs and chest, but leaves their back exposed**. In Seles, **Dart confronts a small squad of knights overseen by a Commander**.

## Trivia

- Their **models are recolors of Sandora Soldier's models located in Hoax and Marshland**.
- The **existence of both Knight of Sandora and Sandora Soldier implies that knights are not just a generic fighting force**. However, it is **not clear what sort of training, armament, awards, or other qualities distinguishes a knight from a common foot soldier**.
- **Armor protecting only the front may have been to discourage deserting a battle, as a bare back is an easy target for archers**. Alternatively, it may have been **due to the economic hardship that gripped Sandora**.
