## Battle

Cacti are very **straight forward enemies by not having any magical attacks or the capability to inflict Status Ailments**. They are plentiful here and their **average damage adds up** so do not take them too lightly, even if you can vanquish them in a hit. Remember, the nearest safe spot, and shop are far apart from you and each other so conserving yourself is crucial here. **Guard will be very effective against these**.

⚠️ **Strategy canon ⭐** :

- **No magical attacks** canon — Cactus pure physical mob (MAT 75 unused offensive)
- **No Status Ailments inflict** capability — pure damage dealer
- **Average damage adds up** = pattern Cactus ×2 formations dangerous when paired
- **Guard effective** = mitigation strategy canon (cohérent Bowling "Defend Charging Spirit" pattern)
- Pattern Death Frontier safe spot/shop far apart → conserving HP critical

## Drops

Upon defeat, this monster may drop the **healing item Recovery Ball** with an **uncommon probability of 15%**. This item can be useful but if you are in need of specific potions due to enemies being relentless, this is probably not recommended because then you would be playing the game of risk. They are **fairly common though, taking averagely 10 minutes to obtain**. As always, this varies per person.
