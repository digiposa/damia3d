## Second Battle (context Guftas)

The second time you encounter Fruegel is **after returning to Hellena Prison to rescue King Albert**. During this encounter he'll **fight alongside his pets, Rodriguez and Guftas**. Fruegel roughly has the same attack pattern as before, albeit much stronger and **will also issue unique commands to his pets as long as they're still in battle**.

Fruegel can use a variety of attacks including:

- **Attack** : Fruegel hits a party member with his club.
- **Throat Chop** : Fruegel chops a party member a few times then knocks them to the ground.
- **Rock Throw** : Fruegel throws a large rock at a party member.
- **Power Up** : Fruegel will use this after being hit with magic, increasing his offenses and defenses by 50%.
- **Command** : Fruegel will order Guftas or Rodriguez to do a unique attack.

### Rodriguez

**Rodriguez is Fruegel's pet bird**.

- **Feather Shot** : Rodriguez throws sharp feathers from its wings to harm a party member.
- **Aerial Attack** : When called by his master to attack, he uses an aerial drop attack on one party member, which causes considerable damage.

---

### Guftas

**Guftas is Fruegel's pet dog**. **He wears a helmet with a spike on top of it**.

- **Bite** : Guftas runs up to a party member, puts his front legs on their shoulders, and bites to harm a party member.
- **Howl** : When called by his master, he unleashes a loud howl to deal confusion to a party member.

## Strategy

This fight can be a lot easier than his first encounter if you are prepared. If you saved the **Total Vanishing items you could obtain from the Prairie and Limestone Cave**, you can use them on the pets to deal with them instantly. **The game does not recognize Guftas and Rodriguez as boss monsters and therefore they are not immune to Total Vanishing**. **Rose's Demon's Gate** will have the same effect and can be done in one turn if you've managed to learn it by this point (which is unlikely, but possible in theory). Alternatively, and arguably a less optimal route, you could ignore Guftas and Rodriguez altogether and use **Spinning Gale on Fruegel** with all three members. **Once he's defeated, his pets will flee**. It's advised to deal with the pets first if you're able to quickly defeat them because with all three of them alive, they attack much more frequently and will easily whittle down your team. Whether you deal with the pets or not, **using Spinning Gales on Fruegel with all party members will deal significant damage and end the fight soon**.
