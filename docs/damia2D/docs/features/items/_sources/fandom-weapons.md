## Dart

Dart's weapon of choice are **two-handed swords**.

| Weapon        | AT  | Buy | Sell | Location                                              | Effect                               |
| ------------- | --- | --- | ---- | ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Broad Sword   | 2   | -   | 10   | Initial, Hellena Prison (Second Visit), Skeleton (2%) | -                                    |
| Bastard Sword | 7   | 60  | 30   | Limestone Cave, Bale, Mr. Bone (2%)                   | -                                    |
| Heat Blade    | 18  | 150 | 75   | Kazas, Kashua Glacier                                 | Fire elemental damage                |
| Falchion      | 26  | 250 | 125  | Fueno                                                 | -                                    |
| Mind Crush    | 34  | 350 | 175  | Forbidden Land, Kashua Glacier                        | Chance to cause Confusion            |
| Fairy Sword   | 39  | 400 | 200  | Ulara                                                 | Generates 50% more SP when attacking |
| Claymore      | 44  | 500 | 250  | Moon                                                  | -                                    |
| Soul Eater    | 75  | -   | 225  | Polter Armor (100%), Loner Knight (2%)                | Lose 10% HP each turn                |

The **Heat Blade** is Dart's Elemental Weapon and can prove itself useful in many stages of the game, despite not being the strongest weapon available in terms of its AT stat. It will deal **double damage** to Lenus, Regole, and many enemies in the Undersea Cavern and the Kashua Glacier including Windigo and often surpass the damage dealt with stronger weapons without an element, such as the Falchion or Mind Crush.

The **Mind Crush** can be useful to defeat Unique Monsters, should the player be able to deal Confusion to the enemy. However, Confusion can also make an enemy run away randomly. The **Fairy Sword** is very useful to grind SP, although at the time it is available, there are chances that Dart has already attained Dragoon Level 5.

Lastly, the **Soul Eater** is extremely powerful, but makes Dart lose 10% of his HP each turn. This effect can be neutralized by equipping Dart with the **Therapy Ring**.

## Shana/Miranda

Shana's and Miranda's weapon of choice are **bows and arrows**.

| Weapon         | AT  | Buy | Sell | Location                  | Effect                               |
| -------------- | --- | --- | ---- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Short Bow      | 3   | -   | 10   | Initial                   | -                                    |
| Sparkle Arrow  | 9   | 50  | 25   | Bale                      | Light elemental damage               |
| Long Bow       | 18  | 150 | 75   | Kazas                     | +10 to A-HIT (Attack Hit)            |
| Bemusing Arrow | 24  | 250 | 125  | Fueno, Arrow Shooter (2%) | Chance to cause Confusion            |
| Virulent Arrow | 30  | 350 | 175  | Kashua Glacier            | Chance to cause Poison               |
| Arrow of Force | 40  | 500 | 250  | Ulara                     | Generates 50% more SP when attacking |
| Detonate Arrow | 50  | -   | -    | Moon                      | Attacks all enemies                  |

Shana's/Miranda's Elemental Weapon is the **Sparkle Arrow**. Despite its weak AT stat, it can be of use in certain battles against Darkness element enemies, such as on the Phantom Ship.

The **Long Bow** is useful when fighting Unique Monsters due to its improved accuracy, and the fact that **the AT stat does not matter against these monsters, as they will always take 1 damage when hit**. The **Virulent Arrow** can also be useful against Unique Monsters, especially the Lucky Jar.

The **Arrow of Force** is useful for grinding SP and improving Dragoon Levels if required, and the **Detonate Arrow** —only found right before the final two battles of the game— is essentially an attack with the **Detonate Rock** Attack Item.

| Weapon         | AT  | Buy | Sell | Location                  | Effect                 |

Rose's Elemental Weapon, the **Shadow Cutter**, is arguably less useful than some other elemental weapons, as there is only a small amount of Light Element enemies in the game. However, Rose can inflict some Status Ailments such as Fear and Stun with the Demon Stiletto or the Flamberge, which can make some regular encounters easier. The **Dragon Buster** is the most powerful weapon in the game, but obtained only right before the final battle of the game.

## Haschel

As a martial artist, Haschel uses his fists and feet in battle, enhanced by knuckles and claws as weapons.

| Weapon         | AT  | Buy | Sell | Location                                  | Effect                             |
| -------------- | --- | --- | ---- | ----------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Iron Knuckle   | 20  | -   | 50   | Initial                                   | -                                  |
| Beast Fang     | 31  | 250 | 125  | Lizard Man (2%), Black Castle, Queen Fury | Chance to cause Stun               |
| Diamond Claw   | 37  | 300 | 150  | Mappi (Home of Gigantos) (25%), Deningrad | -                                  |
| Brass Knuckle  | 43  | -   | 175  | Windigo (100%)                            | Chance of Can't Combat             |
| Thunder Fist   | 49  | 450 | 225  | Ulara                                     | Thunder elemental damage           |
| Destroyer Mace | 55  | 500 | 250  | Moon                                      | Greater damage as health decreases |

As no other element is weak to Thunder, using the **Thunder Fist** has no practical benefits and the drawback of doing less damage enemies of the Thunder Element.

The **Destroyer Mace** has the unique property of multiplying the damage dealt depending on Haschel's HP. At full health, Haschel will deal 100% damage. When his HP fall below half of the maximum (yellow color in battle) it increases to **150%**. At critical (red) HP of less than 25%, the Destroyer Mace deals **200%**, or twice the amount of damage. **It is the only weapon that allows to deal more than 1 damage to Unique Monsters.**

**Meru has no Elemental Weapon.**
