---

Equipment covers five types of armed items which modify stats and sometimes have other effects, namley: weapons, headwear, body armor, footwear, and accessories. A party member can only have one of each type equipped at a time, with many of them being further restricted to specific party members. Only 255 may be held within the inventory at a time. Most Armed items can be bought in weapon shops, but a rare few are only attainable via treasure chests or from defeating enemies. Equipment may be viewed, sorted, and discarded at any time from the Inventory option on the System Screen, while the Equipment option must be used to actually equip the items. It is impossible to change equipment during battle.
