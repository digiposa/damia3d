## Quote opening canon (Doel à Albert)

> "You are here to find out the truth about whether I assassinated your father, my brother, and the reason. I would say...it was to save Serdio from an incompetent leader named Carlo."
> — Doel to Albert

## Description canon

**King Carlo of Serdio** was the **king of unified Serdio** before the **Serdian Civil War**.

He was the **father of Albert**, who was crowned the King of Serdio following Carlo's death at the hands of his brother **Doel 20 years before the events of the game**. Doel, however, refusing to accept the **then-six-year-old Albert** to become the next king, **seceded and founded Imperial Sandora** in the **south of Serdio**, leading to the division of the country and to the **Serdian War**. Carlo's son, Albert, became **King of the Kingdom of Basil in the north** of the country.

## Doel's view canon

**Doel's response** when Albert confronts Doel in the **Moon that Never Sets**, asking him for his treason :

> "save Serdio from an incompetent leader named Carlo"

Doel points out :

- **Overtaxation**
- **Disorder**
- **Corrupt ministers**

under the reign of Carlo. Albert replies that the King was **loved by his people and his vassals**.

## Albert's view canon ⭐ NEW MAJEUR (Disc 4 reveal)

During the **duel between Albert and Doel in the Moon that Never Sets**, Albert reveals that Carlo was :

- **In deep respect and trustful of Doel** ⭐
- Had **doubts about himself not having the stature of a king** ⭐

Quote canon Albert :

> "I'm different from you and I am not alone. My friends have always been at my side during the many occasions when I was dispirited. I've overcome difficulties. When I was young, **my father would often say that he didn't quite have the stature to be King. Without Doel, he couldn't do anything**. He also said that **it is you who was most likely to take the throne**. My father trusted in you. **If you had gotten together and governed the country, this tragedy would never have happened**."
