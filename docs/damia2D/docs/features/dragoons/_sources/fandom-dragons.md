**Dragons are the 105th species that originated from the Divine Tree.**

## History

Dragons, along with humans, were among the **species oppressed by the Winglies in the ancient times**. Eventually, **humans gained the ability to use dragons as living weapons** against their oppressors. It is assumed that this is **where the name of the Dragon Campaign**, the great liberation war of humans and other oppressed races against Winglies, comes from.

> **Dragons are the second most powerful species of all 107 creatures given life by the Divine Tree. Their attacks and life power overwhelm others, however, the creator Soa created a balance by reducing the dragon's intelligence. Therefore, they were to be controlled by Humans with their wisdom. During the Dragon Campaign 11,000 years ago, they had fierce battles against Virages manipulated by Winglies and couldn't avoid being annihilated.**
> — **Albert quoting from a book in the Mille Seseau National Library**

In opposition to humans using the power of Dragons, the Winglies created **two items meant to be used against dragons** :

- **Dragon Buster**, an **ethereal sword designed to kill Dragons**
- **Dragon Block Staff**, an **elaborate staff crafted to reduce the Dragons' formidable power**

## Dragoons

> Main article: Dragoon

Humans that **obtained and merged with the power of Dragons became known as Dragoons**. The power of the Dragoons is drawn from a **crystalloid gem called a Dragoon Spirit**.

Although it is **never fully explained how Dragoon Spirits are created**, events during Chapter 3 and Chapter 4 show that **during the death of a Dragon, its soul can manifest itself in the form of a Dragoon Spirit**.

This is shown when :

- At the time of the **Divine Dragon's death, its soul and power is transferred into its Dragoon Spirit**
- In a **flashback from Rose, she sacrifices the Darkness Dragon to obtain its Dragoon Spirit**

In the **Dragon Campaign, seven Dragoons were sent out by Emperor Diaz** and **spearheaded the liberation war against the Winglies**.

### Feyrbrand

> _Feyrbrand — Feyrbrand, the Green-Tusked Dragon._
> Main article: Feyrbrand

As the **vassal of the Jade Dragoon Greham**, the might of Feyrbrand **broke the balance of power in the Serdian War in Sandora's favor** and caused the re-ignition of the war. He has a **green body and giant tusks** which makes him known as the **"Green Tusked Dragon"**. His body is shaped similar to a **praying mantis**.

### Regole

> _Regole — Regole, the Sea Dragon._
> Main article: Regole

The **vassal of the Blue Sea Dragoon Lenus**. His body is shaped like a **giant sea-serpent**.

### Michael

> _Black Burst Dragon, Michael — The Black Burst Dragon, Michael._
> Main article: Michael

The **"Black Burst Dragon"**, **Michael was named by Rose as a newborn Dragon** and fought in the Dragon Campaign. He is shaped like a **pterodactyl**.

However, **over the centuries, he eventually became savage and uncontrollable** and **attacked Rose**, who in response **had to kill him**.

### Divine Dragon

> _Divine dragon — The Divine Dragon._
> Main article: Divine Dragon

The **king and the strongest of the Dragons and even possibly the mightiest lifeform that ever existed**, he possess incredible magical power. He is shaped like a normal dragon but has a **number of eyes upon his head, with the largest one actually at the front of his lower jaw**. He has **seven wings**.

He was the **only dragon who fought the Winglies on his own before being sealed inside the Mountain of Mortal Dragon without allying himself with the humans**.

## Dragons of the Dragoon Spirits

When a playable character reaches **Dragoon Level 5**, they are able to **summon the Dragon of their Dragoon Spirit to perform a mighty attack**. In these Dragoon magic spells, the **Dragons manifest themselves physically**.

### Red-Eyed Dragon

The Dragon of the **fire** element that originally chose **Zieg Feld** as his master. His body is shaped like a **missile warhead with 6 eyes**. His attack consists of **charging towards the enemy and exploding like a real missile on impact**.

### Jade Dragon

The Dragon of the **wind** element that chose **Syuveil** as his master. He resembles a **giant whale with 4 wings or fins and has a cannon on his head**. His attack consists of **firing the cannon and causing a giant gust of wind**.

### Violet Dragon

The Dragon of the **thunder** element whose spirit was originally used by **Kanzas**. His body is like a **giant stag beetle with giant pincers and claws**. He destroys his foes with an **electric tackle and causes a massive discharge**.

### Dark Dragon

The Dragon of the **darkness** element that was **sacrificed by Rose** to draw the Dragoon Spirit from it. His **asymmetric body is shaped similar to a space shuttle**. He **absorbs opponents into the darkness, then charges through, creating a light trace and eliminating them**.

### White-Silver Dragon

The Dragon of the **light** element whose spirit was originally used by **Shirley** in the Dragon Campaign. His body is shaped like a **spaceship**. He **descends down from the sky and fires a laser to bring judgment to enemies, and heals the entire party**.

### Blue Sea Dragon

The Dragon of the **water** element granted his spirit to **Damia**. His body is shaped like a **giant flying fish**. He annihilates his enemies by **freezing the ground and charges at them from below, causing tidal waves, and then swallows them inside a whirlpool**.

### Golden Dragon

The Dragon of the **earth** element gave **Belzac** the power of the Dragoon. His form is like a **giant armadillo with a cannon on his right side**. He destroys enemies by **sucking them into quicksand, then blowing his cannon into the sky**.

## Trivia

- Upon reading about Dragons in the Mille Seseau National Library, **Dart notices that the book claiming that Dragons "couldn't avoid being annihilated" is wrong**, since the group encountered living Dragons.
- Said book claims that Dragons are the **second most powerful species** that originated from the Divine Tree. It is not revealed which species is considered the most powerful. **Implications being that the Virage Embryo is the strongest species**.
- The **Darkness Dragon is the only dragon seen sacrificed for a dragoon**. Despite the flashback, it is **never fully explained how and under which circumstances a Dragon's soul manifests itself in a Dragoon Spirit**.
- Due to the mystery mentioned before and the seemingly indestructible nature of Dragoon Spirits, **there is speculation that there are immortal dragons (the Dragons that live on in the Dragoon Spirits) and mortal ones (hence "Mountain of Mortal Dragon")**.
- In the **Black Castle**, the **Magical Researchers** speak of **including a Dragon into their research to obtain their magic**, much to Dart's surprise. In a **room in the basement, the corpse of a Dragon can be seen, suspended in the air**. There is speculation whether **considering its color, the color of the magic oil that powers the lifts, and the observation that all other Dragoons can summon and control at least one Dragon, Emperor Doel sacrificed his Dragon for magical research**. Another unconfirmed theory is that the creature is actually the **corpse of the slain Feyrbrand**.
