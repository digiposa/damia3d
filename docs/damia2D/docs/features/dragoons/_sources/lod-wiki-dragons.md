# Dragon

**Dragons are creatures with great destructive capability and magical prowess.** They were common before the eponymous **Dragon Campaign**, where they became the answer to **Wingly oppression**. **Thought to be extinct, some of them have survived.**

## History

Dragons are the **105th fruit of the Divine Tree**, possessing great destructive power. About **11,682 years ago**, their importance grew dramatically. Winglies were **subjugating many species**, including humans and Dragons. Eventually, Dragons became part of a **bloody uprising**. **Humans had discovered that when a Dragon dies, they leave behind a magical gem which can be harnessed and used to don some of their abilities forever afterward**. This gave humans a new power to rival the immense magical capabilities of the Winglies. Presumably, **many Dragons were lost in the ensuing war known as the Dragon Campaign**, as they are thought to be extinct in the present.

## Present

There are **only a handful of known Dragons left** : **Feyrbrand, Regole, and the Divine Dragon**.

## List of Known Dragons

### Past

#### Red-Eyed Dragon

Though its **eyes are actually green**, the Red-Eyed Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Fire element**.

#### Jade Dragon

The Jade Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Wind element**.

#### White Silver Dragon

The White Silver Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Light element**.

#### Darkness Dragon

The Darkness Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Darkness element**.

#### Violet Dragon

The Violet Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Thunder element**.

#### Blue Sea Dragon

The Blue Sea Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Water element**.

#### Golden Dragon

The Golden Dragon is a **six-eyed Dragon of the Earth element**.

#### Michael

The **vassal Dragon of Rose**, Michael was a **five-eyed Dragon of the Darkness element**. They fought in the **Dragon Campaign against Winglies and Virage**.

#### Unnamed Dragon

Little is known about this Dragon. It had **seven eyes** and **fought the Divine Dragon in battle, which it was defeated by**.

### Present

#### Feyrbrand

Seen in **Serdio**, Feyrbrand is **used by Sandora to gain a military advantage over the kingdom of Basil**. It is seen moving through a forest alongside some Sandoran soldiers.

#### Regole

Regole is a **sea Dragon whose home appears to be the Undersea Cavern**. It has struck fear in the residents of nearby villages around **Suncrest Island**.

#### Divine Dragon

With a famous reputation as the **king of Dragons**, the Divine Dragon was **sealed away by Winglies thousands of years ago**. Recently, the seal faded, allowing the Divine Dragon to break free and wreak havoc on a nearby human capital.

#### Deceased Dragon

A **Dragon's carcass can be found within the Black Castle, being harvested for its magical energy.**

## Appearance

Dragons come in a **wide variety of shapes and sizes**. The **eyes are the prime similarity** between them. Their shapes can vary greatly, appearing as **empowered versions of normal creatures like a mantis, beetle, or fish**. Some even look **otherworldly, with strange propulsion on their backsides**.

## Characteristics

The abilities of any given Dragon vary wildly, made similar only by their **eyes, magical properties, and thus their elemental attribute**. **Many, but not all, can fly.**

## Hierarchy

All Dragons born to the world have **two traits that determine where they fit in a hierarchy** :

1. Each Dragon is of a **specific element**
2. Each Dragon has **between 1-7 eyes**

The **eye count determines how much power a Dragon has**, as well as **whether they rule or are subservient to other Dragons**. This eye count is important even when **Dragons die to create Dragoon Spirits**.

**Dragoons also factor into this hierarchy**, though it is only one-way. **Dragoons are able to command Dragons** with certain restrictions :

- **First, the element must match.**
- **Second, the Dragoon Spirit of the Dragoon must have come from a Dragon with at least one more eye than the target living Dragon.**

For example, **Rose's Dragoon Spirit came from a six-eyed Dragon**. Therefore, she can **command any Darkness-element Dragon with five or less eyes**. **Michael, Rose's vassal Dragon from the Dragon Campaign, meets this criteria.**
