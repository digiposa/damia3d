The Dragon Campaign was a **great liberation war of the ancient times in which Humans fought against Winglies to liberate themselves from oppression over 11,000 years before the events of The Legend of Dragoon**.

## The War for Freedom

In the ancient days, the **Winglies, the species born from the 107th fruit of the Divine Tree, ruled over Endiness and over all other races**. With their **ability to fly and their powerful magical abilities**, they lived in **cities and fortresses floating in the sky** and oppressing all resistance under the **leadership of their dictator Melbu Frahma**.

> _Legend_of_Dragoon_-_Cutscene_8_-_History_of_the_Dragoon_Campaign — Librarian Ute sharing his knowledge about the Dragon Campaign._

Under the command of **Emperor Diaz of Gloriano**, humans declared war to the winglies and fought for their freedom, with their declaration originating in **Fort Magrad**. Humans later gained the ability to use the strength of the Dragons, the **105th race from the Divine Tree**, to their advantage by sealing and using their power in the form of **Dragoon Spirits**. **Seven Dragoon warriors (Zieg, Rose, Belzac, Kanzas, Damia, Syuveil, and Shirley) spearheaded the Dragon Campaign**, participating in the devastating conflict fought with the Winglies in a struggle to decide the fate of Endiness. Eventually, at the **price of the lives of many humans and five of the original seven Dragoons**, humans turned out victorious and became the dominating species on Endiness, **living in relative peace for a long time**.

> **There were 7 Dragoon warriors in the legend of the Dragon Campaign, dragoons reputed to be the incarnations of Dragons. With Dragons at their side, the dragoons fought boldy. However, in the age when the legend was reality, the Winglies dominated all, even the gods. The gods answered to dictator Melbu Frahma's prayers and granted him ultimate power; but, the power was abused. The earth of Saint Imperial Gloriano was scorched by the gods' fire and became ashen. The anger and sadness of the people was the fuel for the dragoons' cause. Then at last, they freed the gods. Without the gods' power, the Winglies perished and our age, the Human Age, began. Thereafter, the gods floated in the sky. For 11,000 years, they have been looking over the world as the Moon That Never Sets. That is the story.**
> — Librarian Ute

## 11,000 years of peace and the Serdian War

Presumably, the Winglies **surrendered after Melbu Frahma's death**, and as a sign of peace, **gave the Humans three Divine Moon Objects** which were distributed to the **royalties of the three human kingdoms of Serdio, Tiberoa and Mille Seseau**. The Humans accepted the offerings and **believed the objects held some power, although they were unaware of the nature of the power contained in them**.

**11,000 years after the Dragon Campaign**, shortly before the events of Chapter 1: The Serdian War, **Emperor Doel of Sandora began taking orders from a resurged Emperor Diaz**, **re-igniting the Serdian War** against his nephew King Albert of the Kingdom of Basil. These events set the story of The Legend of Dragoon in motion and lead to a **much greater plot directly connected to the events of the Dragon Campaign**.

## The Final Battle

> _Legend_of_Dragoon_-_Cutscene_6_-_Rose's_Memory — An FMV video with memories from the final battle of the Dragon Campaign in Kadessa._

> WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

During the events of Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow, **Rose remembers the events of the final battle of the Dragon Campaign**, when she is washed ashore near **Lidiera together with Dart** after the events surrounding the **Phantom Ship**. Rose and Dart **barely made the jump to the Queen Fury from the sinking Phantom Ship, but Dart slips off and is held by Rose**. Although he tells Rose to let go, **she refuses to let him go, remembering the events during the final battle of the Dragon Campaign, where she failed to hold on to Zieg**.

In the battle :

- **Kanzas self-destructs to destroy a Super Virage**
- **Zieg fights Melbu Frahma**, who wields the **Dragon Buster**, in a one-on-one duel and **slays him by piercing the Wingly with his sword, but is hit by a petrification spell at the same time and plunges into the depths together with his enemy**
- Meanwhile, the **Darkness Vassal Dragon Michael fights and destroys Virages**
- As **Kadessa, the location of the battle, starts to break apart**, **Shirley and Belzac are threatened by a Virage that holds on to the wall**. **Shirley, presumably knocked unconscious before, was saved by Belzac from a collapsing roof he held up, but the Virage pierced him with one of its claws, fatally injuring him**. **Shirley, seeing this, promises Belzac's death will not be in vain and prepares to shoot an arrow into the Virage's eye, which at the same time charges its laser towards the two Dragoons, likely killing them**
- In the end, in the ruins of the place, **Rose finds Zieg, who is petrified by Melbu Frahma's spell and tells her to go and save herself**. **Rose tries to hold on to Zieg, but fails to do so, floating off and away**

## Personalities from the Dragon Campaign

> WARNING: Section contains major spoilers.

The true backgrounds surrounding the personalities of the Dragon Campaign are revealed during the events of Chapter 3: Fate & Soul and Chapter 4: Moon & Fate.

### Humans and Dragoons

#### Zieg Feld, the Dragoon of the Red-Eye Dragon

> _Zieg, the original Dragoon of the Red-Eye Dragon._

During the Dragon Campaign, **Zieg Feld was among the leaders of the Human army**. **Chosen to be the Red-Eye Dragoon**, his life's work was to **liberate Humans from the Winglies**. **He fell in love with Rose, another dragoon**. **Zieg's enemy during the final battle was Melbu Frahma**, the Wingly Dictator.

In the last battle for liberation, Zieg and Melbu Frahma went head to head. **Melbu Frahma attacks him with a slash from the Dragon Buster, a sword created by Winglies to kill Dragons and Dragoons. Zieg dodges the attack and impales Melbu through the heart, who, while dying, casts a petrification spell on Zieg.**

**Over 11,000 years after the Dragon Campaign, the spell eventually wore off and Zieg married Claire Feld.** Zieg and Claire have **a son they name Dart**. During the **Black Monster's attack on Neet, Zieg takes them outside and returns back into the village to help other villagers, but is never seen again by Dart, who is also left behind by his mother who follows her husband back into the village**.

During the later events of the game, **Zieg makes a dramatic return into the history of Endiness**.

#### Rose, the Dragoon of the Darkness Dragon

> _Rose, the original Dragoon of the Darkness Dragon._

During the Dragon Campaign, **Rose was the fiancé of Zieg Feld**. She witnessed the final battles or deaths of all of her compatriots in the war including that of Zieg. **For the next 11,000 years, Rose overcame the process of aging with the help of a choker given to her by her friend Charle Frahma**.

She **joins Dart's party when she notices that he has the Red-Eye Dragoon Spirit**, and has a **test fight with Dart to prove his worthiness as a Dragoon**, and instructs him on the use of his Spirit. They travel on together to attempt to stop the Serdian War and continue their journeys to Tiberoa and Mille Seseau.

#### Belzac, the Dragoon of the Gold Dragon

Belzac was the original Dragoon of the Gold Dragon. He **died protecting Shirley from the attack of a Virage in Kadessa** and **had a particular fondness for her**.

#### Shirley, the Dragoon of the White-Silver Dragon

> _Shirley, the original Dragoon of the White-Silver Dragon._

Shirley was the original White-Silver Dragoon. She was **killed together with Belzac by a Virage**.

**Her spirit resided for millennia at the Shrine of Shirley in Serdio**, where legend tell that the **Dragoni plant grows**. She **tests Dart, Rose and Lavitz on their worthiness to receive the Dragoon Spirit of the White-Silver Dragon in order to save Shana**.

**Shirley meets the group one more time in Vellweb**, explaining that **she fixed the original Dragoons' souls at Vellweb so they would not be led to Death City Mayfil, and could be released to heaven instead**. She **remained on Endiness to ensure their safety until that time**.

#### Kanzas, the Dragoon of the Violet Dragon

Kanzas was the original Violet Dragoon. **It was Shirley who convinced him to join the Dragoons which contrasted his bloodthirsty reputation**. He **self-destructed to destroy the Super Virage in the final battle of the Dragon Campaign**.

#### Syuveil, the Dragoon of the Jade Dragon

Syuveil was the original Jade Dragoon. He was **afraid of what he saw at Mayfil and so his soul stayed on Endiness**. He **does not appear in the FMV on Disc 2**.

#### Damia, the Dragoon of the Blue Sea Dragon

Damia was the original Blue-Sea Dragoon. The **daughter of a human and a mermaid**, she was **only fifteen when the Dragon Campaign began**. Her **mermaid heritage made her an outcast most of her life**. She was **afraid of being left alone again and so her soul stayed on Endiness**. She **does not appear in the FMV on Disc 2**.

#### Emperor Diaz

The **Holy Emperor of Gloriano** and the man who initiated the Humans' victorious campaign over the Winglies. Upon visiting **Fort Magrad in CD 3, Rose remembers his speech from the ancient times**.

Relatively early in the game, it is revealed that **Emperor Diaz enigmatically is either still alive or returned**, **although Rose denies this each time**, and that he is the **one supplying the Dragoon Spirits to Greham, Emperor Doel and Lenus**.

### The Wingly Army

#### Melbu Frahma

> _Melbu Frahma, the ancient dictator of the Winglies._

Melbu Frahma was the **dictator of the great Wingly civilization**. At the last fight of the Dragon Campaign, he is seen making his final stand against the Red-Eye Dragoon Zieg Feld, where he meets his death by being **impaled by Zieg's sword**. He uses the **last of his magic to inflict a curse of petrification upon Zieg**.

#### Faust

**Possibly the most powerful Wingly to have ever lived**, Faust was **Melbu Frahma's second in command** and the **commander of the Tower of Flanvel**. He was known to be **able to make an apparition of himself equally powerful to himself**. **Melbu Frahma, fearing and mistrusting him, therefore created the "Vanishing Stone" as a safeguard against him**.

#### Virage

> Main article: Virage

The Virage are **powerful, alien creatures that fought against the Dragoons and the Dragons**. They were **used by Winglies similar to how humans used Dragons**. Virages resurge in the story of The Legend of Dragoon at several occasions, and show a **mysterious connection to Shana**.
