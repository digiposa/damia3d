# Dragon Campaign

The Dragon Campaign was a **legendary war between humans and Winglies**. After much suffering, the **oppressed rose up to sever the chains of fate that bound them**. The world was forever changed, as **Winglies were unseated from their superiority**.

## History

Some time after the birth of Winglies from the Divine Tree, a **significant portion of them began to treat other species as inferior**. **Many species were subjugated, even to the point of being forced to fight each other to the death in a coliseum within Kadessa**.

Eventually, a **human named Diaz found the courage to stand in defiance to his oppressors**. He began **rallying fellow humans to his cause, gathering in numbers and inciting rebellion**. During a speech at **Fort Magrad**, he **formally declared war on the Winglies**.

At some point, **Vellweb became the capital for the oppressed**. Diaz gathered more and more victims and their training began. This would become the foundation for the **Liberation Army**. However, regular warriors would not be enough.

Eventually, humans discovered a secret about Dragons. Specifically, they learned that **when Dragons die their eyes merge into a singular gem**. **If harvested at the moment of a Dragon's death, the Dragoon Spirit is activated and can be used by sentient wielders who can tame the beast within**. This discovery gave the rebellion the power they needed to rival the magical prowess of Winglies and obtain their freedom. **Thus it became known as the Dragon Campaign**.

Winglies found this to be a serious threat, but had already **harnessed the magical power of the Virage Embryo via the Crystal Sphere**. They also **learned how to spawn Virage at will without birthing the embryo**, using them as an answer to the rebellion's newfound Dragoons and Dragons. Led by Diaz, Dragoons would fight alongside the Liberation Army, **bringing the war to the five floating cities of the Winglies as well as the floating mobile fortress Flanvel**. One by one, each would fall, until the last battle at Kadessa.

There, in the **final battle**, Dragoons would fight **many Virage as well as at least one Super Virage** in their effort to sack the capital. **Kanzas would sacrifice himself to clear the latter**, as the others pressed on. **Rose's vassal Dragon, Michael**, was present to **defeat many Virage in aerial melee combat as well as using a special laser to eliminate several at once**.

Meanwhile, **Zieg would engage the dictator Melbu Frahma directly** — his **Dragoon form against Melbu's Crystal Sphere and Dragon Buster**. **Zieg struck a fatal blow, but in that moment Melbu would petrify Zieg and embed his essence within the Dragoon Spirit in a last-ditch effort to survive**. They both fell, as **Kadessa finally began to destabilize and crumble**. The **Crystal Sphere would also be broken, releasing the soul of the 108th fruit to begin a tragic new cycle in search of Moon Children**.

As the city fell, **Belzac stopped a structure from crushing himself and Shirley**. However, he could not escape as a **Virage had impaled his entire chest**. Thanks to his Dragoon power and sheer will, **Belzac managed to keep the column up as long as he could**. The Virage began charging its **primary laser**, whereby **Shirley knocked a magical arrow aimed in that direction**. **Zieg's petrification overtakes him**, as he utters his final words and tells **Rose to save herself**. The Dragon Campaign would result in a **victory for humans and all oppressed species, but at great cost**.
