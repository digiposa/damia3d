# Dragon Campaign

> **War foundational TLoD** — légendaire conflit ~**11,000 ans pré-game** entre **Humans + species opprimées** vs **Winglies dictateurs**. **Diaz Holy Emperor of Gloriano** rallies rebellion → declare war **Fort Magrad** → **Vellweb capital oppressed** + **Liberation Army training** + **Spear Shooter weapon vs Wingly cities**. **Découverte canon Dragoon Spirit mechanism** : Dragon eyes merge into singular gem on death → harnessable. Conflit conduit aux **5 floating Wingly cities + Flanvel floating fortress** → final battle **Kadessa** (Wingly capital). **Final battle canon** : Kanzas self-sacrifice vs Super Virage, Michael (Rose's vassal) aerial Virage killer, **Zieg vs Melbu Frahma duel** (Dragoon form vs Crystal Sphere + Dragon Buster) → Zieg dodge + impale Melbu through heart → Melbu petrification spell on Zieg → both plunge into depths + Crystal Sphere broken → **Moon Child cycle starts**. **Belzac + Shirley tragic death scene** (Virage impale Belzac protecting Shirley + Shirley arrow vs Virage laser). **5 of 7 Dragoons died** (survivants : Rose immortelle + Zieg petrified). **Post-war canon** : **3 Divine Moon Objects** distributed Serdio/Tiberoa/Mille Seseau royalties + 11k ans peace + petrification wore off → Zieg married Claire Feld → Dart born → Neet Black Monster attack → Zieg+Claire return village, never seen again by Dart.

## History canon

### Origine canon : oppression Wingly

- **Some time after Winglies' birth from Divine Tree** (107th fruit), **significant portion** commencent à traiter autres species comme inférieures
- **Subjugation canon** : species opprimées **forced to fight each other to the death** dans un **coliseum within Kadessa** (lore tragique, motive rebellion)
- Multiple species opprimées : **Humans + Dragons + Mininto + Gigantos + Mermaids** (probable, à confirmer)

### Naissance rebellion (Diaz humain)

- **Diaz = human canon** (originellement) — pas encore "Emperor", pas encore reborn
- **Trouve le courage** to stand in defiance to oppressors
- **Rallies fellow humans** → gathering in numbers + inciting rebellion
- **Speech canon Fort Magrad** : **formally declared war on Winglies**
- 🆕 **Diaz = leader rebellion canon** (vs antagoniste reborn final)

### Vellweb capital + Liberation Army

- **Vellweb devient capital pour les opprimés** canon (cohérent Vellweb = mausolée des 4 anciens Dragoons morts Disc 4)
- **Diaz gathers victims** → **training begins** → **Liberation Army foundation canon**
- Pattern lore : Vellweb = lieu sacré pour la mémoire de la rebellion (post-Dragon Campaign)

### Découverte Dragoon Spirit mécanisme canon

🆕 **Mécanisme canon précisé** (vs wiki Dragons + Dragons fandom) :

- **Humans discover secret about Dragons**
- **Specifically** : **when Dragons die, their eyes merge into a singular gem** ⭐
- 🆕 **Cette merge des eyes (1-7) = explication eye count canon** ! Eye count = puissance du Dragon = puissance du Spirit
- **If harvested at the moment of a Dragon's death** → Dragoon Spirit **activated**
- **Used by sentient wielders** who can "**tame the beast within**" canon language
- → Rebellion gains rivalry power vs Wingly magic = freedom path
- **Thus it became known as the Dragon Campaign** (name origin canon)

### Réponse Wingly canon

- Find threat serious
- **Already harnessed magical power of Virage Embryo via Crystal Sphere** canon (108th fruit soul mécanisme)
- **Learn to spawn Virage at will** without birthing the embryo
- Use Virage as answer to rebellion's Dragoons + Dragons

### Guerre canon — Liberation Army campaigns

- **Led by Diaz**, Dragoons fight alongside Liberation Army
- **5 floating Wingly cities** = cibles war canon (noms ? probable à investiguer)
- **Flanvel = floating mobile fortress** canon (cohérent **Tower of Flanvel Disc 3** = vestige de cette fortress ? Kashua Glacier site)
- **One by one**, each falls

### Résultat

- **Victory for humans + all oppressed species** canon
- **5 of 7 original Dragoons died** canon (fandom précis) :
  - **4 souls à Vellweb** : Belzac (Gold), Damia (Blue-Sea), Syuveil (Jade), Kanzas (Violet) — **fixed by Shirley away from Mayfil**
  - **Shirley dead** (mais Spirit à Shrine of Shirley séparé, Dragoni plant grows)
  - **Notable** : Damia + Syuveil **soul stayed on Endiness** (Damia afraid alone, Syuveil afraid Mayfil) — pas à Mayfil mais Vellweb tower spirits
- **Survivants canon** :
  - **Rose survives immortelle** (Choker Charle Frahma)
  - **Zieg "dead"** (petrified curse, soul embedded Red-Eye DS, Melbu's essence inside) — **petrification wore off 11k ans plus tard** → mariage Claire Feld + fils Dart
- **Diaz "dies"** mais **reborn/returned 11,000 ans plus tard** canon (supplies DS à Greham/Doel/Lenus — révélé Greham Disc 1)
- Winglies unseated → **Human Age begins** canon
- Cycle Moon Child started (compensation tragique)
