Haschel (**ハッシェル, Hassheru, lit. "Haschel"**) is a playable character in The Legend of Dragoon. He is the **Master of the Rouge School of Martial Arts**.

---

## Character Design

Haschel is an **elderly martial artist with long black hair and thin shaped eyes that look closed**. He wears a **thin laced headband**.

His outfit is a **purple sleeveless martial arts garb with black designs and a black spike design at the back and a red belt** which tuck into his garb, **purple martial arts pants and black boots**. He also wears **red wristbands** in both of his wrists.

As a Dragoon, **Haschel resembles Kanzas, the original wielder of the Violet Dragoon spirit**. He wears a **violet Dragoon armor with sky blue accents and a green gem at the center** as the violet outlines reaches through his **white pants and a violet and white knee-high Dragoon armor boots with sky blue gem at the center**. He also wears a **matching violet gauntlets** which are bulkier and wears a **violet dragon headband and fights barehanded**. The **Dragoon wings on his back is a lighter shade of yellow and violet with sky blue outlines**.

---

## Personality

> "He's never serious except when he is fighting." — Dart about Haschel

Outside of the battlefield, Haschel is **upbeat, jovial and has a good sense of humor**. When fighting, however, Haschel is **serious, calm and collected**. Due to his sense of humor and goofiness outside of battle, he **blends in well with Meru**.

In the past, he is shown to be an **extremely strict teacher to his daughter**, who was the designated heir to his school of Martial Arts, to the point of **refusing to be called "father" by her during training, and insisting on being called "master" instead**, and yelling at her in dissatisfaction about her skills and attitude.

## Gameplay

### Abilities and Attributes

Haschel is a **well-rounded fighter with notably good Speed (the third highest in the cast)** and a strong Physical Attack, average Physical Defense and slightly below average magical attributes. His high agility allows him to get more turns than many other characters.

**Second largest number of additions after Dart**. Drawbacks : Thunder Element double-edged sword (no elemental weaknesses + Thunder resistance, but unable to use elemental weaknesses of opponents). His Dragoon form arguably among the less impressive ones, despite high base 200% stat boost on magical attack. **All of his Dragoon spells are single-target damaging spells without any additional effect**.

## Dragoon

### Acquiring the Dragoon Spirit

The **Dragoon Spirit of the Violet Dragon, the fifth spirit obtained**, is acquired **after the battle with Doel**. With the death of Doel, Dart decides to follow Lloyd to Tiberoa. The other dragoons, Rose, Shana, and Albert, agree to go with Dart as it is "fate." Haschel first intends to leave, but a bright light reveals his "fate", as the Dragoon spirit chooses him as its next wielder, and so he heads to Tiberoa with his fellow dragoons.

### Dragoon Transformation

To transform into a Dragoon, Haschel is **enveloped with lightning and thunder while charging himself**. The white lightning forms his entire Dragoon armor and wings and once it is completed, he demonstrates his short martial arts moves while striking his pose.

### Dragoon Magic

## Haschel's connection to Dart

> "Haschel feels a mysterious connection to Dart." — The manual

In summary, the developers put a large number of hints and clues into the game that point to **Haschel's runaway daughter Claire being the same Claire that became Dart's mother**, with even the manual hinting at it. In a twist of fate or irony, this also means **he met his grandson Dart a few years before the game's events take place**, when they traveled together without knowing of their relationship.

Evidence summary :

1. **Claire Bridge Fletz Disc 2** — Haschel's daughter + Dart's mother share name Claire.
2. **Furni Disc 3** — Shana's lullaby = Claire's lullaby dedicated to future child = Dart's mother's lullaby.
3. **Moon That Never Sets Disc 4** — Haschel says Claire "**does not know about Dart yet**" (= has not given birth to Dart yet).
4. **"Serdian" building piano** — girl plays Shana's Theme = Claire's lullaby melody; both Dart + Haschel react.
5. **Final battle** — Haschel : "Dart resembles Haschel as a young man".
6. **Timeline data Official Guidebook** : Dart 23 years old + Claire chased away 25 years before events → Claire met Zieg Feld shortly after leaving Rouge.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ **Dart = Haschel's GRANDSON canon NEW MAJEUR REVELATION**.
