**Lavitz** is the **head of the First Knighthood in the Kingdom of Basil**. Growing up in **Bale**, Lavitz serves as **one of the king's most trusted knights**. He is **loyal to his country and compassionate towards its people, even those from enemy territory**. His **blonde fringe haircut is paired with heavy-set brows that frame his blue-grey eyes**.

## Story

### Chapter 1: Serdian War

After Dart leaves the burning rubble of **Seles**, he crosses a Forest and enters **Hellena Prison** to rescue his childhood friend, **Shana**. As Dart crosses the wooden bridge and enters the **second pillar of Helena Prison**, he comes across a **Hellena Warden harassing two Knights of Basil**. Just as the wardens begin to answer the goading knights with greater violence, a warden from down the hall is thrown dead on the ground. **A knight in green cloth and silver armor enters the scene spinning a lance, prepared to engage the next two wardens**. The knights greet the new comer as **"Sir Lavitz"**. Lavitz and Dart defeat the guards together. As Dart explains that he must first rescue someone, Lavitz agrees to aid in the rescue before they leave despite the fact that **Lavitz is the lone survivor of his platoon and has important information to share with his king**. Dart quickly respects Lavitz for his straightforward trust and they are able to successfully find Shana. When Shana agrees to fight alongside them, Lavitz accepts her strength for surviving Hellena and offering her bow. The protagonists then fight their way back to the front gates of the prison where they confront **head warden Fruegel for the first time**. Dart's party flee before Fruegal can be fully defeated, as reinforcements were incoming. They narrowly escape, **lunging from a nearly closed bridge on horseback** to flee the island. Guards hot on their trail, Lavitz follows Dart and Shana into the twisting and turning **Prairie path**.

#### Abilities

All Dragoons can use either their **D'Attack** or **Magic** during combat. For an in depth explanation of Dragoon Additions including damage formulas, see: **D'Attack**. Similarly, **Dragoon Magic** covers the damage formula for spells.

> Note: The **"STR %" shown when selecting spells in game is not used in the damage calculation**. Instead they could be used to gauge the strength of spells in relation to one another for that specific character, but some of the values contain errors and so generally cannot be considered reliable. The **Multiplier** value for the spells below is the actual variable used in the damage calculation.

| Jade Dragon DS      | Multiplier | Target           | Effect                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | Cost   | Acquisition   |
| ------------------- | ---------- | ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------ | ------------- |
| **Wing Blaster**    | **100**    | **All Enemies**  | Inflicts **Wind-elemental magic damage**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | **20** | **Initial**   |
| **\*Blossom Storm** | -          | **All Allies**   | **Halves \*most incoming damage to each target**. This effect **persists for a target until they take 3 turns** and is **not removed upon death**. Despite the graphical appearance, this **effect still applies to allies whose HP is 0** and will be present if they are resurrected. | **20** | **D'level 2** |
| **Gaspless**        | **300**    | **Single Enemy** | Inflicts **Wind-elemental magic damage**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | **30** | **D'level 3** |
| **Jade Dragon**     | **300**    | **All Enemies**  | Inflicts **Wind-elemental magic damage**.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               | **80** | **D'level 5** |

\*1: Despite the **name of the spell being "Blossom Storm"** in the Status menu, when **unlocking it initially**, and when selecting it with the Magic command, the **voice line clearly says, "Rose Storm."** When **Albert inherits the Dragoon Spirit**, the **name in the Status menu and Magic command switch to "Rose Storm."** As the **graphics for these two spells does differ (Lavitz's version shows pink petals reminiscent of cherry blossoms, while Albert's clearly show red roses)**, it is plausible that the voice line is in error.

\*2: **Blossom Storm halves incoming damage by enabling the Power Up variable multiplier for party members when enemy damage calculation occurs**. This can be observed in game as the **item Power Up and Blossom Storm do not stack**. Additionally, **attacks which do not include the Power Up state in their damage calculations such as any Rare Monster's Rare Attack and Ghost Commander's Haunting Bolt completely ignore Blossom Storm**.
