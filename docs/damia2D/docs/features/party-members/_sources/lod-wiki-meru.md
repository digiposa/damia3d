Meru is a perky dancer that is first introduced in the city of Donau. She is an incredibly optimistic and bright character, often serving as the comic relief. Her frame is petite, with platinum hair and vibrant magenta eyes.

Abilities
All Dragoons can use either their D'Attack or Magic during combat. For an in depth explanation of Dragoon Additions including damage formulas, see: D'Attack. Similarly, Dragoon Magic covers the damage formula for spells.
Note: The "STR %" shown when selecting spells in game is not used in the damage calculation. Instead they could be used to gauge the strength of spells in relation to one another for that specific character, but some of the values contain errors and so generally cannot be considered reliable.
