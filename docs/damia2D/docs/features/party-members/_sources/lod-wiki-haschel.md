## Story

### Chapter 1: Serdian War

Haschel is **defeated by Lloyd in the semi-finals** and advises that **Dart use his upcoming battle with the finalist as "a test for himself,"** implying that Dart will not be able to win either. Haschel is **crowned third strongest in Endiness, under Dart and Lloyd**.

Returning to the general event area, Haschel is introduced to Dart's friends and states that he's been **looking for his daughter since about the same time as Dart's search for the Black Monster**. To return to levity, he attempts to entreat himself to the women of the party and assumes that his requests are being denied due to one of them being Dart's sweetheart. He continues to tease Dart in this way, offering to play minigames with Shana when Dart doesn't immediately state whom he'd go with.

#### Abilities

All Dragoons can use either their D'Attack or Magic during combat. For an in depth explanation of Dragoon Additions including damage formulas, see: D'Attack. Similarly, Dragoon Magic covers the damage formula for spells.

> Note: The "STR %" shown when selecting spells in game is **not used in the damage calculation**. Instead they could be used to gauge the strength of spells in relation to one another for that specific character, but **some of the values contain errors** and so generally cannot be considered reliable. The **Multiplier** value for the spells below is the actual variable used in the damage calculation.
