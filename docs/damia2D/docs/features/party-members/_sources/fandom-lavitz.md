## Character Design

Lavitz has **short blonde hair which is styled in spiky ends on front** and **brown eyes**. His main outfit consists of a **silver armor with gold designs and a Basil emblem on front**, beneath his outfit is a **dark green turtleneck with a matching pants** and a **silver knee-high iron boots with gold designs**. He also wears **silver iron gauntlets with a gold trim** and **wields the spear**.

**As a Dragoon, he resembles Syuveil**, the **original wielder of the Jade Dragoon Spirit**.

He wears a **jade green Dragoon armor in a reddish brown trim** along with a **matching greaves** and a **large reddish brown high-neck collar where the red gem was embedded on his upper chest**. His **pauldrons from his Dragoon armor had three reddish brown designs** and is equipped with a **jade green Dragoon gauntlets in a reddish brown trim**, **black pants** and **jade green Dragoon armor boots with a brown gem at the center**. He also has a **jade green dragon headband on his forehead with brown studs**. His **spear also transforms into a Dragoon Spear in a brown motif**.

## Personality

Lavitz is a **selfless, loyal, kind, and chivalrous man who frequently puts others before himself**. He puts **charity before obedience**, when he offers refugees from the enemy country of Sandora food and lodging, and is somewhat of an **idealist when it comes to uphold the honor of his country and the dreams of his comrades and the people who count on him as a knight**. He also puts **candid aid to a friend above merely appeasing them**, as when he advises Dart in Lohan to take his relationship with Shana more seriously. He also **loves his mother very much**, shown as how excited he is to be home and to introduce her to his new friends, also in going back to gratefully accept her offered present when no one else is present.

## Gameplay

### Abilities and Attributes

Lavitz is a **physical-oriented fighter with a large HP pool as well as strong physical offense and defense**, at the cost of a **below-average speed and poor magical abilities**. Throughout the entirety of **CD 1**, he is a **competent fighter with high survivability and a notoriously fast availability of all his four basic Additions**, with two of them, **Rod Typhoon and Gust of Wind Dance**, being particularly useful, the first one providing a **good balance between damage and SP**, and the latter being the **strongest addition available in the early game**. This excellent availability of additions theoretically allows him to get his **final addition before the second visit to Hellena Prison**, although this requires extensive addition grinding.

Lavitz is not without weaknesses, however. While being the **physically strongest character of the entire cast available on CD 1**, he also has the **slowest speed**, which slightly compromises his ability to rack up damage. His **poor magical attack** makes him a terrible choice for using any **Magical Attack Items**, and his equally **poor magical defense** makes him take heavy damage in case of being hit by a magical attack. However, in contrast to his successor, Albert, this flaw is not as apparent with Lavitz, as only few enemies in the early game rely heavily on magical attacks.

Lavitz' Dragoon form is very useful as well. While gaining a strong **200% base bonus on magical attack**, his first spell, **Wing Breaker**, rarely deals significant damage even when used on an earth element enemy due to his abysmal magical attack stat. However, his second spell, **Rose Storm**, is arguably among the **best Dragoon Magic spells in the game**, cutting any damage for the next three turns of the entire group in half, and being a great asset during boss fights. It is questionable whether he is able to reach **Dragoon Level 3** for the time he is in the party, but **Gaspless** has a somewhat higher potential of dealing high damage and benefitting from his Dragoon bonus for magical attack, particularly when used on an enemy of earth element.

Overall, Lavitz is a competent fighter with a good Dragoon form. During the time he is in the group, his weaknesses in terms of magical defense are not as apparent as with his successor, Albert, who later faces significantly more enemies and bosses who rely on magical attacks.

## Dragoon

### Acquiring the Dragoon Spirit

The **Jade Dragoon Spirit is the third Dragoon Spirit received in the game**. Lavitz gets the spirit **after the battle with Feyrbrand and Greham**. As **Greham lays dying**, he explains that his **admiration for Servi turned into a consuming fear of realizing one's limitations**. The only way to overcome that fear was to overcome Servi **with the help of the Dragoon Spirit which he obtained by defecting to Doel and Sandora**. **Greham tells Lavitz to live strong and vanishes in a brilliant green light**. The same green light shines on the floor and reveals itself to Lavitz to make him **the next Jade Dragoon**.

### Dragoon Transformation

To transform into the Dragoon, **Lavitz spins his spear as he activates the Jade Dragoon Spirit at the center of his chest in flashes of light**. He is **covered in a giant tornado** to unveil both his Dragoon outfit and weapon and makes a pose being **embedded with wind and green leaves**.

### Dragoon Magic

The **Jade Dragoon's spells are mainly for attacking all enemies or assisting all allies**. Lavitz' and Albert's **subpar magical attack** makes their magical offensive prowess somewhat lacking. However, **Rose Storm/Blossom Storm is considered among the best Dragoon Magic spells in the game and a significant benefit for boss fights**.

## Story

> WARNING: Section contains major spoilers.

### Backstory

From his childhood, **Lavitz' dream was to become a knight to protect the country of Serdio ever since he was little**. He grew up in the **Kingdom of Basil as the son of famed knight Servi Slambert**, who was **betrayed by his friend Greham, when Serdio split 20 years before the story begins**. Because of this, **Lavitz continues to hold a grudge against Greham**.

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

#### Hellena Prison

Dart meets Lavitz in Hellena Prison as Lavitz **attempts to escape the prison fighting off the Hellena Wardens**, and trying to **rescue his own surviving knights from the First Knighthood, who are however pushed over a cliff and killed**. He joins Dart in his quest to free Shana and to escape from Hellena.

#### Prairie

When Dart, Lavitz and Shana escape from Hellena, **Lavitz is hit with an arrow from pursuing Hellena Wardens**. Although Dart offers to assist him, he decides to go on his own. Later, he is in a bit of pain and calls the wound "pathetic", and **after Shana treats him with first aid at a nearby shack**, Lavitz explains to Dart the current state of the Serdian War.

> Quote canon : "Currently, our Kingdom of Basil is at war with the **Imperial Sandora**. The war has spread all over Serdio. Because of the Dragon, the legendary monster added weight to Sandora and broke the balance of power. Then **Emperor Doel** started to be more aggressive." — Lavitz

When Dart shares his knowledge about the Dragon, Lavitz realizes the urgency of the situation and suggests to see **King Albert in Bale** as soon as possible.

On the way to the **Limestone Cave**, the group meets a **Sandoran refugee family**. Although the father shows a hostile attitude at first, **Lavitz generously gives some food to the family's child and offers them to come to Bale and live in his house**.

#### Limestone Cave

In the Limestone Cave, Lavitz' friendship to Dart deepens when he **almost falls of a stepping stone down a waterfall, but Dart quickly jumps to Lavitz and rescues him**. Lavitz thanks Dart for saving him for the second time, and promises they will have a long friendship. When the group discovers **Rock Fireflies**, he and Dart have common knowledge about their use.

#### Bale

In Bale, Lavitz receives a **portrait of himself upon visiting an enthusiastic painter**. He reports to **King Albert** and introduces Dart and Shana to his king. At this point, it is also revealed that **Lavitz is Albert's friend since childhood**. As Dart and Shana enjoy the view from the balcony of **Indels Castle**, Lavitz takes part in the **war council**, informed that **Imperial Sandora plans an attack on Hoax**.

After the events at Indels Castle, the group visits **Lavitz' mother**, who is enthusiastic to meet her son and mistakes Shana for Lavitz' bride. Although the misunderstanding is cleared soon, Lavitz' mother begs him to find a spouse soon.

> Quote canon : "Lavitz, please find somebody like her so that I can hold a grandchild in my arms. You are not a child anymore." — Lavitz' mother

**Lavitz' "treasure", a view on Indels Castle**. While Shana and his mother prepare lunch, Lavitz shows Dart his house, telling some anecdotes about his parents and about himself, such as when **he made a cut into his cushion when measuring his own height**. In the library, he guides Dart to the roof to show him **his "treasure", a beautiful view on Indels Castle he often took as a child, dreaming of becoming a knight who defends his country and his people**.

> Quote canon : "[...] The reality was much harder than I imagined back then. I feel all the more strongly how great my father was. To protect this country I would tear the flesh from my body. It's the same passion you have towards Shana." — Lavitz

#### Hoax

In Hoax, Lavitz first meets with **Kaiser**, and vows to keep fighting for his dead company from the First Knighthood.

> Quote canon : "Yeah, it was my mistake. But I won't let the dreams of my company end in death and be in vain. I'll end the war and unify Serdio! I will!" — Lavitz

He stays with Kaiser for a council while Dart and Shana explore the town, and **defends the town against the nightly raid by Imperial Sandora together with Dart**. **Lavitz watches in awe as Dart transforms into the Dragoon of the Red-Eye Dragon for the first time**.

#### Marshlands

In the Marshlands, Lavitz is recognized by the **dying Head of the Tenth Knighthood of Basil in the Seventh Fort**, who warns him of the **Dragon's poison**.

#### Nest of Dragon

In the Nest of Dragon, Lavitz is **shocked to see Greham there**, the **former head of the Second Knighthood of the Kingdom of Basil**, who **turned treacherous many years ago and killed Lavitz' father Servi**. Vowing to revenge his father, Lavitz confronts Greham, who then **transforms into the Jade Dragoon and challenges the group, summoning Feyrbrand to his side**. Eventually, Lavitz and the others defeat the Dragon and Greham. **Greham, in his final moments, praises Lavitz for having the strength of his father, and shows regret about his own betrayal**. After he dies, **the Dragoon Spirit of the Jade Dragoon choses Lavitz as his new master**. Lavitz vows to **use his newfound power for justice**.

> Quote canon : "You have Servi's strength. My efforts were to no avail. I even used betrayal to obtain these powers... Nobody could be stronger than your father. That was the only thing I could never conquer. I had... admiration for him, as a mate... and as a friend. But time goes by and it turned into this feeling... This fear, from realizing the limits of your ability... This fear... that goes nowhere. The only thing you can do is curse yourself for weakness... [...] If only I could obtain a new power, I could be equal to, or surpass him. That was... the Dragoon Spirit I got from His Majesty Doel. Lavitz...live strong. Now...I can go...to be with Servi...." — Greham in his last moments

#### Shrine of Shirley

In the Shrine of Shirley, **Shirley puts Dart and Lavitz through a test to see whether they are worthy to receive the Dragoon Spirit of the White-Silver Dragon**. Lavitz undergoes a test of his will when **Shirley transforms into the shape of Albert and asks him to return**. Lavitz, however, refuses to do so, and emphasizes that **Shana needs him**.

> Quote canon : "Lavitz, well said. That's the renowned Knight of Serdio. Life is precious whether it's one or many. Help out with Shana." — Shirley (in form of Albert) to Lavitz

#### Second Visit to Lohan

After Shana is cured, Dart intends to enter the Hero Competition and tells Lavitz not to go lightly with him, but Lavitz quickly makes clear that **as a knight of Basil, he is not allowed to participate in such a tournament**.

After the tournament, Lavitz tries to get Dart and Shana to spend some time together, and **even pretends to go out with Rose, although Rose quickly slaps him to the ground for dragging her along**. After Dart went out with Shana, Lavitz goes outside with Dart to give him some candid advice to take his relationship to Shana more seriously, and to re-consider his quest for revenge with the Black Monster.

> Quote canon : "Dart. Revenge doesn't solve anything. I learned that from the death of Greham, the guy who betrayed my father, the guy who defected to the Sandora.... The guy I hated. The person you should be looking for is not from your past. Right? You should pay attention to the present. I'll accept a couple of drinks for this advice. When the war ends, let's have a drink in Bale." — Lavitz to Dart

When the group re-unites and sets out to report to King Albert about their successful quest to overcome the Dragon, a **dying soldier of Basil stumbles into the town and tells Lavitz that Bale has fallen to a surprise attack of Sandora and that King Albert has been kidnapped and taken to Hellena Prison**. Lavitz is enraged and immediately wants to head to Hellena to liberate his king, but is held back by Dart. **Haschel rushes in and delivers a blow to Lavitz, knocking him unconscious**.

As Lavitz awakes at the clinic where Shana was, **Haschel scolds him for losing his self-control**. Haschel decides to join the group in their quest, and they set out together to liberate the king.

#### Second Visit to Hellena

As the group approaches Hellena, **Lavitz rushes towards the gate, holds onto the bridge pulled up, jumps over and kills a warden to let the bridge down again**. As the group is **trapped into the lair of Jiango**, Lavitz expresses the urgency to get out as quickly as possible to save King Albert.

When the group encounters and **defeats Fruegel for the second time, Lavitz delivers the final blow**. However, at this moment, **the Man in Hood takes the Moon Gem from Albert**. **Lavitz charges in to help Albert and transforms into a Dragoon, but the Man in Hood takes out an ancient legendary weapon, the Dragon Buster. He impales Lavitz through the Dragoon Armor, fatally wounding him**. Dart and Lavitz share their last moments, **Lavitz entrusts everything to him**. **The Jade Dragoon spirit is passed onto Albert**, who thanks his friend.

When Albert gathers with the rest of the group in Seles, he remembers Lavitz and pays his respects to his knight.

> Quote canon : "I owe you and Lavitz a debt of gratitude. [...] _Grin_ It reminds me of what Lavitz happily told me about you. That he had met 'guys he could finally call friends.' Now, I understand why. Dart, Shana, Haschel, and Rose, Lavitz was my most loyal and strongest knight, and at the same time, he was my precious friend. Yes, he was a great 'guy'. I would like to show you my gratitude. Thank you." — Albert

Albert then decides to go to confront Emperor Doel in the Black Castle and take up the weapons on his own, claiming that **Lavitz taught him how to fight**.

> Quote canon : "I will show them the full force of my lance, which I learned how to use from Lavitz!" — Albert

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

Lavitz is mentioned several times during Chapter 2, most notably by Albert. When King Zior asks him why he is on this journey, Albert replies that **wanting to settle Lavitz's score is one of the reasons**.

> Quote canon : "It might be self-gratification. However, I would like to settle my friends' score with my own hands. We are pursuing a man who manipulated the war with Serdio from behind the scenes. My friend was killed by that man. At the same time, the Moon Gem, which belonged to Serdio, was taken away. We have different reasons, however we are all linked to that man, and here we are." — Albert

### Chapter 4: Moon and Fate

#### Mayfil

In **Mayfil**, Dart and his group make a fateful encounter with their old comrade Lavitz, **whose Spirit dwelled in the city of the dead**. Dart and Albert are overjoyed at seeing their old friend again, who tells the group more about the nature of the souls that stay at Mayfil.

> Quote canon : "Souls that are attracted here remain with a variety of thoughts. That's why they are attracted by the gate of hell. Negative thoughts, attachments are the favorites of the devils from hell." — Lavitz

**Lavitz, however, gets possessed by a demonic device attached his back, loses his mind and runs away**. Upon chasing after him, he attacks the group and involves them into a battle. By **talking to Lavitz, Dart makes him stop attacking and turn his back towards the group, giving them the opportunity to attack and destroy the device on his back**. Afterwards, the **true villain behind the manipulation of the souls shows himself, a demon named Zackwell**. Dart and his companions fight and overcome Zackwell. However, even after the fight, **the demonic device re-appears of Lavitz' back, making him go berserk again and attack Dart, knocking his sword from his hands**. Just as he is about to finish off Dart, **Dart yells his name, making Lavitz return to his senses**. **Lavitz uses his spear, impaling himself through the chest in order to destroy the device on his back for good**.

As Lavitz is finally freed from the demonic influence, he apologizes to Dart and Albert for causing trouble and **uses his remaining power to lead their way to the Signet Sphere**, saying a final goodbye to his friends.

#### Epilogue

In the **epilogue FMV of the game**, there is a scene in Bale where **Lavitz' mother opens the doors to her home**. **Dart enters Lavitz' room upstairs, where his portrait and helmet are displayed**. As Dart leaves, **two cups with a drink and ice cubes are seen, with one empty**. As the scene zooms in, **the ice cube of the second glass is also seen moving for a moment, referencing the promise that Dart and Lavitz would be having a cold drink in Bale together once the Serdian War is over**.

In **Mayfil on Disc 4**, Lavitz is **not fought as a normal boss, rather an event boss much like Shirley**. Due to a **creature called Zackwell attached to his back**, he **blindly and violently attacks the party and cannot be damaged**. **In fact, he cannot be defeated, nor should the party attack him as this leads to a string of counterattacks**; for some this may be an enjoyable aspect of the battle to see Lavitz perform his additions, as **when attacked he will either use Rod Typhoon or Blossom Storm**.

The only way to end this battle successfully is to **wait for the option to "Talk to him"** (**if knocked out, Dart will even raise from dead to speak, and then fall down again**). This causes Lavitz to **overcome his rage and turn around for a few rounds**, thus **exposing the demonic device attached to his back and allowing to attack and destroy it**. After destroying it, the **true boss enemy of Mayfil, Zackwell will show himself**. After winning the battle, there is a **50% chance of the powerful Halberd weapon to drop**.

## Trivia

- According to the **Official Guidebook**, Lavitz is **34 years old and 170cm (5'7") tall**.
- The data above makes him **8 years older than Albert**.
- Lavitz's **idle poise** (only seen when Shana and he are running with Dart through the Prairie or when Dart, Shana and him are jumping over the rocks in the Limestone Cave) has him **putting his arms on his hips and standing still and straight**.
- When **Lavitz is in critical health** he **kneels down and holds his spear up to his hip**.
- When **dispirited**, Lavitz **leans back and slouches to the right slightly and he lets his spear hang freely**.
- When **defending**, Lavitz **holds his spear out horizontally in front of him**.
- Contrary to popular belief that **Albert & Lavitz's final addition "Flower Storm" is erroneously named**, this is not true; the **name is meant to reflect the favorite flower of both men**. When Lavitz uses Flower Storm successfully he says **"Rose Storm,"** but Albert says **"Blossom Storm."** This is **not present in the Japanese version**, both **Lavitz and Albert call it the same: "Cherry Blossom Blizzard"** (**桜の吹雪, sakura fubuki**).
- In their **Dragoon form, they explicitly use the favorite bloom of one over the other in their magic**, demonstrating the **closeness and the strength of their friendship**. This implies that the **defensive property of the magic "Blossom/Rose Storm" stems from their strong friendship and desire to protect people**. This is **present in the Japanese version as well**.
- In the **plaza of Bale of 20 years ago, inside the Moon That Never Sets**, there's an NPC that **resembles a young Lavitz**. When talked to, he says he **wants to grow up to become a respected knight like his father and protect Serdio**.
- Even when Lavitz is not in the group, **he gets a mention in every single chapter of the game**.
