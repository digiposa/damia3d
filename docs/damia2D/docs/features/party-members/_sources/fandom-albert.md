**Albert (アルバート, Arubāto)** is a playable character in The Legend of Dragoon. He is the **King of Basil** and the rightful heir to the throne of Serdio. He later travels with the group and lives as a Dragoon for a while, instead of immediately returning to the throne after the Serdian War ends.

## Character Design

Albert has **long golden brown hair** pulled back into a ponytail. Blue eyes. He dons :

- Dark green cape, silver armor with gold trimmings
- Pale green long sleeve tunic, hanging loose with gold trim + gold cross straps front
- Silver belt with gold round designs
- Dark brown gloves + boots, light brown pants

**NPC outfit (king Bale)** : elaborate royal green cape with red straps, royal green tunic, white pants, black boots, black gloves, royal staff.

**Hellena captured** : shirtless, light brown pants, dark brown boots, white belt.

**Dragoon form** : similar to Lavitz but **more slender / less bulky**.

- Jade green Dragoon armor with **orange gem center**
- Matching long greaves + gauntlets, black pants
- Jade green knee-high Dragoon armor boots with orange round design center
- Jade green dragon headband
- Spear becomes Dragoon Spear (brown motif)
- Wings : pale yellow + jade green with orange outlines

## Personality

**Generous, compassionate, level-headed king**. Modest and humble, lacking arrogance/entitlement despite royalty. Deeply loved by his people and knights. Wishes to be treated as a simple friend by the group (like Lavitz).

**Poetic + well-educated**. Smart, often references historical facts, loves knowledge and reading. Knowledgable about Dragoons, Winglies, Virages, statistics. **Displays "nerdy" stereotype** :

- First visit Fletz : uses statistical data + anatomical analysis to dismiss Haschel/Dart blood relationship
- Mille Seseau National Library : overflows with enthusiasm

Often the **calm and rational mind** in the group.

## Gameplay

### Abilities and Attributes

Albert retains Lavitz's attributes :

- **High HP**
- **Strong physical offensive and defensive** abilities
- **Subpar speed** (40, second lowest in cast)
- **Second worst Magical Attack and Magical Defense** in the game

**Role** : hard-hitting fighter with good survivability, **vulnerable to magic** (slightly worse than Kongol even though MDF marginally better). Some boss fights are hard due to magic weakness. Compensates via highest damage outputs with **Gust of Wind Dance** + Final Addition **Blossom Storm**.

**Strength clé** : **availability extrêmement précoce** de toutes ses basic Additions. Rod Typhoon et Gust of Wind Dance particulièrement utiles (Rod Typhoon = balance damage/SP, Gust of Wind Dance = parmi les plus hauts damage outputs non-final). Albert peut théoriquement obtenir son Final Addition dès CD 1 (avec grinding).

**Dragoon form** : useful. Attack spells limited by low MAT despite 200% base boost. **Rose Storm** est cité comme **l'un des meilleurs Dragoon Magic spells du jeu** (boss fights).

### Dragoon

At the end of **CD 1**, Albert receives the **Jade Dragoon Spirit** from Lavitz (Lavitz mortally wounded by Lloyd, Spirit chooses Albert as next master).

#### Dragoon Transformation

Albert spins his spear, puts both hands in place, activates the Jade Dragoon Spirit at center, covered in flash of light. Dragoon form appears in tornado storm with green leaves, quickly makes a pose.

## Story

### Before the events of the game

20 years before the game, Albert's father **King Carlo** was murdered by his brother **Doel**, leading to division of Serdio. Albert was **6 years old** when crowned. Doel refused, defected, founded **Imperial Sandora** in the South. Civil war since. The northern part (Kingdom of Basil) under Albert's rule.

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

#### Prairie

Lavitz explains the Serdian War situation. Dart mentions Dragon attack near Seles → Lavitz realizes urgency, decides to report to Albert.

#### Bale

Lavitz reports to Albert (throne room). Albert deep relief. Minister Noish teases Albert (couldn't focus on day-to-day business). Albert thanks Dart, offers help.

> "I thank you for saving Lavitz. I appreciate it from the bottom of my heart. In gratitude for this, tell me if you need anything." — Albert

Dart asks Shana to stay in castle. Albert refuses : no safe location in Serdio anymore due to war. Comments that **11,000 years ago** the Dragon Campaign ended, around the same time the Black Monster started appearing in history (per Noish).

#### Lohan

After defeating the Dragon, group spends free time in Lohan. Wounded knight arrives : **Bale has fallen** to surprise attack of Imperial Sandora, **Albert taken to Hellena Prison**. Lavitz tries to rush away, stopped by Dart and Haschel. Group sets out to Hellena together.

#### Hellena Prison

Fruegel taunts Albert. Despite Doel's order to keep him alive, Fruegel intends to kill the king. Also claims citizens of Bale will be killed. Albert offered himself as hostage.

> "The head of the knights and the red one!! [...] Both the stars and you will be gone when the sun rises!!" — Fruegel to Albert

As sun rises, Fruegel raises club. Dart + companions arrive. Defeat Fruegel. **Hooded Man** uses opportunity to extract **Moon Gem** from Albert's body. Lavitz turns Dragoon, rushes Hooded Man, who draws **Dragon Buster** and **fatally injures Lavitz**. Hooded Man = **Lloyd**, who flees.

**Lavitz dies in Dart's hands**. His **Dragoon Spirit chooses Albert** as next master.

#### Seles

Group returns Seles. Shelter near center of village. Albert expresses gratitude.

> "Lavitz was my most loyal and strongest knight, and at the same time, he was my precious friend." — Albert

Albert reveals story of **Moon Gem** = national treasure of Serdio, sealed inside body of royal heir. Lloyd worked as double agent for Sandora, stole it for Doel to become legitimate king. Group decides to head to Doel in Black Castle in Kazas.

> "I will show them the full force of my lance, which I learned how to use from Lavitz!" — Albert

#### Kazas

Group meets **Popo**, young representative of New Serdio Party. Albert reveals identity.

> "It is amazing. I heard about the party, but I didn't think the representative would be this young. We've never met before, but you might know my face. I am Albert, the King of Basil." — Albert to Popo

#### Black Castle

Group meets Magic Researchers. Albert particularly interested in research (Purple Flame). Throne room confrontation with **Emperor Doel**. Dart + Albert demand answers : why kill Carlo 20 years ago, why kidnap Shana. Doel claims he saved Serdio from "incompetent leader Carlo". Albert replies Carlo was loved by his people.

Battle. Victory. Dying Doel commends Albert, tells him to **pursue Lloyd in Tiberoa**, find his answers.

> "You have become... strong. What you should know is not the past, but the future. Pursue... Lloyd. He is in the Western country of... Tiberoa. He knows everything." — Doel to Albert

Albert decides to **postpone royal duties** and live as Dragoon for a while, in Lavitz's name.

### Chapter 2: The Platinum Shadow

#### First Visit to Fletz

Capital of Tiberoa. **Claire Bridge** at entrance — name = Haschel's daughter + Dart's mother. Albert displays "nerdy" behavior with statistics/anatomy. Witnesses Princess Emille mistreat pregnant woman → Albert shocked ("if this is a dream, please wake me up!"). Meet **Nello** (gardener restoring Barrens). Albert engages on greenery topics. Albert affirms trust in Doel's final words, wants to be treated as equal/friend.

#### Second Visit to Fletz

Group visits Twin Castle. **Maid Libria** addresses Albert as "Your Majesty". Meet **Princess Lisa** who uses astrology to identify Albert. Story of Princess Emille's behavior change.

#### Valley of Corrupted Gravity

Fight Virage. Rest. Albert contemplates Shana's powers + Dragoons/Dragons/Virages since Dragon Campaign.

#### Third Visit to Fletz

Truth about false Princess Emille (impostor). Real Princess Emille sealed in parallel dimension. **Albert wakes her up**. After exposing **Lenus** as imposter and defeating her, Albert reveals identity to **King Zior**.

> "I would like to settle my friends' score with my own hands." — Albert

#### Queen Fury

On board, **Kongol seeks out Albert**. Discussion on friendship.

> "Maybe 'friends' are the people who walk on the same road. [...] Although we have different objectives, we are walking side by side. Kongol. You are already walking the same road as us." — Albert to Kongol

Albert socializes with Meru (cushion prank), questions her seriousness about the journey.

#### Phantom Ship

Queen Fury collides Phantom Ship. Albert identifies it as **royal ship from Mille Seseau** (heraldry). Realizes souls were killed by **Black Monster** — group witnesses tragedy from past.

#### Undersea Cavern

Group corners Lloyd and Lenus on Prison Island.

> "You killed Lavitz, let us settle this score now!!" — Albert

#### Fourth Visit to Fletz

Real Princess Emille visits people. Albert awestruck at her beauty/grace. **Romance develops** between them. Banquet : Albert reveals he'll go to Mille Seseau with Dart. **Emille promises to wait forever**.

### Chapter 3: Fate & Soul

#### Deningrad

Albert "nerdy" mode in church (Bishop Dille, Librarian Ute). Overflows with enthusiasm at prospect of **Mille Seseau National Library**.

> "The treasure of knowledge is shining and calling me! Let's go!" — Albert

In Library : reads books on politics, Dragons. Joint conclusion with Dart that the book errs (Dragons not extinct, ruled by Dragoons).

#### Forbidden Land

Ancient royal capital of Winglies. Albert encourages Meru about Wingly past sins.

> "The sins of the past are there just to tell us not to repeat them anymore." — Albert to Meru

Group finds Dragon Block Staff. Albert contemplates Lloyd targeting **Divine Moon Object** of Mille Seseau.

#### Mountain of Mortal Dragon

Albert expresses belief Lloyd targeting Divine Moon Object → Miranda tells story of **Moon Mirror** (national treasure of Mille Seseau) and realizes **Queen Theresa is in danger**.

#### Tower of Flanvel

Group fights and defeats **Lloyd**.

> "I trust Dart with everything. He will bring us the right answer." — Albert

### Chapter 4: Moon & Fate

#### Death Frontier

Miranda slaps Rose in argument. Albert intervenes.

> "Stop it! Rose, nobody can understand the weight of fate you have carried. But we can at least lighten the burden for you." — Albert

#### Ulara

Splits from Charle Frahma. Albert + Kongol in weapon shop.

#### Aglis

Albert undergoes **test of courage** by Savan. Minister Noish appears, tells of "eastern tribe" emergency. Albert puts world first.

> "I am a Dragoon, before I am a King. Minister Noish, please attend to the matter of the invasion by the eastern tribe. Do everything possible to avoid a fight." — Albert

**Lulu** reveals eastern tribe doesn't exist. Savan + Lulu praise Albert.

> "Albert had to weigh the world's crisis against his people's. But making cruel choices is the responsibility of a king. The real leader has to be able to weigh the effects of a death. That is the courage of a king." — Savan

#### Zenebatos

Ancient Law City of Winglies. Albert : doesn't think he can "learn anything from such a mess".

#### Mayfil

Ancient Death City. **Fateful encounter with spirit of Lavitz**. Lavitz possessed by demonic device. Group fights demon **Zackwell**. Lavitz impales himself to destroy device, final goodbye.

#### Moon That Never Sets

Group finds inhabited Serdian building. Albert recognizes Bale from 20 years ago (drunk man = **Dran**).

Group splits (attacked by Michael). Albert leads second group. Witnesses scene : young Doel saving young Kongol from bandits, sharing vision of equality between species.

> "The truth about [his] Uncle Doel." — Albert reflection

Rematch with Doel in alternate Bale throne room. Albert defeats Doel one-on-one.

> "I'm different from you and I am not alone. My friends have always been at my side. [...] Without Doel, [my father] couldn't do anything. He also said that it is you who was most likely to take the throne. My father trusted in you. If you had gotten together and governed the country, this tragedy would never have happened." — Albert to Doel

Final battle.

#### Epilogue

Albert **back in Bale**, **gets married to Princess Emille** under blessings of Noish + Libria. Wears NPC outfit (residing king Bale). Waves to people. Fireworks all over city.
