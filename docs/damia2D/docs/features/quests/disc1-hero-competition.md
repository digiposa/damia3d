### Final Round — Lloyd (None element, HP 6,000/8,500 JP) ⭐ UNWINNABLE

- **Platinum-haired swordsman** + black clothing swirly white designs
- **Invincible** canon : easily evades Dart attacks + magic items no effect

- ⚠️ **Final unwinnable canon** : Lloyd counters Dart with **3 quick slashes to chest** → Dart unable to keep fighting
