## Story

### Chapter 3: Fate & Soul

After it is revealed in the Tower of Flanvel that **Emperor Diaz abducted Shana in Deningrad**, Dart and his group decide to meet Diaz personally. They traverse the Snowfield **together with Lloyd** and as a Blizzard comes up, the group rests inside a cave, where **Lloyd reveals the backgrounds of his plot**.

> _Lloyd meets Emperor Diaz in the Forbidden Land._

He tells the story of him meeting with **Emperor Diaz in the Forbidden Land three years ago**, where Diaz told him about **the decay of the world and the loss of magical power and his plans to create an utopia by giving birth to the 108th species**.

> "The world now is rotten, like a fallen fruit. It has the same smell as the world ruled by **Melbu Frahma, the Wingly dictator**. The world... where inertia is considered to be peace, and the easy path leads to devolution. The overture to the destruction has already begun. [...] It is the danger all species are facing. [...] **You Winglies are merely the 107th species. The great will, the creator Soa prepared the last...the 108th species. The last species is the last god, which will regenerate the world and lead us to utopia!**" — Emperor Diaz to Lloyd

**Rose objected** to the story that the Moon Child would bring peace to the world, but would not elaborate further. As Dart presses Lloyd on why he abducted Shana, **he insists that the truth will be revealed in Vellweb**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

The Snowfield is a **short pass-through location**, however in the second screen, it is possible to **slide down a slope near the wooden sign**. Depending on when the player presses X, Dart changes the course of sliding and can acquire different items (see the subsection "Slide Mini-Game").

At the bottom of the slide, it is possible to continue to **Fort Magrad, although this is optional**. To progress to Vellweb, the player can simply make their way back up and exit the Snowfield **towards the west**.

### Healing Spot

The cave in which Dart and his group rested can be used as a **Healing Spot**.

### Slide Mini-Game

To acquire the Dancer's Ring and Magic Shield one must slide down the cliff and **hit the appropriate action icons to stop themselves in front of the chests**.

- To get to the chest with the **Dancer's Ring**, you should slide down to the **right side of the warning sign** and press the X-button at the **second and fourth Action Icons**.
- For the **Magic Shield**, you slide down the **left side of the warning sign** and press the X-button at the **first, third and fourth Action Icons**.
