**Kazas (帝都カザス, Teito Kazasu, "Imperial City Cazas")** is the **capital city of Imperial Sandora**. It hosts the **Black Castle**, where Emperor Doel rules from his throne.

## Appearance

The city of Kazas is in **strong contrast to Bale** (capital of Kingdom of Basil). The overall atmosphere is :

- **Darker, plain, industrial**
- Buildings along the main road are **relatively uniform** in appearance and size
- **Narrow alleys** between buildings
- **Advanced technology** : possibly serving as power grids, **lines resembling electrical grids connect buildings over the roofs**
- **Life quality notably worse** than Basil — perceived tyranny of Doel and Sandoran military
- **Slum** : notable portion lives in poverty, makeshift homes built on top of each other from **scrap wood**

At the end of the main road, the **Black Castle**, a **dark but elaborate palace with Gothic influences**, raises itself high above the city.

## Story

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

After the events of Hellena, **in Seles**, Dart, Albert and their companions decide to **confront Emperor Doel and end the war for good**.

They travel to Kazas :

- Streets guarded by **Sandora troops**, although the Sandorans **do not recognize them as their enemies**
- Group wanders to look for clues on how to approach Doel
- Citizens experiencing **hardship under emperor's reign**
- Mercenaries being hired
- Find out about **civil resistance movement** : **New Serdio Party**

Dart's group decides **against entering the Black Castle directly** (force = trouble). Exploring further :

- Find a **slum** with makeshift housing area (was supposed to be a fort in the past)
- In the **fort house**, meet **Popo** : **a kid who is the leader for the New Serdio Party**
- New Serdio Party = civil movement aiming to **end the Serdian War + support the people of Sandora**

Popo believes Dart's group could be **potential candidates** to join the New Serdio Party, asks them **three questions**.

Upon passing the questions :

- Albert reveals his identity + plans to end war personally
- **Overjoyed Popo** leads group towards **underground tunnel in the fort** leading to Black Castle
- **Popo speaks of his hopes to finally search for his parents when the war ends** (lore : war orphan)
- Dart's group climbs down into the underground caveway from Popo's house → find way up to Black Castle

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

- In the building **left and right** : several **optional cutscenes** can be seen, several pieces of Stardust collected
- To progress : head towards the Black Castle then to the **slums**
- Further Stardust in the slums building
- Near the bottom : meet **Popo** who asks **three questions** to test attitude

#### Popo's 3 questions — canon right answers

1. **"I cannot say which"**
2. **"Take care at home"**
3. **"To protect something"**

Popo will then guide the group to an underground tunnel that leads to the **Black Castle, the final dungeon of Disc 1**.
