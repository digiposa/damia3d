The **Limestone Cave** (**鍾乳洞**, **Shōnyūdō**) is a **location visited during Disc 1 of The Legend of Dragoon**. It **connects the area of Bale with the eastern areas of the Kingdom of Basil, where Seles lies**.

## Story

After leaving the **Prairie**, **Dart, Lavitz and Shana pass through the Limestone Cave in order to reach Bale**. While making their way through the cave, the **party comes across some stepping stones**. As **Dart and Shana make it safely past the stones, Lavitz suddenly slips and is dangling over the edge of the waterfall**. **Dart rushes over to help and successfully pulls Lavitz back up to safely**. The **two men thank and encourage each other, agreeing that they will have a long lasting friendship**, while **Shana feels left out**.

> _A large area near the entrance of the limestone cave with water slides._

Continuing through the cave, **Dart and Lavitz teach Shana about Rock Fireflies and their healing powers**. Towards the **end of the cave, they witness a creature is waiting for them, possibly the rumored "guardian" of the cave**. The **creature, the insectoid Urobolus, attacks the group**, but **with their combined strength the group overcomes the monster**. Once the **battle is over, the party attempts to leave the cave**, but suddenly, **Urobolus springs back up once again and targets Shana**. **Dart rushes to her defend her but is halted by a strange bright light that starts to emit from Shana**. The **light vaporizes Urobolus, leaving Shana unharmed**, while **Dart and Lavitz are left baffled about what just happened**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

- After **entering and going deeper into the cave**, there is a **large area with a waterfall and "water slides" on the right side**. The player can use the **direction inputs to get towards the chests here**. **When they are missed, the player can always re-climb the area to slide again**.
- Upon **entering the area with the stepping stones, Lavitz is saved from falling by Dart in a cutscene**. When continuing, the **group will find a swarm of Rock Fireflies, which offer the company a chance to restore their HP to full**.
- For treasure, a **lake-like area to the north with a frog-like creature jumping around can be investigated**. In the **next area, by going north, the player can return to the lake-like area where a creature resembling a frog jumps over the stones, but on the lower floor**. Here, the **creature can be caught if chased quickly enough and going in a circle**. It will **drop a chest with a Poison Guard, a useful item for the boss battle**. To proceed, the **player has to go over a natural "bridge" out of stone to the east**.
- In the **next area, when going deeper into the cave, the hissing sounds of Urobolus can be heard**. Upon approaching, the **boss fight takes place**.
- After **beating Urobolus, the player can simply follow the way to exit the area**.
