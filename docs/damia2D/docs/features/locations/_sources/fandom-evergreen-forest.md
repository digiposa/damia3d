The Evergreen Forest is a forest in central Mille Seseau and traversed by Dart and his companions **several times during Chapter 3** of The Legend of Dragoon.

## Geography

The Evergreen Forest is located in central Mille Seseau and connects to several locations such as **Furni, Deningrad, Forest of Winglies, Mountain of Mortal Dragon and the Kashua Glacier**. It is filled with a plethora of evergreen trees, and there is **snow patches** located on the rocks and the floor. It also has mountainous areas with many quick turns and zigzags for pathways. A **small lake** can be found within this forest near a rotten tree stump.

## Story — Chapter 3: Fate & Soul

### First Visit (Furni to Deningrad)

In Furni, Dart and his group received a request from the mayor search for the boy named **Teo** and to rescue him, and to possibly find and kill a wild wolf creature named **Kamuy** that was once Teo's pet. The heroes travel through the Evergreen Forest coming from Furni in order to reach the capital city of Deningrad to search for Lloyd and uncovering his plot.

Proceeding through the forest, Dart and his group find Teo safe and unscathed, but at this moment, other warriors show up and scatter around him stating that they will return with him and to come with them, however, **Kamuy, a large and wild beast, jumps towards the warriors and bashes them back, then escapes further into the woods with Teo mounting his back**. Teo desperately asks for Kamuy to be left alone, explaining that **Fa was attacked by another creature**.

Dart and his group find Kamuy and Teo in a different part of the Forest. Upon approaching, the mercenary **Bulgus** appears and tries to attack Kamuy but is quickly thrown aside. A battle begins with Kamuy fatally wounded, much to Teo's sadness and distress. **Shana, seeing this, uses the healing powers of the Dragoon Spirit of the White-Silver Dragon and saves Kamuy, with the creature turning into a small, docile and harmless wolf cub again**. Resident Knight is relieved at Teo being safe and brings Teo, together with the now harmless Kamuy, back to Furni.

The group continues to Deningrad, where suddenly **Rose swiftly jumps across the cliff to the other side and tells the group that she needs to go and run some errands and that she would rejoin them in a little bit**. At the same time, **Meru attempts to back away slowly** when Shana notices her separating herself from the group. **Meru then explains that she too has an errand to run, and only explains she needs to see her family again**. Without Meru and Rose, Dart and his remaining companions make their way to Deningrad.

### Second Visit (Forest of Winglies)

> WARNING: Spoilers follow from here.

After the events in Deningrad, Dart and his group search for the legendary **Dragon Block Staff** in hope to be able to fight the Divine Dragon. Rose, having returned to the group, assumes that the equally returned Meru might know something about its location. In the **northern part of the Evergreen Forest, which originally was a dead end, Meru reveals to the others that she too is a Wingly, and demonstrates proof by opening the portal into the Forest of Winglies, granting permanent passage from there on out**.

### Third Visit (Deningrad to Mountain of Mortal Dragon)

Despite their success in finding the Dragon Block Staff in the **Forbidden Land**, Dart and his group are too late to prevent the **destruction of Deningrad by the Divine Dragon**. After leaving the now ruined city, the Dragoons head to the Mountain of Mortal Dragon by passing through the Evergreen Forest yet again and taking a **new route which is now open to travel, whereas in the past it was guarded by a knight of Mille Seseau**.

### Fourth Visit (Mountain of Mortal Dragon to Deningrad)

After the defeat of the Divine Dragon, Dart and his group keep chasing Lloyd. It turns out **Lloyd came by in the Evergreen Forest, coincidentally at the time when the Younger Bardel Brother attacked Sacred Sister Wink and some of the soldiers accompanying her**. Lloyd saves Wink again, fending off all the attacking spells by his fellow Wingly, and **striking him with the Dragon Buster**. The Younger Bardel self-destructs in a final attempt to take Lloyd with him, but Lloyd is only lightly injured.

Wink thanks Lloyd, who uses the opportunity and the trust he gained from Wink to **go to Deningrad and abduct Queen Theresa in hopes of getting the Moon Mirror, the Divine Moon Object of Mille Seseau**, in his hands.

Dart and his group find one of the soldiers in the Forest, who tells Miranda the news that **Queen Theresa has been abducted**. Miranda is furious at this, and the group rushes back to Deningrad.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

Throughout the events of Chapter 3, the Evergreen Forest must be traversed **four times**:

1. From Furni to Deningrad,
2. From Deningrad to the Forest of Winglies,
3. From Deningrad to the Mountain of Mortal Dragon,
4. From the Mountain of Mortal Dragon back to Deningrad.

A **fifth passing** is needed to obtain the **Stardust in Neet**.

### Layout of the Forest

The layout of the Evergreen Forest is much less intuitive than that of the Barrens, that were a similar "pass-through" area during Chapter 2. The forest leads to several other locations and has the following main areas:

- **Southern Area**: The player enters here from Furni; it has the Saving Spot. To the east and west, there are two treasure chests with the Destone Amulet and Body Purifier. North of it is the Central Area.
- **Central Area**: Teo and Kamuy are first found here, but escape. This area leads to the other areas where the Forest can be exited to other location.
- **Eastern Area**: A Destone Amulet is found in the lower part and the path on the upper part leads to **Neet**.
- **Western Area**: It leads to **Deningrad** towards the south (via another area with a cliff, where the Body Purifier is found on the other side), to the **Mountain of Mortal Dragon** towards the west and to **Kamuy's hiding place in the north**.
- **Northern Area**: originally was a dead end; its function as the gate to the Forest of Winglies is revealed later in the game.

### First Visit (Furni to Deningrad)

In the first visit to the forest, coming from Furni, the player should head north towards the central area and approach Teo on the high spot. **Resident Knight Harris** and other mercenaries gather, but Kamuy and Teo will escape. As a remark, the path to the east (leading to Neet) is blocked by **one of the mercenaries using the model of Serfius**.

To solve the sidequest with Kamuy and Teo, the player should head to the western area and then go north, where Kamuy will be waiting. Upon approaching, **Bulgus** will appear but quickly be taken care of by Kamuy. After the following boss fight, Shana uses her Dragoon Spirit to save Kamuy and make him a small, docile wolf cub again. When now visiting Furni again, **Resident Knight Harris will give the player a reward of 500G**, and **Fa will have regained her ability to speak**.

To proceed, the player must then go south from the western area, where both Rose and Meru will temporarily leave the group. The player should continue south to Deningrad.

### Second Visit (Forest of Winglies)

After the group is reunited in Deningrad, they should head to the northern part of the Forest, which originally was a dead end and is accessed by going north from the central area where Kamuy and Teo were seen the first time and the mercenaries gathered. A cutscene will play where Meru will open the portal leading to the Forest of Winglies.

### Third and Fourth Visit

For all other visits to the Evergreen Forest, it is simply a pass-through area with no specific gameplay or storyline taking place at the location, similar to the Barrens in Chapter 2. If the player wishes to pick up the Stardust in Neet, the Forest must be traversed a fifth time.
