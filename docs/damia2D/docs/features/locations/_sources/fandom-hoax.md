**Hoax** (**ホークス, Hōkusu, "Hocus"**) is a village on the border of the Kingdom of Basil near Imperial Sandora and as such has been transformed by the Serdian War, **serving as a fort and outpost**.

---

## Appearance

Hoax appears to what looks like a **colonial village, fortified by massive walls and watchtowers** to serve as the **western frontline outpost of the Kingdom of Basil**. With the wall around it, it gives the vibe of a castle or base. There is a **villager who tells Dart the wall was originally intended to protect the town against wild animals**.

---

## Story

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

Dart, Shana, and Lavitz decide to go to Hoax after the **war council of King Albert suggests that it might soon be attacked by Sandora's Dragon, Feyrbrand**. When they arrive, Shana is surprised to see that the town is so fortified that it looks more like a fortress. In the headquarters, Lavitz introduces Shana and Dart to **Kaiser, the Head of the Eighth Knighthood of Basil**.

The group is soon given tasks to aid in the defense of the fortress-town. **Shana is asked to cook for the soldiers**, while Dart and Lavitz take up the watch with the other soldiers. While Lavitz discusses military matters with Kaiser, Dart and Shana go look around in the town. They meet **Midwife Gilda** in one of the house and learn more about what happened between **King Carlo and Emperor Doel** in the past, and that the **envy of Doel for the then 6-year old Albert who was crowned the next king eventually lead to the seperation between Sandora and Basil**.

After the end of meeting with Kaiser, Dart and Lavitz take over the watch in the town.

For a while, the **hooting of owls gives Dart and Lavitz the assurance that no enemy soldiers have infiltrated the woods around Hoax**. When Dart leaves his position and go down to get something to eat, the **hoots stop and the entire town is under a sudden attack by Sandoran troops**. Dart and Lavitz fend off the first enemies, including the **Sandora Elite**. However, things take a turn when a **Giganto warrior named Kongol appears**. Dart and Lavitz fight hard against the Giganto, but are unable to overcome him. **Kongol knocks Dart down and raises his axe to finish him off**.

Suddenly, there is a glimpse light appears in the sky, as **Rose, a winged Dragoon warrior comes in and interrupts the fight**. She **calls upon the Dragoon Spirit of Dart, and Dart begins to glow**. As Dart transforms into a Dragoon, a **burst of energy flings Kongol away**. Lavitz stands by and is amazed to see Dart transformed.

> "**The knight of the Dragon, the Dragoon was born.**" — Rose

With Kongol knocked out, a Sandoran Soldier calls for retreat, with **Kongol vowing to return**. After seeing Shana, Dart **transforms back and faints**.

As Dart rests in the inn, Shana blames Rose for causing trouble. When Dart wakes up, he explains to Shana that **Rose is the one who saved his life from the Dragon on his way to Seles**, and thanks her. Rose told them that while **saving Dart was a test to see whether he really was a Dragoon**, she also **saw her old friends who struggled and still remained strong in the past in Dart and Lavitz**.

As they go outside, they see Hoax at dawn, having taken a lot of damage during the raid. At this point, the group hears the news that the **Seventh Fort in Marshlands is under heavy attack by Sandoran troops and the Dragon**. Rose states than **any human effort against the Dragon would be futile, however Dragoon, the rulers of Dragon, might be able to overcome the Dragon**. As Dart vows to change the future of the country using the power of the Dragoon, the group decides to head to the Marshland under the blessings of Kaiser.

### After the Serdian War

After the end of the Serdian War, the **survivors and remaining soldiers of the village are in high spirits again, having overcome the losses they suffered during the battle of Hoax**, although the village is still **heavily damaged and visually unchanged**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

- Player visits HQ first — central building (wooden door ground floor between two stone stairs)
- After Kaiser, explore optional — Midwife Gilda cutscene via attic upper window (house southeast, integrated wall)
- Return to Kaiser → directed to walls watchtower (west)
- Night : Dart goes for food → ambush starts
- Defeat 2 Sandora Soldiers → Sandora Elite → Kongol fight
- Post-Kongol : Rose offers to train Dart in Dragoon form
