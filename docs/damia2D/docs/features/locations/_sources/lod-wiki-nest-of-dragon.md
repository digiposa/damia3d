The **Dragon's Nest** is the heart of a **dense, gloomy forest in the mountains of south Serdio**. Unlike the Forest that the party encounters early on, the dragon's forest has been **warped and poison by the dragon's presence**. Beneath the ground lies a **warren of twisted organic growth with Feyrbrand and Greham at the center**.

## Story

After emerging from the crises at **Volcano Villude**, **Dart, Shana, Rose, and Lavitz** cross the mountain range and enter the forest surrounding the dragon's nest. Something stirs in the air as they enter, and **Rose remarks on it first** :

> **Guys, don't you feel something?**
> — Rose

When they turn to look around at the mysterious growth, Rose continues :

> **This forest is contaminated by the dragon's poison.**
> — Rose
