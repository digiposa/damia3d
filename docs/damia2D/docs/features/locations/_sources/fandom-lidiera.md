## Story

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

#### First Visit

After the **Phantom Ship sinks after the haunting encounter**, **Dart and Rose fall together into the darkness of the ocean at night**, with the **crew of the Queen Fury unable to find and retrieve them**.

**Washed ashore near Lidera**, **Rose looks after an unconscious Dart and reminds her lover Zieg before remembering the tragic events in the final battle of the Dragon Campaign**, with **many of her companions dying in the battle**.

> Quote canon Rose : "**Dart and Zieg, they are so much alike. Not only are they Dragoons recognized by the Red-Eyed Dragon, but there is something else, something that attracts me ... After 11,000 years, I now feel the strength and tenderness of Dart ... and even ... his frailty. These tame the insanity of the Dragoon for me. Zieg ... If my hands could grab him, I wouldn't have had this bitterness. I saw Zieg in Dart. But ... Dart is Dart. He is not Zieg. No ... he is not Zieg.**"

> Cutscene 6 - Rose's Memory — Rose remembering the events of the Dragon Campaign.

In **Rose's memories**, **Kanzas self-destructs to destroy a giant creature**, **Zieg fights Melbu Frahma**, **who wields the Dragon Buster, in a one-on-one duel** and **slays him by piercing the Wingly with his sword**, but is **hit by a petrification spell at the same time** and **plunges into the depths together with his enemy**. Meanwhile, a **Dragon fights and destroys Virages**, and as the **location of the battle, a large floating palace in the sky, starts to break apart**, **Shirley and Belzac are threatened by a Virage that holds on to the wall**. **Shirley, presumably knocked unconscious before, was saved by Belzac from a collapsing roof he held up**, but the **Virage pierced him with one of its claws, fatally injuring him**. **Shirley, seeing this, promises Belzac's death will not be in vain and prepares to shoot an arrow into the Virage's eye**, which at the same time **charges its laser towards the two Dragoons, likely killing them**. In the end, **in the ruins of the place, Rose finds Zieg, who is petrified by Melbu Frahma's spell and tells her to go and save herself**. **Rose tries to hold on to Zieg, but fails to do so, floating off and away**.

As **Dart and Rose are both asleep the following morning, a young boy named Pete and his dog Pooch find the two and take them back to his village**. They discover that **Pete's mother is sick and they all need to make their way to the biggest nearby town, Fueno**, however, **Pete and his mother were unable to go as there is a monster in the Undersea Cavern that leads to Fueno**. As Dart and Rose want to leave the village, **Pete and Pooch asks them to accompany him and his mother to Fueno**, with the two Dragoons agreeing and **Rose saying that she can "annihilate the monster for you"**, with **Dart, Pete and Pooch reacting with a blank stare**.

As **Dart, Rose, Pete, Pooch and Pete's mother pass through the undersea cavern**, **Pete shows them a flooded path deeper into the cavern**.

> Quote canon Pete : "**Since the monster appeared, the water has never ebbed. But it's okay cuz Fueno is not this way.**"

#### Second Visit

After Dart and his group are **re-united in Fueno**, they decide to **search for the rumored Sea Dragon**, which they **hear roar upon traversing the Undersea Cavern again**. They **make their way to Lidiera and talk to the Mayor and the other two card players**. The **Mayor is impressed by Dart's determination and seriousness to "see the monster"** and **orders to open the tide gates and the passage through the Undersea Cavern to Prison Island**. He **explains that he used a simple trick to block the access deeper into the Undersea Cavern by waiting for the flood and then by simply closing the tide gate**, in order to **prevent adventurers from putting themselves into danger**.

> Quote canon Mayor : "**You need to 'see' the monster!? ... ... You look serious. The appearance.... You are warriors, aren't you? I got it. I'll tell ya. But it's up to you, whether you can survive there or not! I closed the route to the Prison Island, after the monster problem arose.**"

**Dart and his group enter a cavern with some treasure inside and use a valve to open the gates**, making the **previously blocked path in the Undersea Cavern accessible**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

#### First Visit

- Starting from Pete's house, the player **must use the small boat to go ashore on the eastern side**.
- By **climbing up the ladder on the eastern building and entering it from the roof**, the player can **pick up a Stardust**.
- When **exiting the village, Pete and Pooch will catch up to Dart and Rose and ask them to accompany him and his mother to Fueno**.
- The player can now **make their way to Fueno through the Undersea Cavern**.

#### Second Visit

- **Using the small boat on the shore**, the player should now get to **the small wooden construction in the sea where the mayor and two people are playing cards**.
- When asked for their reasons on why they want to go to Prison Island, **answer with "We need to see the monster"**.
- The player can then **access the cavern through some stepping stones at the rear of Pete's house and has to activate the valve** (**two treasure chests which are inaccessible afterwards are located inside the cavern as well**).
- The player can now **enter the depths of the Undersea Cavern that lead to Prison Island**.

## Trivia

- **Lidiera is the town in the game with the smallest amount of visible houses, at a total of just three.**
- When **answering the mayor's question with the funny answer "Danger is my middle name"**, the mayor will answer as follows:

> Quote canon Mayor : "**What!? I cannot let crazy people like you go there! I didn't close the way because it was fun! Get out of my sight!**"
