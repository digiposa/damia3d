Home of Giganto **sits west of the Valley of Corrupted Gravity** and was **once the land belonging to the Giganto species but now is the makeshift lair of bandits belonging to the Gehrich Gang**. The towering stone structures have already begun to **break down and weather with no one tending them, now appearing as ruins of a civilization instead of a proper town**. **Dart and the party travel here in hopes of re-acquiring Dart's stolen Dragoon Spirit**.

## Services

### Save Points

At the top of the stairs to the left of the entrance to the throne room.

### Rest Areas

| Type               | Price | Effect                                                                     |
| ------------------ | ----- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Rock Fireflies** | Free  | Removes all Status Ailments and **fully restores HP and MP for the party** |

**Rock Fireflies** can be found in the area just before the entrance to Gehrich's hideout, **inside the cavern that the party investigates in anticipation of an ambush**.

### Shops

None.
