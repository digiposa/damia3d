## Appearance and Culture

Fueno's main attractions are the **hot spring baths at the clinic and hotel** and **a harbor big enough for the Queen Fury**. Fishermen and traders regularly sail back and forth **between Fueno and locations in Tiberoa and Mille Seseau**. Fueno seems to have a **relatively warm and mild climate, similar to Furni in the south of Mille Seseau**. In a bit of similarity to **Lohan in Serdio**, it houses **Tiberoan merchants and street vendors**.

## Story

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

#### First Visit

After Rose and Dart went missing in the ocean in the events surrounding the **Phantom Ship**, the rest of the group, **Albert, Haschel, Meru, Kongol and Shana** kept on by traveling to Fueno.

> Dart and Rose arrive in Fueno with **Pete, Pete's mother and Pooch**.

Meanwhile, Dart and Rose were stranded in **Lidiera** and met **Pete**, who asked them to escort him and his **sick mother** from Lidiera through the monster-infested **Undersea Cavern** to the **clinic and hot springs in Fueno**. As they arrive in the town and bid their farewells, **Pete asks Rose and Dart once more whether they are in love, hinting at some intimacy when they were both asleep**, but is quickly interrupted by Rose and leaves to the clinic, but not before giving a playful last remark about it.

> Dart and Rose meet Meru, Albert, Haschel and Kongol at the hotel.

They go to the town's harbor past the merchants' stalls, where the **Queen Fury is docking**, and ask **Kayla** about the location of the other five Dragoons, with Kayla rejoicing on seeing them alive and well. She directs them back to the town, where Dart bumps into Meru who scolds him for being blind, before realizing that it is Dart and Rose. Meru is overjoyed at their return and takes them to the hotel, particularly mentioning the need for Dart to quickly see Shana.

In front of the bar of the hotel, **Meru repeats her prank of covering Dart's eyes**. His eyes uncovered, Dart is surprised to see Albert, Haschel and Kongol again. Dart tells the the group about their journey from Lidiera after falling from the Phantom Ship. Meru speculates that something may have happened between Rose and Dart; **Haschel scolds her for rudeness, and then is unable to contain himself, asking more or less the same question**. Rose reveals that Dart only spoke about Shana, and Dart is asked to go upstairs to Shana, **who didn't speak a word since they were lost**. Finally, Haschel remarks to Albert that **Rose seems to have changed and calmed a bit compared to her usual way**.

> Dart and Shana share and intimate moment of reunion.

Dart and Shana share an intimate moment of reunion, with Shana crying tears of happiness. **Meru, and again a Haschel old enough to know better, are hiding behind the door eavesdropping, with Albert scolding them**. Hidden behind the door they may be, but not at all silent, Dart opens the door suddenly and they all fall in, comically running out again in embarrassment. The group then move on with their quest to **find Lenus and retrieve the Moon Dagger**.

> Drippy telling the story of the Sea Dragon.

While walking around Fueno, the group meets a **drunkard named Drippy** telling his story. He wails about a **Sea Dragon on Prison Island near the Undersea Cavern that destroyed his home**. Although it seems to come from a somewhat unreliable source, it corresponds to the general state of things and rumors in Fueno and Tiberoa and reiterates what **Commodore Puler** said about a Sea Dragon attacking the ships nearby and scaring all the merchants away. The group decides to investigate the matters around the Sea Dragon; Pete guesses that it may live in Undersea Cavern. Finding the passage to Prison Island blocked, but hearing the roars of the Sea Dragon when passing through, the group searches for a way to get to Prison Island.

> "I saw a Dragon on the Prison Island! You don't know that! My ship was smashed by the Sea Dragon rumored to live over the Undersea Cavern! I gotta have a drink or something! But! You cannot go there anymore! There are lots of monsters there! My house was taken by those monsters! I gotta have a drink! **It was a half year ago that the monster showed up**. It's all the monster's fault that I'm like this now. Give my ship and my house back. ZZZZZZ..." — Drippy

#### Second Visit

> ⚠️ WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

After overcoming **Lenus and the Sea Dragon Regole at Prison Island**, Dart and his group return to Fueno one more time, intending to board the Queen Fury to **get back to Fletz and report to King Zior**. When Dart speaks to **Commodore Puler** and tells him about the **failure to retrieve the Moon Dagger**, the captain gives Dart some encouraging words and **invites him to possibly become a man of the sea in the future**. However, even if Dart considers it not a bad idea, **Shana quickly intervenes and tells him that they have to keep pursuing Lloyd**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

#### First Visit

1. When arriving at the town together with Pete, the player should go **east** and head to the harbor where the Queen Fury docks.
2. Upon speaking to **Kayla**, the player should now return to the town and will bump into Meru. Meru will direct Dart and Rose right into the hotel, where some scenes of reunion play.
3. Afterwards, the player can explore the town and pick up some Stardust and use the shops, and when going towards the exit of the town, the **scene with Drippy plays, giving the player a hint on where to find the Sea Dragon**. The player now has to continue to the Undersea Cavern, where a short cutscene plays, and then to Lidiera.

#### Second Visit

In the second visit after the final battle at Prison Island, the player simply has to get back to the harbor to board the Queen Fury by talking to **Commodore Puler and Kayla**. The player can then **control the Queen Fury on the world map** and board it any time, and return to the world map by speaking to Commodore Puler and accepting the offer to take the steering wheel.
