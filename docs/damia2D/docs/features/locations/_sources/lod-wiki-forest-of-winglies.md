Forest of Winglies is found in Mille Seseau, northeast of Evergreen Forest. **Hidden from the outside world with a magical seal**, it is a place where Winglies that survived the Dragon Campaign made a self-sufficient society. **This forest is Meru's hometown.**

## Story

The Forest of Winglies is introduced when Meru separates from the party to visit her childhood friends. Her visit is unwelcome, narrowly dodging a magic attack from her fiance **Guaraha** who mistook her for an intruder. Relieved to see her, his frustration of having never been told where she was going spills out as he questions her. He gives a parting warning as she walks onward, that the others will not be as forgiving that she **committed a taboo**. As Meru steps into town, she is generally met with scorn. **Blano**, ancestor of the community, insists that she must leave at once. Guaraha tries to reassure her, sharing that the timing is especially poor because **the seal placed on the Divine Dragon is fading**. Meru just wants to see how everyone is doing, and feels dismayed by the attitude of the community. She hopes that one day her people will be more open-minded.
