**Deningrad** is the **capital of Mille Seseau**, located on the **northern coast** between two groups of mountains. The region is ruled by **Queen Theresa**.

## Services

### Save Points (état dépendant)

- In the **alley leading to the Crystal Palace**. **Disabled after the Divine Dragon's attack**.
- In the **Hotel**, but **only after the Divine Dragon's attack**.

→ Pattern : save point migration suite à un story event (Divine Dragon attaque la ville).

### Rest Areas

| Type   | Price | Effect                                         |
| ------ | ----- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| Hotel  | 50G   | Fully restores HP and MP for the party         |
| Clinic | 50G   | Fully cures all Status Ailments from the party |

> ⚠️ **Pre/post-Divine Dragon state** : After the attack, the **first Clinic becomes inaccessible** and the **upper floor of the Hotel opens up to reveal a second Clinic**.
