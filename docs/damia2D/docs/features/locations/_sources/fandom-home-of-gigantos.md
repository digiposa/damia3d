The **Home of Gigantos is an area in the west of Tiberoa, just over a range of mountains from the eastern reaches of the Death Frontier**.

**Once the home of the Gigantos, the 97th species from the Divine Tree, the area was destroyed and raided by humans that feared the strong creatures**. During the events of the game, it is the **hideout of the Gehrich Gang that terrorizes the citizen of Tiberoa**.

## Story

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

After Dart and his group pass through the Valley of Corrupted Gravity, they find the ancient home of the Gigantos. They are **targeted by traps shooting arrows at them**, and in front of a **massive gate, end up surrounded by Bandits of the Gehrich Gang**. They open fire, but Dart swiftly evades them and charges towards the gate, which is however closed. As the bandits lose their patience, they call one of their leaders to fight Dart and his group.

> **The gate in the center of the Home of Gigantos, leading to a large hall**.

When the **Gangster prepares for the fight, Haschel recognizes his stance and style as the Rouge School of Martial Arts**. After defeating the Gangster, **Haschel presses him on where he acquired the style and asks for whether he knows a woman named Claire**, but instead he replies that **his boss Gehrich, who was supposedly kicked out of the school about 20 years ago, has taught it to him**.

> "**The Rouge School of Martial Arts is a tradition found only in my village. All the people who use the Rouge style from outside of my village must have a connection there. Do you know a woman fighter called Claire?**" — Haschel to the Gangster

**Haschel then proceeds to give Dart a demonstration of the true power of the Rouge Style of Martial Arts, focusing his entire power into one strike to destroy the gate that was closed before**. In the next area, the **group splits up to search for the Bandits**, but **Haschel claims to have some back pain and to require some rest**. While the rest of the group searches inside a cave and finds some **Rock Fireflies**, Meru is waiting alongside Haschel, but **Haschel knocks her out and runs off into the large hall before him alone**.

Inside the hall, **Mappi and Gehrich are having a conversation**. Haschel confronts Gehrich, before Dart and his company interfere. The **following fight ends with Mappi defeated and Haschel dealing a finishing punch to Gehrich**. As Mappi dies, he says "**I'll crush you!**" as his final words and a **tremor shakes the entire building; a huge statue arm collapses down towards the party**. Just as all hope is lost, **Kongol appears out of nowhere to catch the pillar and save the lives of his former opponents**.

> **Gehrich, the leader of the Gang**.

In Gehrich's final moments, he reveals that the **Princess Emille they met is an imposter and the real one is hidden somewhere in the Twin Castle**. **Kongol reveals he intends to join Dart, as he was able to overcome Emperor Doel**. While **Haschel buries Gehrich**, the rest of the group **find Lynn and Dart's Dragoon Spirit in the basement of the Gigantos Temple**.

### Chapter 4: Moon & Fate

After the events at Ulara, Dart and his group **pass through the Home of Gigantos one more time**. They **unlock a hidden tunnel in front of the large hall**, and make their way to **Fletz to board the Queen Fury one more time**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

- Upon entering the area, **arrows will shoot at the player, but they will not cause any damage when hit, but slow the player down**. In order to deactivate those, the player must enter the "house" on the left side and approach the **"spark" on the floor which is a switch**. When pushing it, the arrows on the outside will be deactivated.
- Moving up the stairs to the north, **Dart will draw out his sword**. When entering the square, Bandits will start shooting arrows. The player should now avoid the arrows and approach the gate in the north, which will be closed by the enemies. When trying to escape back to the south, more enemies will surround the player. After a while, a **Gangster and two Crafty Thiefs must be defeated in a fight**.
- **Haschel will then destroy the closed gate** and the player can proceed to the next area. There, the group will split and **Haschel will knock Meru out in order to run off on his own to the large hall**. The group can now **heal with the Rock Fireflies in the area** and go after Haschel. **Saving is recommended**, as the boss fight follows upon entering the hall. **In the fight, Haschel must be a member of the party**.
- After the boss fight against Gehrich and Mappi, the player should now **descend down the stairs into a storage room, where Lynn will be released**. The group will split up to search for the Dragoon Spirit. The player will find it in the **bottom right corner of the room**.
- After coming back up, **Kongol will join the group** and the player should now return to Fletz via the Valley of Corrupted Gravity and the Barrens. If desired, an **optional scene of Kate and Lynn's wedding can be seen by following Lynn to Donau before going to Fletz**.

## Trivia

- After the events in **Ulara on Disc 4**, it is discovered that there is a **secret entrance to the Home of Gigantos**. The **curious stone face across from the Rock Fireflies conceals the tunnel**. According to the Winglies in Ulara, **one of them was close friends with the Giganto race and constructed the tunnel**.
- In the **final dungeon of the game on Disc 4**, the group finds the **Home of Gigantos, presumably in the state where it was several decades ago**. The large hall looks more or less the same, and upon descending to the basement, the **prison and storage room where Lynn was kept is found in the supposed state of some decades before**.
