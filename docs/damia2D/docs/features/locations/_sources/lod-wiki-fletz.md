## Description canon ⭐⭐⭐ MAJEUR

> "Fletz is the capital city of Tiberoa, located in the southern region. It borders the Aquaria Coral Reef, and includes a harbor for ships. The city's architecture is made up of white and blue marble-like buildings; the blue marble usually starting at the bottom and becoming completely white at further increasing levels of the buildings. The Twin Castle is located further north into the city, which includes two large towers. One tower is called the Tower of Moon, while the other is the Tower of Stars. They both act as quarters for each of the princesses of Tiberoa. **King Zior** is the ruler of the region."
