**Bale (王都ベール, Ōto Bēru, "Imperial City Bale")** is the capital of the Kingdom of Basil, the northern state in the country of Serdio, and the city where King Albert resides in **Indels Castle**.

## Appearance

The city of Bale resembles a city during the **renaissance**. The roofs are topped with what appears to be clay tiles while the base of buildings are often composed of stone. The entire city has a **"wavey" design** too, which is most evident in the metal workings of the city that bend or curve around the river.

Bale's look is in **contrast with the look of Kazas** in the rivaling state of Imperial Sandora, which looks more **industrial**. Like Fletz in Tiberoa, Bale is **surrounded by rivers** and has a **large channel flowing through the center and beneath the city**.

The trees are **golden and auburn as though it were autumn**.

## Story

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

When Dart and his companions arrive at Bale, Lavitz suggests seeing King Albert first to tell him about **Feyrbrand** and Sandora's advance into Basil. They meet the king at Indels Castle, where Dart asks if Shana can stay at the castle out of harm's way, much to Shana's disappointment. However, Albert explains that **it is not possible, as no place in the country is safe anymore because of the Dragon**.

Dart retracts his request and asks Minister Noish about the **Black Monster**. Noish explains that he doesn't know many details, but that **the Black Monster started appearing in history about 11,000 years ago, roughly at the same time the Dragon Campaign** (a great liberation war) took place.

**Folklore Dragon Campaign (Noish, verbatim)** :

> _"As if they were gods, Winglies ruled over all creatures and used them as slaves. The enslaved suffered a terrible domination and injustice. Their anger turned into a flame of fury. Then, a gust of wind blew throughout the lands, spreading the flame to a blazing fire. The wind was **Emperor Diaz**. **Seven incarnations of Dragons** served the emperor. Their bravery inspired people to take up arms. Thus began the Dragon Campaign. It was a harsh war. Both people and Winglies suffered countless injuries and fatalities. After a long period of suffering, it was the humans who acquired the future. The age of humans had begun."_

A soldier walks in and tells Albert that the **war council** is ready to begin. Albert suggests that Dart and Shana enjoy the view from the balcony. Shana hastily excuses herself ; Dart follows.

On the balcony, Dart and Shana wonder how Seles is. Lavitz enters and explains the town is well and they will be able to rebuild once the war is over. He then reveals that **Sandora is readying itself to attack Hoax using the Dragon** and that he plans to head there to maintain the front line.

Dart decides to go with Lavitz. Shana follows. For preparation, Lavitz says that Bale has shops + they can stay at his mother's home.

### At Lavitz's home

Lavitz's mother **confuses Shana for Lavitz's new bride**. Lavitz explains Shana is Dart's sweetheart. Mother (a bit saddened) tells Dart he needs to speak up next time, and tells Lavitz he needs to find a girl like Shana. She asks if he would be staying the day ; if not, at least have lunch. Shana offers help in making lunch.

While women prepare lunch, Lavitz gives Dart a tour. When they reach the **library**, Lavitz shows Dart his **"treasure"** — moves the ladder to the window, invites Dart to the roof. **From the roof : a perfect view of Indels Castle.** Seeing this view every day, Lavitz grew up hoping to be an admired knight like his father. Lavitz compares his desire to protect his country to Dart's desire to protect Shana.

After lunch, they prepare to go to Hoax. As they leave town, they **bump into Martel** who hastily disappears into the city.

### After the Serdian War (return visit, late game)

> WARNING: spoilers.

When the group reaches Tiberoa, they can return to Bale. **Most citizens mention Lavitz.** **Minister Noish acts as regent** in Serdio's day-to-day businesses and manages the peaceful country.

If the player visits **Lavitz's mother**, you find out she is **living with the refugee family** that the group met at the Prairie. Lavitz' mother is in **deep sadness over Lavitz's loss** ; however, the Sandoran family gives her comfort and is in **deep gratitude for Lavitz inviting them to his home**. In later stages of the game, Lavitz' mother is **overjoyed at the child of the family calling her "grandmother"**.

## Gameplay

Bale is the **first town visited by the group to have all four town features** : Item Shop, Weapon Shop, Clinic and Hotel. **Its hotel is also the cheapest, costing only 10G per stay**.

There is an underground jetty under Indels Castle. It can only be accessed via **underground waterway** — Dart has to buy **Good Spirits** (**100G**) from the nearby inn and give it to **Dran**, who is drunk and blocking the way. The waterway is located **under the big fountain in the main street**.

Bale is the **first of the temporary locations for Martel**, who is looking for **Stardust to cure her child**. She can be met in the house next to Lavitz's house.

### Walkthrough

- **Optional side event** : the Goods item **"Lavitz' portrait"** can be obtained by entering the first house on the left after entering the city.
- The player should proceed to **Indels Castle**, right to the west after entering the town, and head to the **throne room** on the top.
- After cutscenes, the player can return to the town. **Hidden area** : passing through the dining room of the castle on the 1st floor towards north and then to the west, they find a **ladder going down**. This leads to a very small room on the ground floor with another ladder leading downwards. Climbing down : below the castle, where they can **operate a rudder to open a gate to a small boat dock**. Necessary for all Stardust.
- After exiting the castle, the player can buy "Good Spirits" from the nearby inn for **100G** and give it to **Dran** (entered the city, following the stairs downwards below the fountain). Dran moves away → player can use the boat at the docks below the castle to go around the city and collect treasure / Stardust.
- To proceed the story, head **east from the entry to the city** → "Slambert Plaza" (large square with spring center). **Lavitz' home is the one to the left of the barn, the large building with the door that looks like the entry to a wild west saloon.**
- After cutscenes, **choose "show me your home"** → Lavitz can say something to each location. To progress, head to the **library**. After another cutscene + lunch, the player can leave or gather remaining Stardust.
- When exiting the city, **bump into Martel**, who is in the **house next to Lavitz' house** at Slambert Plaza.
