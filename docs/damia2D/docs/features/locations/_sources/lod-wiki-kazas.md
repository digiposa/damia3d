Situated on the **southwestern coast of Serdio**, Kazas is the **capital of the imperial nation of Sandora**. The town houses nervous residents and fearful revolutionaries kept in check by patrolling soldiers. Looming above, the **Black Castle** harbors the greatest powers of the Sandoran military. All of this sits squarely in the grasp of **Emperor Doel**.

## Story

Dart and company arrive in Kazas to discover that it is currently in a state of **civil grief**. Along the main thoroughfare :

- Sandoran soldiers are on patrol
- **Opportunistic mercenaries** enlisting for Sandora change their mind when they realize that the status quo in the war is changing
- Civilians complain about their government in **"griping rooms"** (rooms falsely promised to be impervious to eavesdropping)
- A Sandoran soldier takes from the weapon shop a **Mysterious Adventurer** lying about having defeated the dragon **Feyrbrand** and peddling a feather purported to be able to weaken dragons
- Despite being Sandoran, many Kazas residents seem to lament the **capture of Bale** and Basil's apparent imminent defeat

## Trivia

- Although **Popo** considers Basil to be an incorrect answer when he asks Dart about which side should win the war, Popo's remarks about Albert liberating Kazas imply that he prefers Basil to win.
- Many NPCs do not appear to be concerned about expressing to Dart, a stranger, how sympathetic they are to Basil and King Albert and how critical they are of Imperial Sandora and Emperor Doel.
