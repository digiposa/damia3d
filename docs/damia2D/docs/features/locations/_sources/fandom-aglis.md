**Aglis, also known as the Magical City**, was an ancient Wingly city where magical phenomenons were studied. During the events of the **Dragon Campaign**, it sunk into the **Straits of Walter** near Rouge, and is visited by Dart and his group during the events of **Disc 4**.

## Story

### Chapter 4: Moon and Fate

Dart and his allies observe the city first from Rouge. As they think about a way to get there, the **sea suddenly splits**, "inviting" the group to it. Upon entering Aglis, the **sea closes behind Dart and his group, trapping them inside** and forcing them to work their way through the ancient city.

As they proceed through Aglis, they are greeted by peaceful, sentient magical beings overbringing messages by someone named **Savan** to the group. Dart and his companions eventually meet Savan, who is a **Wingly from the ancient days**. **4,000 years after the end of the Dragon Campaign**, he discovered Rose, who took the tragic task of killing the **Moon Child** in order to prevent the destruction of the world. He committed his life in supporting Rose's cause, creating an artificial Signet Sphere, the **Moot**, and developing the **Psychedelic Bomb**.

He leads the group deeper into the city towards his laboratories and undergoes a test with them, as he requires the heroes' **courage** in order to finalize the Psychedelic Bomb. Each character is transformed into a remote dimension, where, by the assistance of Savan's magical beings **Buckle** and **Lulu**, they have to face up their fears, encountering people from their past or having to decide between two harsh decisions.

> _Savan's monologue (verbatim wiki)_ :
>
> _"I survived the Dragon Campaign and continued studying magic to reconstruct a Wingly world. Yes, even making myself **ageless and immortal**. However, the world outside the mirror hasn't required me. The world of Winglies is no longer desired. I, an immortal body with no purpose. **4000 years of solitude** deprived me of everything. Even the significance of life and the meaning of death. But **7000 years ago, I was saved by Rose**. I found the meaning of life in the **Black Monster in the mirror**. Rose, who now is confronting her fate. I restarted my study for Rose. And its crystallization is this Psychedelic Bomb and Moot. **We will stop the murderous deeds of Zieg, who continues the plan of the creator**, by using the attacking spell, the Psychedelic Bomb. And with Moot, **we will completely seal the Moon That Never Sets**. [...] Moot will be completed soon. But, in order to activate the Psychedelic Bomb, **I need the Courage of all of you**."_
>
> — Savan

Depending on whether all questions were answered correctly, the group either receives the **mighty Psychedelic Bomb X** (a **repeat item** usable in each battle), or the **regular Psychedelic Bomb** (single use).

After giving the group the Psychedelic Bomb, Savan guides the group further into the center of Aglis, eventually arriving at the **Signet Sphere**. The Signet Sphere is guarded by the **Last Kraken**, the most powerful magical being created by Savan, and **Moot** is built up in the same room.

However, as Last Kraken appears, it suddenly gets **out of control as it is manipulated by Zieg** and proceeds to destroy the Signet Sphere. Dart and his companions fight the Last Kraken and prevent it from destroying the Signet Sphere, however **the field of power by the Dragoons causes a runaway reaction of Moot**. Savan, noticing the danger, **teleports the group to safety outside of the room and locks the door**. Despite Dart's fierce protesting, **Savan chooses death** and promises that his heart will remain.

> _"Even if my body vanishes, my heart will remain. It will give you wings to fly the sky, to the Moon That Never Sets, where Zieg is heading."_
>
> — Savan

As the group returns through Aglis, they meet **Savan's magical creatures which are dying, as they cannot live without Savan**. However, they finish his work and activate the **teleport connection to the law city Zenebatos**, where the group heads to protect the next Signet Sphere.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

- Despite being a rather large location, Aglis is **relatively linear**, and the player has to use **teleporters** to proceed.
- There are occasionally several teleporters to choose from, of which some lead to treasure chests.
- The screen in which the player meets **Ruff** is the same where the teleporting device for leaving the location is, but it can only be used after clearing the dungeon.
- When progressing through Aglis, the player will meet **Savan's magical creatures**. In areas they work, **there are no random encounters**.
- Eventually the player will meet Savan, who will explain the project of completing Moot and the Psychedelic Bomb and will lead the player to a **courage test**. The player can save the game before each test.
- After the tests of courage, the player must head west to an area which is a bit of a **"teleporter maze"** ; to progress, the player should always take the teleporter **furthest to left** on the "teleporter lanes".
- The player will meet Savan in the next room and receive the Psychedelic Bomb.
- Continuing southwest, the player has to follow Savan. The room with the Saving Spot is the room before the Signet Sphere. When entering the room, a cutscene will play and the player has to fight the **Last Kraken**.
- After the battle, the player has to return all the way back to the start of the dungeon, where they initially met **Ruff**. On the left side is a door that leads to the teleporting device that transports the player to Zenebatos.

### The Psychedelic Bomb

In Aglis, the **only Repeatable Item that is also an Attack Item** can be found : **The Psychedelic Bomb X**. In order to obtain it, all questions of Savan's tests of courage need to be answered correctly.

Réponses canon (ordre du jeu) :

- **Kongol** : "Take Doel's sword."
- **Miranda** : Any answer is okay on the first question ; the second question has to be answered with "I cannot die now."
- **Albert** : "I ... cannot do that."
- **Meru** : "I still cannot die!"
- **Haschel** : "I couldn't stop her."
- **Dart** : "I will save Shana no matter what!"

> _Note : Rose absente de cette liste fandom. Le wiki LoD avait noté que Rose "answer does not change the outcome". Cohérent avec l'absence ici._

If one or more questions are answered wrong, the group will only receive the regular Psychedelic Bomb which can only be used once.
