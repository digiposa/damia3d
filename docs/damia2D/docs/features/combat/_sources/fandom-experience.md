**Experience, also known as EXP in-game, is gained every time the characters defeat a monster, certain unique monsters, or a boss.**

Like in other JRPGs, if one character gain enough experience they will increase their level by one.

⚠️ **Experience levels are the only requirement for characters to receive new Additions, except for their Master Level addition.**

## Mechanics

The experience mechanics follows these rules:

1. The gained experience is divided equally to each battle character(s) in the party.
2. Knocked out battle character(s) will not gain any experience.
3. Each character outside the party will gain half of the experience of what one battle character gains.

⚠️ Note : Fandom **omet la mécanique Petrification = 0 EXP** présente dans wiki tier 2.
