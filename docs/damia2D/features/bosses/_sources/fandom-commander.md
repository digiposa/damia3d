## Description canon

The **Commander (小隊長, Kotaichō)** is the **first minor boss a player faces in The Legend of Dragoon**. He appears in **Seles alongside two Sandoran Soldiers**. Later in the game, **another stronger version appears at the optional Seventh Fort in the Marshlands**.
