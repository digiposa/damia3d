## Story

> WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

#### Third Visit to Fletz

After having witnessed unlikeable and displeasing behavior by **Princess Emille** during their first and second visits to Fletz, contrary to her reputation of being a graceful and fine woman, **Dart and his group follow the traces of the Gehrich Gang** and find out that the **Princess Emille they met is an imposter, who took the place of the real Emille six months before**.

During the group's third visit of Fletz, at the **day of the coming of age ceremony of Princess Emille**, when the **Moon Dagger is supposed to be passed to her**, Dart and his group **rescue the real princess and interrupt the ceremony**. The **false Princess Emille subsequently steals the Moon Dagger from King Zior's hands and reveals her identity to be Lenus**. She is then fought in a battle, and afterwards, **Lenus jumps from the tower and flies off, revealing that she is a Wingly**.

Dart and the group later set off to **retrieve the Moon Dagger by boarding the Queen Fury** in the direction Lenus fled, towards the **Prison Island in Illisa Bay**, a region that recently has been **troubled by a Sea Dragon terrorizing the sea**.

#### Undersea Cavern / Prison Island

Lenus is encountered on the **Prison Island** where she is found with **Lloyd**, **candidly showing her romantic interest in him while handing over the Moon Dagger**. Lenus is shown to be the **cause behind the Sea Dragon**, **turning herself into the Dragoon of the Blue Sea Dragon and fighting the group alongside the Dragon Regole**. After being defeated in battle, **Lenus attempts one final attack by flinging her weapons at Dart, but fails to hit him**. She **collapses, slowly dying while calling out to Lloyd**. Her **Dragoon spirit is then passed on to Meru**.

In her **first encounter**, Lenus proves to be a **challenging enemy**. She **performs a variety of powerful magical attacks**, and has **two physical attacks, with one of them targeting the whole group**. It is recommended to use a **fighting group with members of high Magical Defense such as Shana or Meru** and use **equipment maximizing Magical Defense**, and to bring **plenty of healing items such as Healing Breeze and Angel's Prayers**.

In the **second battle**, Lenus will fight as the **Dragoon of the Blue Sea Dragon alongside Regole**. During the battle, **Lenus will use her Dragoon Magic as well as some physical attacks**. The **Dragoon Magic** she uses are listed below.

- **Bubble Burst**: **Lenus throws one of her razor fans at the party, opening a portal of water that sucks the party down**, where **she attacks the party by hitting them and then slamming them together and hurling them towards the surface**. Before they reach it, **she charges up her magic and sends a beam of water at them, bursting them out of the water and back onto the battlefield**.
- **Pillar Break**: **Lenus descends from one of the jagged stalagmites and hops around the party on one foot summoning pillars of water where each of her footsteps landed**. The **pillars then glow and trap the party in a barrier of water**; **before the party drowns Lenus' heel smashes one of the pillars, breaking it and destroying the dome**.

There is **no generally recommended strategy on whether to target Regole or Lenus first**, but **Regole tends to take higher magical damage, particularly from Dart's Dragoon Magic**, and **might be the first target of choice**.

#### Game-Crashing Bug and Fix

Using the **Dragoon form in the battle against Lenus and Regole is infamous for possibly causing a game crash**. In a discussion, it has been pointed out that **not using Dragoon at all in the battle will fix this bug**; it has been tested successfully that it is possible to **use Dart's and Shana's Dragoons in a party with Meru during the battle, as long as all party members are in normal form at the end**.

## Personality

Lenus is a **brash, arrogant, selfish and entitled woman**, showing displeasing behavior when posing as Princess Emille. However, she is shown to be **extremely loyal to Lloyd and in deep romantic interest towards him**, as she **readily gives her life at the end of the chapter for him**, and is **very candid about her feelings towards him**.

> Quote canon : "**Lloyd, my life...is for you!!**" — Lenus' dying quote
