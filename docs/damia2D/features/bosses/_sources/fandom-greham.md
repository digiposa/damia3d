**Greham** (**グラハム, Gurahamu, lit. "Graham"**) is the **former head of the Second Knighthood of Basil** and was a close friend to **Lavitz' father Servi Slambert** before he switched allegiance to **Imperial Sandora**.

---

## Story

> ⚠️ WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

### Chapter 1: Serdian War

#### Before the events of the game

Many years before the events of the game, Greham was a **Knight of the Kingdom of Basil** and a **trusted friend of Lavitz' father**. However, out of **jealousy and frustration over his own limitations**, he **betrayed his friend Servi, killed him and sided with Imperial Sandora**.

A short time before the events of the game, he presumably received the **Dragoon Spirit of the Jade Dragon by Emperor Doel**, giving him the ability to **rule over Dragons and control the Dragon Feyrbrand**, leading to a **shift of power towards Sandora and a re-ignition of the Serdian War**.

#### Nest of Dragon

> Main article: Nest of Dragon

After searching their way through the **Nest of the Dragon**, Dart and friends come to a webbed chamber and find Greham. **Lavitz, is enraged upon seeing Greham and accuses him of betrayal, killing Servi and betraying the whole of Basil. Lavitz vows to avenge his father**, while **Greham reveals himself as the Jade Dragoon and summons Feyrbrand to his side**.

> Dart and his group meeting Greham.

> "**It shouldn't be a surprise. Only the Dragoons can manipulate Dragons. Behind the Dragon, there should be...**" — Rose to Greham, right before Feyrbrand appears.

Before the fight begins, **Rose asks Greham where he has the Dragoon Spirit from**, to which he reveals that **Emperor Doel received his power and intelligence from Emperor Diaz**. This **leaves Rose in entire disbelief**, as **Diaz was a historical figure that died over 11,000 years ago**. Greham, however, refuses to elaborate, and challenges the group to a fight.

After the battle, **Greham is fatally injured**. As he lies dying, he explains that that he honored Lavitz' father and looked up to him as a stronger warrior, but **he became corrupted by his own grasping for power**, leading him to **betray best friend**. He **shows regret for his deeds**, and **in his death, looks forward to see Servi again**. He **dies from the wounds caused from the battle and vanishes**, as the **Jade Dragoon Spirit passes on to Lavitz**.

> "You have Servi's strength. My efforts were to no avail. I even used betrayal to obtain these powers... Nobody could be stronger than your father. That was the only thing I could never conquer. I had... admiration for him, as a mate... and as a friend. But time goes by and it turned into this feeling... This fear, from realizing the limits of your ability... This fear... that goes nowhere. The only thing you can do is curse yourself for weakness... [...] If only I could obtain a new power, I could be equal to, or surpass him. That was... the Dragoon Spirit I got from His Majesty Doel. **Lavitz...live strong. Now...I can go...to be with Servi....**" — Greham in his last moments.

## Jade Dragoon

> The "Dragon Crucification".

Greham uses the **Jade Dragoon Spirit** to become a **Dragoon** and to **control Feyrbrand, the Green-Tusked Dragon**. He fights the party as a Dragoon with his **vassal Dragon** at his side. The attacks he uses are listed below.

- **Spear Combo** : Greham **flies down and hits one party member four times**, then he **uses his spear to fling them up in the air where he knock them back to the ground**.
- **Wind Magic** : Greham **spins his spear to gather energy**, and **releases it out through his left hand**. This energy turns itself into a **moderately powerful, but random Wind magic**.
- **Dragon Crucifixion** : Greham **summons up a giant spear-shaped pillar that traps one party member**, then **charges up and throws four energy spears at the trapped ally**, then he **charges one more energy spear and throws it at the party member**. It hits the trapped ally and also **shatters the pillar, sending them flying causing moderate damage**.
