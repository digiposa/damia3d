**Fire Bird** (ロックハーケン, _Rokkuhāken_) is a fire creature that lives in **Volcano Villude**.

## Story — Chapter 1: The Serdian War

When Dart and his companions first enter the Volcano Villude, they see Fire Bird in the distance. Merely being in his presence turns the air into a reddish haze and threatens to set everything ablaze. **Rose recognizes the creature, as if she has first hand knowledge of it, or as if it were a legend of the past.** Fire Bird recognizes the group and flies in, but Dart and his group escape through the volcanic haze and the caverns of cooled and flowing lava within the depths of Volcano Villude.

After they overcome the **Wounded Virage** in the Villude and rescue the **travelling merchant Dabas**, upon exiting the volcano, Dart and his companions are ambushed by the Fire Bird, which uses all the power it draws from the fiery volcano to attack them, flying out of the lava to **summon fire spirits**, and spraying them with fire and magma. Dart and his friends withstand the onslaught and defeat Fire Bird.

## Battle

### Strategy

**Magical Attack Items are very effective**, particularly **Spear Frosts** which can be bought at Bale and when used by Rose or Shana. **Dart has an easier time in this fight due to his fire element, as he takes less damage from some attacks.** As it often happens with both bosses and regular enemies, **Fire Bird uses progressively stronger attacks as its health gets lower**. Its attacks deal mostly moderate damage, but **Fire Dive and Volcano Ball Summon can deal serious damage to Lavitz as he has low Magical Defense**.

To some beginners of the game, they will find it surprising to **meet two bosses consecutively in the same location**, which is the **Wounded Virage as the first boss and Fire Bird as the second one**, so it might be **advisable to save the game after the fight with the Virage**.

### Attacks (fandom canon names)

> Volcano Ball stats fandom : **HP = 50**

Fire Bird's attacks are as follows :

- **Volcanic Peck**: Fire Bird flies to a character, wraps him in his tail feathers and begins to peck at them.
- **Presto Fire**: Fire Bird shoots a ray of flames at your team that causes the ground to fly up with immense heat. The Fire Bird uses this attack only when its **health is blue or yellow**.
- **Summon**: Fire Bird dives into the lava pit below and pulls up **4 Volcano Balls** that he can (at any time) use to attack your party. The **Volcano Balls can also be attacked to less or stop this move**. Fire Bird only uses this move for about **1-2 times**, especially when its health turns red.
- **Dive Bomb**: Fire Bird flies straight up and dives on Dart and friends, cracking the ground and dip Dart and friends in magma, **dealing more damage than the Peck and Fire Quake**, then rising up again from the lava pits below. The Fire Bird uses this attack **only when its health turns red**.

⚠️ **Fire Quake mentioned indirectly** ("Peck and Fire Quake" comparative) — non détaillé fandom mais probable ability canon distincte.

- **Fire Bird resembles the legendary bird Phoenix.**
