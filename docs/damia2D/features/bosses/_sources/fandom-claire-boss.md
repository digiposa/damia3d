## Battle

Claire has a special attack, the **"Four-Gods-Destruction"**. Her first set of attacks are a simple set of physical attacks. After about halfway through the battle, she uses her special. This attack will **reduce Haschel only to 1 HP each time** ⭐.

⚠️ **Four-Gods-Destruction targets HASCHEL ONLY canon ⭐ MAJEUR** :

- Wiki said "Single target" générique
- **Fandom précise : Haschel only** — pattern story-canon ability target spécifique
- Cohérent thematic "daughter attacks father" canon

She uses this once or twice then continues with the physical attacks, but from here she does **two or three attacks in a row**, so after the round of special, it is recommended to **heal or guard**.

⚠️ **Combat pattern canon ⭐** :

- Phase 1 : physical attacks (Rouge Art)
- Phase 2 (halfway through) : Four-Gods-Destruction Haschel 1-2× → Haschel reduce to 1 HP
- Phase 3 : 2-3 attacks in a row → heal/guard critical strategy

## Trivia

Although it is not stated directly in the game that **Claire is both Haschel's daughter as well as Dart's mother**, it is shown to be **all but a certainty** when Haschel discovers in **Furni on Disc 3** that **Dart knew the same lullaby as his daughter**, who had said she would **dedicate it to her child one day**.

⚠️ **Identity reveal canon Furni Disc 3 ⭐ MAJEUR** :

- **Lullaby = Shana's Theme** (Furni mayor's child Fa lullaby)
- Dart knew same tune (childhood mother lullaby)
- Claire intended lullaby for "her child one day" canon
- → **Strong evidence Claire = Dart's mother (Claire Feld)** canon

Haschel hints at being aware of his connection to Dart when he says to Dart before the fight that **"she does not know [him] yet"**, as the Claire on the Moon is a **younger one, who has not given birth to Dart yet**.

⚠️ **Time displaced Claire canon ⭐** :

- Moon Claire = younger version (pre-Dart birth)
- Pattern Moon That Never Sets = manifest younger Claire from past
- Haschel aware of Dart connection pre-fight canon
