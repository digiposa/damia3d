The **Hero Competition** is an event hosted by empresario **Ginger** in the arena of the **Commercial City of Lohan in Serdio** to determine the strongest warrior of Endiness.

According to **Lavitz, soldiers such as himself are ineligible to join**. As a participant, Dart faces five powerful warriors. His friend **Haschel also competes, but is defeated by Lloyd before he has the chance to face Dart**.

For entering each fight, Dart has to talk to the **Attendant** in the waiting room, who gives him some insight on the opponent he is about to face, motivates him and **eventually becomes a fan of him**.

**Gorgaga** (ゴルガガ, _Gorugaga_, "Golgaga") is a large brutish man that has large metal shoulder pauldrons and a fox-skin cap, he has no element. He seems to be devoid of honor as his **first move in the tournament is poisoning Dart using a Poison Needle**. When Dart yells out foul play Gorgaga merely states that **"Winning is winning"**.

Gorgaga's signature weapon is a **large axe**, which he wields with both of his claw-like hands. Besides his poison, he seems pretty uncreative in his attacks as he usually just slashes once with his axe. Occasionally he will use magic attacks.

**Dart progresses in the competition even if he loses the round, due to Gorgaga's use of poison being against the rules, and for attempting to kill Dart in cold blood, which is prohibited.**

**Serfius** (セルフィス, _Serufisu_, "Serfis") is a **small but powerful knight** that wields a **longsword** and dons a full suit of **plate mail**. He has a **winged helmet with a large red plume** that drapes down the back. According to the attendant of the tournament, **Serfius seems to be quite famous**. He is of the fire element. He starts out the battle with a single sword attack.

After he is cut down to **half of his health**, Serfius proclaims that Dart is just as powerful as he expected and that he will show him his **"true power"**. He **powers himself up and his defense and offense double**. After this, he attacks Dart with a **jumping two-hit combo** that deals much more damage. Upon his defeat, Serfius claims that Dart is "too powerful" and gives up.

If Dart loses to Serfius, **Serfius's chronic illness flares up, requiring him to withdraw from the tournament**; he tells Dart they will fight again some day.

**Danton** (ダンドン, _Dandon_, "Dadon") is a **very large and bulky warrior** who wears intricate red armor over his entire body. He also has a metallic **brown cape made out of chain mail** and in battle he wields an equally proportionate **black mallet**. Danton is of the earth element. His red helmet has a **large protruding white spike** on the forehead area. Although very powerful, Danton's armor drastically reduces his **speed and mobility**. He makes up for this with a very powerful mallet smack which deals a lot of damage.

One of his tricks is to throw down his mallet and yell **"Make my day kiddo, bring it!"** as he crosses his arms. Upon attacking him in this state, Danton will exclaim that Dart left himself open and will **grab him by the neck and punch him several times**. When his health is low, he will use an **all-out attack which is a two-strike combo with his mallet that will reduce Dart's HP to 1**.

In the case that Dart's HP drop to zero, **Danton will stumble and fall over while trying to deal the final blow. Due to his heavy armor he is unable to get back on his feet and Dart is declared the winner of this battle.**

**Atlow** (アトロウ, _Atorou_) is a **skilled archer that uses a longbow** as a his weapon. He **came in as the second place winner in the previous tournament**. He has **reddish-brown hair and is bare-chested**. Atlow wears **metal plate legs and has large spikes on his knees**, he also has a **brown tattered cape and matching gloves**. He is quite confident in his arching abilities and has a unique technique. Atlow is of the darkness element.

When he attacks he targets certain points of his opponent, **outlined in light blue hexagons**. After using this skill **four times in a row**, Atlow uses his finishing attack, the **"5-sense-blocker"**. Atlow throws **three apples into the air, spins around, and shoots an arrow that pierces all three and strikes Dart in the heart**. Dart hovers in the air and **five hexagons appear on his head, arms, and knees and explode in blue light**. This throws Dart back causing high damage and the **fear status**. Atlow will also use **healing items on himself when he is low in health**. When he is defeated, he states that Dart is a **monster** and that no one could have survived his attacks.

If Dart loses the fight it will be discovered that **Atlow's arrows were tipped with illegal poison**. He is disqualified and Dart moves on to the final round.

**Lloyd** is a **platinum-haired swordsman** who **defeated the master of the Rouge Art, Haschel, in the semi-finals**. Lloyd has **black clothing with swirly white designs**. In battle Lloyd is **invincible, as he easily evades all of Dart's attacks and magical attack items have no effect**.

Lloyd attacks with a **single sword strike**, but he can also use an **intricate six-strike combo** that Dart states is **inhumanly fast**. When using this attack or evading Dart's attack, Lloyd leaves behind **several after-images**. Lloyd seems to outclass Dart in all areas and soon becomes bored. Dart tries one more time to attack the mysterious swordsman and **Lloyd counters this with three quick slashes to the chest which makes Dart unable to keep fighting**. Dart congratulates him and Lloyd says a few cryptic lines that had to do with their **fate** before leaving.

> "You haven't reached your limits. You will be stronger. You will have to be. You too. You'll become stronger."

## Results

After the fight between Lloyd and Dart, **Ginger announces the results of the tournament to the crowd**.

- **1st Place** : "Lloyd, the platinum-haired swordsman"
- **2nd Place** : "Dart, the 'young flame'"
- **3rd Place** : "Haschel, master of the Rouge Art"

Despite Lloyd being named the **"toughest and strongest man in all of Endiness"**, it is not shown that any prize on top this is given to him. For the second and third place, evidently no prize is handed out, as the player does not receive any reward after the event.

## Trivia

- **The character models of most of the warriors from the Hero Competition appear again on Disc 3 in Furni to battle and kill the monster Kamuy but they are not the same warriors.**
- **Assuming a perfect tournament system, in a five-round-tournament thirty-two warriors participate in the Hero Competition.**
- **Since Haschel finished 3rd, it can be assumed that he would have faced off against Atlow in a 3rd place consolation match if Dart beats Atlow, or would have taken 3rd automatically if Atlow got disqualified.**
- **Even if Dart loses every round, through some technicality he will still advance to the next round.**
- **If the save-over glitch is used to transform into Dart's Dragoon form in the final, the game will soft-lock when Lloyd performs his counterattack.**
