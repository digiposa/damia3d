## Definition canon Boss (fandom version)

> This is a list of all the enemies that **must be fought to proceed through the game**. When fighting them, **Escape cannot be used**. **Bosses do not respawn**. So, for example, the Hoax version of the Sandora Elite is a boss, but the Black Castle version, although it is impossible or nearly impossible to avoid, **respawns** so it is not a boss.

⚠️ **Critères canon Boss fandom complémentaires wiki** :

- **Must be fought to proceed game** (story progression OR optional designed)
- **Escape cannot be used** ⭐ NEW canon mechanic — Escape command disabled fights boss
- **Bosses do not respawn** ⭐ NEW canon — pas de re-spawn possible
- **Same creature cannot be fought a second time in same circumstances** (same location)
- **Encounter usually unavoidable**
- **Finishing battle required to advance story** (exception : Optional Kamuy / Faust)

### Boss minions canon ⭐ MAJEUR (fandom interpretation)

> **boss minions, even though they are substantially weaker than their boss, are counted as bosses and not as regular monsters**

⚠️ **Divergence wiki tier 2 vs fandom** :

- **Wiki tier 2** : Boss Extras = "neither Minor Enemy nor Bosses" (3ème catégorie distincte)
- **Fandom** : boss minions = "counted as bosses" (subsumés Bosses)
- **Damia adopt** : likely fandom interpretation pour simplification — boss minions = Bosses category. "Boss Extras" wiki = peut-être autre concept (spawn adds, NPCs interactifs) à investiguer.
