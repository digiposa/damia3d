## Battle

These opponents are **not too difficult** all in all, however, their damage can **add up especially if you say fight against three of them simultaneously**. They are **very annoying creatures** as they can **steal 50 gold from you**, to make matters worse, they enjoy **escaping shortly after and successfully stealing your money**.

If you can **kill them immediately after they steal**, you get a **Bastard Sword instead of your gold**. They are **fairly quick and can counter quite often**. They should be able to go down in a few hits.

⚠️ **Strategy canon ⭐** :

- **Annoying creatures** = thematic descriptor canon
