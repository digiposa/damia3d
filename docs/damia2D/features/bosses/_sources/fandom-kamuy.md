## Story

**Kamuy was Teo's normal and lovable household pet** until the **recent change of behavior of the animals in the vicinity**. Now **mutated to a large beast and savage**, Kamuy is made the **target of a mercenary group under Resident Knight Harris on the order of the mayor of the town of Furni**, whose daughter, **Fa**, was said to have been attacked by the beast.

> **WARNING: Spoilers follow from here.**

**Dart and his group find Teo and Kamuy in the Evergreen Forest**. **Mercenary warriors enlisted to kill Kamuy and Resident Knight Harris surround him**, but they **ignore Teo's pleas for Kamuy's life**, as Teo insists that **Kamuy tried to protect Fa when she was attacked by a different monster**. Kamuy **leaps towards the warriors and knocks them aside**, hops upon the plateau Teo is on and **grabs him, then escapes into the woods**.

When finally cornered, Teo desperately asks for Kamuy to be left alone, again explaining that **Fa was attacked by another creature**. The mercenary **Bulgus arrives and wants to attack, but Kamuy jumps on him and launches him into the lake**. Dart and his group then **draw their weapons and fight against Kamuy**. When Kamuy is been defeated, Teo runs towards the giant wolf lying dying and is in **great sorrow and distress**. **Shana, in this moment, uses her Dragoon Spirit and its glow not only revives it but restores its original form of Kamuy into a harmless, docile wolf cub**.

> "**The White Silver Dragon healed the savage soul that changed Kamuy.**"
>
> — **Albert**

> _Teo playing with the healed Kamuy, with Fa watching._

With Kamuy back to his old self, **Teo happily returns to Furni together with Kamuy and Resident Knight Harris**. There, **Dart and his group can get a reward by Resident Knight Harris for their deeds**, and **Fa, regaining her ability to speak, confirms Teo's story that she was indeed attacked by a different monster and protected by Kamuy**.

## Battle

**Kamuy does not have very many attacks, but they are rather strong, although the fight is not considered very challenging overall**. **Kamuy himself is No Element**, but defeating him yields the **Darkness Stone, which halves Darkness damage**.

- **Attack**: Kamuy **pounces on one party member and bites them**.
- **Howl**: Kamuy **howls causing the Stun status**. **This causes no damage**.
- **Spirit Wolf**: Kamuy **runs to the top of his tree and howls causing energy to be released from the tree**, this **energy flies and hits the party**. It's **dealing a Wind-Based magic damage**.

After the battle, you can return to Furni and go to the area where the first meeting was about Kamuy and enter the building. Upstairs is the Knight in charge of the operation and you can collect the **"5 times larger reward" which is 500g**.

## Trivia

- This creature is **based on the spiritual entity from Ainu mythology with the same name**.
- The **battle with Kamuy can be done later in the game, even after Miranda joins the group**. When Kamuy is dying, **Miranda saves Kamuy with the reason that it is "what Shana would do"**. It is **confirmed that you can wait until after the Forest of Winglies, and even after returning from the Forbidden Land and the second attack of the Divine Dragon on Deningrad**.
- After defeating Kamuy and solving the quest, the **mercenary Bulgus can also be met at the house of Resident Knight Harris, showing that he survived being tossed away by Kamuy**.
