**Dart Feld** (ダート・フェルド, Dāto Ferudo) is the **protagonist** of The Legend of Dragoon. He is the **son of Zieg Feld and Claire Feld**, and was **one of the few survivors of the tragedy that befell his hometown of Neet**, in which the mysterious **Black Monster** attacked and **destroyed the town 18 years before the events of the game**. Years later, Dart would set out to seek out revenge on the creature who destroyed his hometown.

## Character Design

Dart has **short spiked blonde hair with fringes** in each sides, and **blue eyes**. He usually wears a **red headband**.

His trademark is a **vibrant red armor with a single gauntlet on the left** and a **black high-neck undershirt**, **blue-gray pants with a brown belt** along with a matching side belt on his waist and **red knee-pads** on his knees and **dark brown leather boots with beige folded sleeves**. He also wears **dark brown gloves** and wields a sword. In his CGI art, his pants appears to be **black instead of blue gray**.

As a **child during the tragic event in his hometown in Neet**, he wore a **brown winter jacket with a white high-neck undershirt, dark green pants and black shoes**.

> _Dragoondart — Dart in his Red-Eye Dragoon form._

In his **Red-Eye Dragoon form**, Dart's appearance is **identical to his father Zieg** in his Dragoon form who wears the **red Dragoon armor with a green gem in the center of the chest**, matching greaves, **blue-green pants** and **light red knee-high Dragoon armor boots with a green gem in the center**. He also wears matching gauntlets and his sword transforms into a **Dragoon Sword**. His red headband is brighter and with a **dragon motif**, while his Dragoon wings are **pale green and light red with blue-green outlines**.

> WARNING: The following part contains spoilers.

In his **Divine Dragoon form**, his Dragoon armor envelops his **entire body**, resembling the **Divine Dragon's appearance**. The armor is **white with red and orange accents** with an **olive green gem with red line in the center of his armor**, resembling the **Divine Dragon's eye**. His **left arm becomes like a "cannon"**, similar to the **Divine Dragon Cannon**, with dark red outlines. He further wears a **reddish-orange headband with seven green gems** on its forehead and a **green optic eyepiece on his left eye**. His Dragoon armor boots with **round, reddish-orange designs resembles a dragon's feet** and his **six Dragoon wings** are of **glowing orange and white with gray outlines**. In his right hand, he wields a mighty Sword that resembles a **sharp dragon fang-like blade with light red outlines**.

## Personality

Dart is a young man whose life turned tragic when an attack on his village wiped out everything he holds dear. Although his heart is heavy, Dart has a **positive and idealistic outlook on life** and will always help a friend in need. His **loyalty and unwavering bravery** stand out throughout his adventure. While otherwise **straightforward and practical**, during his travels as a Dragoon he **accepts and expresses his feelings for Shana**, treats each of his companions with respect and camaraderie, and eventually comes to a **closure about his past**.

## Gameplay

### Abilities and Attributes

Dart is the **all-rounder** in the party. Literally being average in all of his stats with the exception for his **above average HP**, Dart has a **high survivability**, making him a solid and consistent contributor throughout the entire game, enhanced by the fact that he is a **permanent and non-replacable** part of the player's battle group. He also is the **male character with the strongest Magical Attack and Magical Defense**.

Among the cast, Dart also has the **highest amount of Additions**, with almost all of them having the potential of being useful at some point in the game. While some Additions deal high damage and give only a small amount of SP (such as **Volcano** or **Moon Strike**), others give high SP at the cost of lower damage (**Burning Rush** and **Madness Hero**). His 4th Addition, **Crush Dance**, can be seen as a solid all-around addition. Having by far the **longest play time** of all characters, Dart has the potential to master all of his Additions and unlocking his Final Addition **without the need of any specialized Addition grinding**, and is **almost guaranteed to reach Dragoon Level 5** during the regular course of the game as long as the player is able to pull off his additions consistently.

Dart's **Fire Element** can also be seen as beneficial, considering that a **decent amount of enemies in the game are of the Water Element**. This comes particularly in effect when using his Dragoon form and Dragoon Magic, which is simple yet effective. **All of his magical spells deal damage to single or multiple enemies with no further effect**. Given his decent Magical Attack, Dart can deal high damage as a Dragoon, most notably with **Final Burst**.

Overall, Dart has no outstanding strengths, but no noteworthy weaknesses either. He shines the brightest when good use is made of his **Fire Element and Dragoon form**, and is arguably the **most consistent and least polarizing character** of the cast.

### Additions

> Main article: Additions
>
> Dart has the **largest amount of Additions** among the cast. > His Final Addition is the **third weakest (or fourth strongest) of the cast at a maxed 450% damage multiplier**, but provides a **very respectable 150 SP** at the highest level.

## Dragoon

### Red-Eye Dragoon

#### Acquiring the Dragoon Spirit

> _Young dart — The young Dart finds the Dragoon Spirit in the ashes of Neet._

Dart is the game's **first playable Dragoon along with Rose**. He **originally has his spirit with him at the start of the game** but is given access to it **after the battle at Hoax**. As Dart and Lavitz struggle against Kongol, Rose arrives and calls forth the spirit of the Red-Eyed Dragon. **Dart bursts with the mystical energy of a Dragoon and knocks out Kongol.** From there on, he has the ability to use his spirit.

#### Dragoon Transformation

To transform into his Dragoon form, Dart **crosses both of his arms** as the Red-Eyed Dragoon spirit activates in a **form of white light that bursts into flames**. His main outfit and weapon quickly transforms into his Dragoon form in flames as the **flames itself forming Dragoon wings on his back** and making his pose.

#### Dragoon Magic

> Main article: Dragoon Magic
>
> Dart's Dragoon Magic Spells of the Red-Eye Dragoon are **exclusively offensive spells with no further effect**. As Dart is the male character with the highest magical attack, Dart can deal high damage using his Dragoon Magic.

### Divine Dragoon

> _Divine Dragon Spirits — Dart transforms into the Divine Dragoon._

> WARNING: The following part contains spoilers.

> _Promo legend of dragoon cast — A promotional image depicting the cast in their Dragoon forms, showing Dart in his Divine Dragoon form._

#### Acquiring the Dragoon Spirit

The spirit is **obtained before the final battle of the game from Lloyd after the initial fight against Melbu Frahma**.

#### Dragoon Transformation

To transform into a Dragoon, Dart **carries the Divine Dragoon spirit on his left hand and raises it** to activate in a **flash of light**. His entire main outfit is covered in a **bulkier armor in a glowing yellow light** and forms **six wings on his back in a glowing purple light** as it flashes in white to reveal his Divine Dragoon form while **carrying both his Divine Dragoon Sword in a dragon-fang like blade motif and the Divine Dragon Cannon**.

#### Dragoon Magic

The Divine Dragoon and the Divine Dragoon Magic spells are of **No Element**. This, however, gives the Dragoon Magic the **unique property of dealing double damage against enemies of all other elements with the exception for non-elemental enemies**, greatly enhancing its power (see Element for more details on how Elements work).

## Trivia

- According to the **Official Guidebook**, Dart is **23 years old and 178cm (5'10") tall**. _(Note : 178cm ≈ 5'10", wiki LoD dit 5'8" — fandom plus précis pour la conversion)_
- If left idle, Dart has **two poses** he will do :
  1. Folds arms around chest, leans slightly, taps foot
  2. Leans slightly, puts hand on hip, taps foot
- When **dispirited**, Dart lets his sword hang freely and **slouches forward with knees bent slightly**.
- When Dart is in **Critical Health**, he leans **down on one knee** and lets his sword hang freely.
- When **defending**, Dart holds his sword **horizontally in front of his face**.
- Dart is seen **transforming into his Red-Eye Dragoon form 3 times outside of a player-controlled battle** :
  1. **First scene** : first transformation as a Dragoon to **defeat Kongol** before he escapes
  2. **Second scene** : blocks the **vengeful elder Bardel Brother's magic**
  3. **Third scene** : failed attempt to strike on his father **Zieg, who is possessed by Melbu Frahma**, who however overpowers him with one blow
- It is **unclear whether Dart realizes that Haschel is his grandfather**. However, before the final battle against Melbu Frahma, **Haschel told him about how he looks like Dart when he was young**. Dart nods, although it is disputable if this gesture implies that he knows and realizes about his grandfather.
- **Dart is the only character with 2 dragoon spirits and 2 types of element in the entire game.**
- Dart was going to be in **PlayStation All-Stars Battle Royale as a DLC** alongside Abe from the Oddworld series. Character artist Mike Edwards even created a 3D model for the game (He published the renders in his website in June 27, 2013) but developers **canceled Dart due to low sales**.
- Dart's **early field model had orange armor and long boots**.
- If the player uses a **cheat device to obtain both the Divine Dragoon Spirit and Red Dragoon Spirit**, **Dart only uses the Divine Dragoon Spirit**.
- In some early screenshots, Dart's sword looks different. Its handle appears gray and the yellow pattern on it looks completely different. The interesting part is that **this sword actually appears in the finished product**. It can be seen very briefly during Dart's Divine Dragon transformation sequence when he holds up the spirit. In said screenshots, his arms and legs looks way more blocky as well.
- Dart makes a **cameo appearance in Astro Bot**.
