**Endiness is the continent where the story of The Legend of Dragoon takes place. The history of the continent spans more than 11,000 years, where different species dominated the continent.**

---

## Geography

> _Official artwork map of Endiness._

### Mainland

Endiness consists of **three major countries** : **Serdio in the east, Tiberoa around the center, and Mille Seseau in the north**. West of Tiberoa, there is a **desert region with arid climate known as the Death Frontier**, and in the northwest lies an **ancient and now uninhabited country called Gloriano**. South of the Death Frontier, there is a **larger peninsula, however, it is not visited during the game and has no name on the official map of Endiness** ⚠️ NEW canon.

### Oceans and borders

South of the mainland lies a **vast ocean with the Aquaria Coral Reef**, which **continues into the "Straits of Walter"** to the west, **separating the mainland of Endiness from the Broken Islands** in the west. North of the Straits of Walter and the Broken Islands, to the west of the Death Frontier, lies an ocean called the **"Rudra Bay"**, which connects to the **"Fourtos Ocean"** in the northwest, a grand sea border spanning around the entirety of northwestern and northern Endiness.

To the northeast of Mille Seseau lie the **"Straits of Merzis"**. These again continue to the South into **Illisa Bay**, another large ocean region bordering on all three major countries of Endiness. The southwestern part of Illisa Bay, in the triangle between Tiberoa, Mille Seseau and the Death Frontier, is visited by Dart and his group throughout the game in CD 2, as are the **Islands at the heart of Illisa Bay, Suncrest Island and Prison Island**.

Apart from Illisa Bay, these oceans and borders, although visible on the official map of Endiness, are not mentioned in the game. The **southern ocean with the Aquaria Coral Reef is transitioned when travelling from Fletz to Rouge**.

### Unexplored and remote regions

> _Eastern regions of Endiness, with locations called Seilnder, Nissel and presumably Lidalio._

To the west of the main continent, separated from the mainland by the Straits of Walter and Rudra Bay lie the **Broken Islands**, a series of islands with presumably thin civilization ; a notable village on the islands visited in the game is **Rouge**. To the east of Serdio is **a continent or region connected by a forest path to Seles**, which is **possibly the region where Dart had traveled to before returning to his home**. Places named in this region are :

- plains called **"Nissel"**
- a passage between mountains called **"Seilnder"**
- a mountain region presumably called **"Lidalio"** ⚠️ (fandom variant — wiki LoD dit "Vidalio")

> _The northeastern, remote areas of Endiness, with regions called Azeel, Edinoro and Losta._

In the most northeastern parts on the map of Endiness, a mountain chain can be seen going into the oceans east of the Straits of Merzis, with the most outer mountain region called **"Azeel"**, another being called **"Edinoro"** ⚠️ (fandom variant — wiki LoD dit "Ediroso") and plains between called **"Losta"**.

**None of the eastern and northeastern regions are visited throughout the game.**

## Serdio

> Main article: Serdio

During the time of Dart's return to Serdio, the country is **split up into the Kingdom of Basil located in the north and Imperial Sandora in the south, as the country is currently in a civil war**. It has a **temperate climate and is rich in resources, which is the cause for the country's diverse culture**. This is the main area explored in the first chapter of Dart's adventure.

### Seles (Serdio)

The **surrogate home of Shana, and the town in which Dart grew up**. It suffers major damage at the beginning of the game due to a surprise raid by Sandora and is subsequently under reconstruction.

### Forest (Serdio)

The forest outside of Seles and the **first dungeon of the game**.

### Prairie (Serdio)

Between the Forest, Hellena Prison, and the Limestone Cave, roads wind through a **usually peaceful grassland down to the sea**. It seems to have been habitated at least at some point, with sheds and fenced areas for keeping horses, and a **woodchopper coming back to the location during later parts of the game**.

### Limestone Cave (Serdio)

The path to Bale became infested with the **serpent Urobolus** and prevented knights of Basil from strengthening the eastern front until it was defeated by Dart, Shana and Lavitz.

### Bale (Serdio)

> _Bale, the capital of the Kingdom of Basil._

Capital city of Basil and Lavitz' home town. This is where **King Albert, ruler of the Kingdom of Basil and rightful heir to the throne of Serdio, resides**. **Canals run through and under the city and connect with secret passages there**.

### Hoax (Serdio)

Close to the border of Sandora and Basil, it became the **western front line in the Serdian War**. It used to be a normal town with **large walls to protect the people from wild animals**, however, the civil war turned it into a fort.

### Marshland (Serdio)

Between Hoax and Volcano Villude, a **humid forest area rich in wildlife, as well as monsters**. Within the Marshlands, the **Seventh Fort, an outpost of the Kingdom of Basil**, is found.

### Volcano Villude (Serdio)

Winding through canyons filled with smoke, soot and ashes, the only paths are along sheer cliffs above the burning lava of Volcano Villude and **that lava flows down to the Dragon's Nest**.

### Nest of Dragon (Serdio)

This forest is full of poison, in the air, land and water, causing plants to mutilate and grow in irregular ways. The poisoning of the land is caused by the **Green-Tusked Dragon Feyrbrand's presence**. Despite of this, there is a **pure water spring in the inner area of the dragon's nest**.

### Lohan (Serdio)

> _The center of Lohan, the merchant town._

A **politically neutral merchant town** with all kinds of exotic wares in the bazaars, and a pulsating center of commerce. It is here that the **Hero Competition to determine the strongest adventurer of Endiness** contest is held.

### Shrine of Shirley (Serdio)

A majestic shrine in the mountains and **the only known location in which supposedly the legendary Dragoni Plant grows**.

### Hellena Prison (Serdio)

Located in Sandora and governed by the brutal **head warden Fruegel**, Hellena is an **infamous hell-on-earth prison where prisoners of war and other prisoners are supposedly being fed alive to a monster**.

### Kazas (Serdio)

> _The Black Castle in Kazas._

The capital city of Sandora. In the Black Castle, Emperor Doel resides. Compared to locations in the rivaling Kingdom of Basil, the **citizen have a notably worse life quality, with some even living in a makeshift underground fort**. However, the Black Castle is shown to use very innovative magical technology, such as **purple flames to power elevators and pulleys**.

## Tiberoa

> Main article: Tiberoa

Tiberoa lies west of Serdio and roughly at the center of Endiness next to the Death Frontier. Its ruling family consists of **King Zior and the Princesses Emille and Lisa**. It has an **arid climate** and only a portion of the country is inhabited, and most of its people have **dark skin and are fond of astronomy and astrology** canon ⚠️ NEW. This is the main country explored in the second chapter, together with Suncrest Island and Prison Island in the Illisa Bay.

### Fletz (Tiberoa)

> _Fletz, with the Twin Castle in the background._

The capital of Tiberoa and home of the **Twin Castle, where the royal family resides**. A lively town with beautiful buildings and shops, **rich in symbols of astronomy and astrology**.

### Barrens (Tiberoa)

A **dry and arid desert devoid of any inhabitants or greenery**.

### Donau (Tiberoa)

Also known as the **Flower City**, this pleasant town lies at the **northern coast to Illisa Bay**.

### Valley of Corrupted Gravity (Tiberoa)

A **desolate Valley with gravity irregularities, supposedly caused by the events of the Dragon Campaign over 11,000 years ago**. Entering it requires the **allowance of the King of Tiberoa**.

### Home of Gigantos (Tiberoa)

Once the **homeland of the Gigantos, a human-like race with gigantic body builds**, this **abandoned place has become a hideout for bandits** during the event of the game.

To the **west of the Home of Gigantos lies the Death Frontier**.

### Lidiera (Tiberoa)

Lidiera is a **small village on the islands in Illisa Bay**. It is **connecting to Suncrest Island and Fueno via the Undersea Cavern**.

### Undersea Cavern (Tiberoa)

An **underwater cavern that connects Suncrest Island, the Prison Island and the small island with Lidiera**.

### Prison Island (Tiberoa)

A **small and uninhabitated island in Illisa Bay, only accessible through the Undersea Cavern**.

### Fueno (Tiberoa)

> _The town Fueno on Suncrest Island._

A **rather large town on Suncrest Island famous for its healing hot spring baths and a harbor big enough for the Queen Fury**.

## Mille Seseau

> Main article: Mille Seseau

Mille Seseau is the **large country in the north of Endiness**. Its capital is Deningrad, which houses an elaborate palace made out of crystal ; the **palace was formerly part of a Wingly city**. Mille Seseau has a **very cold climate, especially in the winter, and consists mostly of plains and snowy forests**.

### Furni (Mille Seseau)

> _Furni, the Water City._

Also called the **Water City**, this town lies **directly at the ocean and is traversed not only by foot, but also by boat rides through the water channels flowing through the entire town**. In contrast to the other parts of Mille Seseau, it appears to have a **more temperate climate**.

### Evergreen Forest (Mille Seseau)

A **large forest in central Mille Seseau**.

### Deningrad (Mille Seseau)

> _Deningrad, the capital of Mille Seseau, with the Crystal Palace in the background._

The capital of Mille Seseau, built around the **Crystal Palace, the remains of a former Wingly city**. Queen Theresa as well as the **four Sacred Sisters of Mille Seseau** reside here.

### Neet (Mille Seseau)

> _The remains of the tragic village Neet._

Once a regular town close to Illisa Bay, this town **suffered a tragic fate when 18 years before the events of the game, the Black Monster attacked and destroyed the entire village and killed the majority of its residents upon its pursuit of the Moon Child**. The village has since then **not been rebuilt, its ruins still remind of the dramatic events of the past**.

### Forest of Winglies (Mille Seseau)

> _The Forest of Winglies in Mille Seseau._

Many **surviving Winglies either fled to this place, or have since come here**. They have **built a closed, self-sufficient society, living a peaceful and anonymous existence apart from humans**.

### Mountain of the Mortal Dragon (Mille Seseau)

A **volcano in the north of Mille Seseau, where the Divine Dragon was sealed in millenials ago**. **Many dragons still live here**.

### Kashua Glacier (Mille Seseau)

An **icy glacier that separates Mille Seseau from the western Gloriano**. **Giant icicle columns lead to large underground caves**, and even a **glimpse of the ancient past is hidden here, with the Tower of Flanvel being buried deep below its icy structures**.

## Gloriano

An **ancient holy land ruled by Emperor Diaz 11,000 years ago**, this land in the **northwest of Endiness is now a desolate snow desert under everlasting ice, although majestic ruins of the ancient days can still be seen today**.

### Snowfield (Gloriano)

A **snowy passage in the center of Gloriano, leading to Vellweb and Fort Magrad**.

### Fort Magrad (Gloriano)

The historical place where the **historical Emperor Diaz rallied his troops, the human liberation army and the Dragoons to the Dragon Campaign to end the oppressive reign of the ancient Winglies**.

### Vellweb (Gloriano)

> _A view over Vellweb, with the Spear Shooter, an ancient weapon, and the towers of the seven Dragoons visible in the background._

In the ancient days, Vellweb used to be **the capital of the humans in the lands of Gloriano**. At its center lie **seven tall towers, each dedicated to one of the original seven Dragoons**, who led the humans to freedom in the Dragon Campaign ⚠️ NEW canon — **7 towers design**.

## Death Frontier

> Main article: Death Frontier

The Death Frontier is a **desert region in the center of Endiness**. The **only known inhabitated location in this region is the Spring Breath Town Ulara, a Wingly city**.

### Ulara (Death Frontier)

> _Ulara, the Spring Breath Town._

Another **Wingly resort of the modern days**, a beautiful and oasis-like city called the Spring Breath Town. **Charle Frahma, the sister of the ancient wingly dictator Melbu Frahma, resides here** ⚠️ NEW canon — Charle Frahma = sister Melbu Frahma. The town is **not visible to travellers without using magic**.

## Ancient Wingly Cities

### Kadessa (The Forbidden Land)

> _The Forbidden Land, the ruins of the ancient Kadessa._

The **ancient capital of the Wingly empire under Melbu Frahma was its political and military center**. It is the **"Forbidden Land" now**, as it is a mere shadow of the past glory, and the **traces of the cruel and oppressive reign of the Winglies can still be witnessed here**.

It can be **assumed that the ruins of Kadessa lie somewhere in Mille Seseau**, as the **Divine Dragon flies above it throughout the events of the game**.

### Tower of Flanvel

> _The Tower of Flanvel in the depths of the Kashua Glacier._

Now half buried within the Kashua Glacier, this Wingly tower was once **both a fortress and giant mobile war machine, capable of moving in the skies**, and used in the suppression of non-Winglies according to the orders of Melbu Frahma and his **second in command, Faust**. Faust was **Commander of the Tower of Flanvel** when, like the cities of the Winglies, it floated in the sky. **Faust's tower was finally disabled by the Spear Shooter, a weapon designed by humans, based in Vellweb** ⚠️ NEW confirmed canon.

### Aglis

> _The Magical City Aglis below the oceans near Rouge._

Also known as the **"Magic City"**, like all Wingly cities it was once a flying city, and is now sunken under the sea. **Its magic seals out the ocean ; the whole of the cityscape lies in a vast bubble of air beneath the water**. The Wingly society **studied magical powers here, with the entire city of Aglis devoted to learning more about the principles and practice of magic**.

### Zenebatos

> _A view over Zenebatos, the Law City._

The **"Law City", where the Wingly society had an entire city devoted exclusively to the creation, revision, and enforcing of Laws**. The city was **run with the assistance of robots called Lapto** ⚠️ NEW canon. Many of the Laws were used to extend the Winglies' reign over other species. **Trials and execution as well as lawmaking were carried out here**. Even **11,000 years after the Dragon Campaign, the city is still partially functional, with oblivious Lapto still working on legal issues as in the ancient days**.

### Mayfil

> _The entrance to the Death City Mayfil._

Also known as the **"Death City"**, Mayfil was a **gathering to which souls traveled when they died, and which was supposedly the gate to hell**.

## Broken Islands

> Main article: Broken Islands

To the west of the Endiness mainland, separated from it by the Rudra Bay and the Straits of Walter, lie the **Broken Islands, a remote series of islands with presumably thin population**.

### Rouge (Broken Islands)

> _Rouge, the only village of the Broken Islands visited in the game._

A village in the eastern Broken Islands, where **residents focus on fishing as their day-to-day business**, however, it is also the **origin of the Rouge School of Martial Arts** ⚠️ NEW canon (Haschel lien probable).

## Divine Tree

> _The Divine Tree._

The **legendary tree which was said to have been sown by the creator Soa**. Out of all of its 108 fruits, all life on Endiness is said to have developed.

The **Divine Tree is visited in the finals of the game, however, its exact location in Endiness is not revealed**.

## The Moon That Never Sets

> _The Moon That Never Sets._

A **Moon not affected by day and night, always shining above the continent of Endiness**. It plays a major role towards the end of the game, when its **true meaning and connection to the creation of Soa is revealed**.

## Trivia

- At the **bookstore in Bale**, all the content misspell the continent as **"Endines"** ⚠️ typo canon
- In the story of the **third visit to Fletz**, there is a **geographical contradiction** when comparing the events with the official map of Endiness. Fletz, located at the **south coast of Tiberoa**, is on the border to a **large southern ocean with the Aquaria Coral Reef**, as it can also be found out from game dialogue when checking the **large telescope** on the left side before the entrance to the Twin Castle and when talking to **Kaffi** during the events of Disc 4. However, after Lenus escapes from the **Chamber of the Sun** in the castle to the ocean, **King Zior somehow concludes that she fled to Illisa Bay**, which however is located exactly in the opposite direction to the north of Tiberoa, with Donau located on its coast. From the map of Endiness, it is **more or less impossible to reach Illisa Bay from the southern ocean unless sailing around the entire continent on the western side**. → **Writer's mistake canon documented**.
- While having his courage tested on CD4, **Albert is warned of an "Eastern Tribe" that is attacking Serdio, though it is a false alert to test his courage to set priorities** ⚠️ NEW canon (Moon trial Aglis Disc 4).
- While trying to **calm down Syuveil who is about to meet his fate in death, Rose tells him of a story in the eastern lands about how they believe life doesn't have a beginning or end, it just revolves like a circle** ⚠️ NEW canon (Syuveil Vellweb scene Disc 4).
- Although the lands to the east of Serdio are neither visited nor mentioned in the game by their name, the game hints at them as the areas where **Dart traveled to before the events of the game**. On the world map, when leaving Seles, a **"dotted" path towards the east is visible**, and when using cheat codes to actually leave Seles from the direction Dart enters the city at the start of the game, the player is actually placed on this path towards the East. However, the path **just ends at some arbitrary point and leads to no area that can be entered**.
- In Fletz, as the group talks about a possible relationship between Dart and Haschel due to **Dart's mother and Haschel's daughter having the same name**, Albert cites some statistics: According to the **Royal Personnel Authority of Serdio, there are approximately one million people living in Endiness**. About **550,000 are women and about 4% of them, or 22,000, are named Claire** ⚠️ NEW canon — population statistic canon.
- The statistic above leaves some room for interpretation regarding the size of the cities visited in the game, as there is only a handful of larger cities in Endiness (namely **Bale, Kazas, Fletz and Deningrad**), a few medium-sized cities (**Lohan, Donau, Fueno and Furni**) and some smaller towns or villages (**Seles, Hoax, and Lidiera**), not counting cities and villages inhabited by Winglies. Assuming an urbanization degree of 50%, meaning that the large cities make up half of the population of Endiness, each of them would require an average population of over 100,000 people to be consistent with about a million inhabitants, however they appear much smaller in the game.
