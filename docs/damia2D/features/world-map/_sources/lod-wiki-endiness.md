# Endiness

**Endiness** is a **large continent in the Tesfer Realm** whose history stretches back over **11,000 years**. Based on the visible world map, **Endiness is a peninsula anchored to land in the east while otherwise surrounded by the Fourtos Ocean and the Aquaria Coral Reef**. There are also **six lands to the far east that are only seen on the world map** : **Azeel, Ediroso, Losta, Nissel, Seilnder, and Vidalio**.

## History

### The Age of Winglies, more than ~11,000 years ago

**Initially the world was completely barren and devoid of life before Soa planted a seed into the earth from which the Divine Tree grew**. Its **108 fruits each bore a unique species to populate the world**. From the **107th fruit came winglies**, who held supremacy over all other forms of life **even going so far as to suspend the birth of the 108th species**. Born with both **intelligence and vast magical power**, they were capable of **wondrous feats**. While there were those who wished to **live as equals with other races**, many winglies **held no regard for the lives of slaves and treated them as little more than playthings**.

The oppression of winglies led humans to found the **Holy Empire of Gloriano as a seat of rebellion**. Yet open conflict would not begin until humanity discovered that the **power of Dragons crystalized at the moment of death and could be taken at that critical moment**. Known as **Dragoon Spirits**, these powerful artifacts granted those who they recognized the power to transform into a **Dragon Knight, a Dragoon**. Armed with the **power of seven Dragoons**, the humans and other enslaved races fought against their oppressors in what would come to be known as the **Dragon Campaign**.

### The Dragon Campaign, ~11,000 years ago

Over the course of the war the **back wash of magical power laid waste to Gloriano, changing much of the landscape into a deadly desert that would later be known as the Death Frontier**. Similarly, a **large island to the south was ripped apart, transforming it into the Broken Islands**. The climax of the war came when the **Dragoons assaulted the Royal Capital Kadessa, and sent it crashing to the ground**. While most of the Dragoons lost their lives, the wingly dictator was defeated resulting in the surrender of the remaining winglies. As a show of good will, the **surviving winglies granted the humans three potent magical artifacts before hiding themselves with magic in fear of further hostility or annihilation**.

### The Age of Humans, Present Day

Once humans came to power, they **divided the continent into four regions**. While the Death Frontier was left untouched, the other three territories became the **present day countries of Serdio, Tiberoa, and Mille Seseau**. Although destroyed, **Gloriano is still noted on the north-west region of the World Map and also as a lesser title of Death Frontier**. One possible explanation is that **only the region that was reduced to a desert is referred to as the Death Frontier, while the northern territory remains Gloriano since it at least possesses ruins**.
