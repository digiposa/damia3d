## Trivia

- Due to a **memory leak in their Physical Attack Barrier ability**, **fighting one long enough will eventually result in the game crashing**.
- The **texture map for Lizard Man**, which is applied to the **3D model during battle, has secret text**. The **letters "D A K" appear on part of the image**. However, this **part of the image is not mapped to any part of the 3D model, making it a hard-to-find easter egg**.
