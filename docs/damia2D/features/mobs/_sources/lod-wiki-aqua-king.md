## Gallery / Trivia / References

### Trivia canon

- **Their model is a recolor of the Merman found in Marshland.**
- ⚠️ **This foe is one of two enemies in the entire game capable of using Magical Attack Barrier, the other being the Treasure Jar encountered between Lidiera and Fueno.**
- ⚠️ **Due to a bug, they possess no abilities which deal damage, rendering them trivial to encounter.** This can be observed by using Power Down on Aqua King, and noting that it often wears off before they take 3 turns.
