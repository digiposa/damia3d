## Hellena Warden (overview)

> File:Mob Hellena Warden.webp

**Hellena Warden is a Minor Enemy**. Several wardens have **custodial control of the day-to-day operations of the prison**. The wardens are **cruel and take pleasure in taunting prisoners with their impending fate of being fed to a monster**. They are also **expendable: their commanding officer Fruegel kills at least two of them on a whim**. After Dart, Lavitz, and Shana escape from Hellena Prison, **a squad of Hellena Wardens pursue them as far as Prairie**.

Although all instances of Hellena Warden are **visually identical**, there are actually **three unique units with varying statistics and abilities**. They are identifiable, as :

- the **first appears only on the first visit** to Hellena
- the **second appears only within the fight including Fruegel**
- the **last appears only on the second visit** to Hellena
