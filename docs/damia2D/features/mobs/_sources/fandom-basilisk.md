Basilisk is an **Earth-element based monster** of which appears within **The Tower of Flanvel**. It is **immune to the Erase effect of Can't Combat Weapons, Total Vanishing and Demon's Gate**.

⚠️ **NEW canon "Erase effect" mechanic precise** :

- **Can't Combat Weapons** : Gladius / Brass Knuckle / Indora's Axe (Instant Death proc weapons)
- **Total Vanishing** : Attack Item canon one-shot (kills minor enemy direct, NOT Repeat Item)
- **Demon's Gate** : Attack Item canon one-shot (kills minor enemy direct, NOT Repeat Item)
  → Basilisk **immune** à tous "Erase" effects canon (instant kill mechanics).

## Appearance

An **arthropod-like draconic monster, similar in appearance to that of the dragon Feyrbrand**.

⚠️ Visual canon : Basilisk resembles Feyrbrand (Wind dragon Disc 1) — pattern asset reuse OR species lineage canon.

## Battle

Basilisk deals both **significant physical damage** and has the capability to **petrify a single target at a time**. Its **defence is quite high, equal to that of Madman** and its **health is second to none here**.

Essentially, it would do you better to **eliminate these first and foremost if coupled with Madman**, and if not then to go **all out and end the battle quickly**.

### Abilities (fandom canon names)

- **Munch** — Walks towards a single target and deals medium to high physical damage
- **Petrifying Glare** — Petrifies a single target upon hit with a given probability
- **Attack** — Approaches a single target and smacks them, dealing medium physical damage

⚠️ Munch (= wiki ~Triple Bite) — fandom canon name officiel. **Attack** listed separate from Munch (possible distinct basic attack OR same).
