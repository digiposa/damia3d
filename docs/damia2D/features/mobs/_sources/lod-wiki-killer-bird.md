## Trivia

- Their **model is a recolor of Vampire Kiwi located in Prairie**.
- The enemy ability **Ultrasonic is different each time it appears on the three enemies who can use it**. **Tricky Bat's version has a 50% chance to cause Confusion**, and **Screaming Bat's cannot inflict confusion and deals physical damage instead of Non-Elemental**.
