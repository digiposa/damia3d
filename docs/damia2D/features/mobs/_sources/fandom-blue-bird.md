Blue Bird is a **Unique Monster** that appears between the **Home of Gigantos and the Valley of Corrupted Gravity**. It can also **rarely appear between the Barrens and the Valley of Corrupted Gravity, but it seldom occurs**. It gives **no gold when defeated, but earns 1000 EXP** if the player manages to defeat it.

## Battle

The Blue Bird's attack **deals 10% of the target's max HP as damage**. All physical attacks to it deal 1 damage, and it has **4 HP**. It is **unaffected by magical attacks** and has a **high evasion**.

To defeat it, the **generic strategy for defeating Unique Monsters** by using the **Magical Stone of Signet**, **Speed Down** as well as **Speed Up** can be used. It is recommended to use characters with **high accuracy and equipment increasing hit rate**, such as the **Wargod's Amulet**, the **Sallet** and the **Long Bow**. Having characters with high speed also helps, so items such as **Dancer's Shoes** or **Bandit's Shoes** are useful as well.
