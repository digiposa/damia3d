Freeze Knight (**フリージングナイト, Furījingunaito, lit. "Freezing Knight"**) is a Water element creature with **high Magic Defense** which is located within Kashua Glacier.

## Appearance

This opponent **resembles a full armor set consisting entirely of ice**, with the exception being the fine detail of its body. Its **arms / hands are simply massively long icicles that it motions as if sharpening while in an encounter**. Its **shoulders and head are rather tall icicles standing far higher than that of any human**, and its **feet have 5 icicles all around the base, including one behind**.

---

## Battle

Main article: Battle

This opponent is more concerning, at least for Dart. They will **deal massive damage to Dart since he is Fire element, however, the reverse can be said as well**. These creatures are **fairly potent with magic** and their **melee stab deals average physical damage**, enough to annoy, they are **really bad to be left at critical health since they will spam Fatal Blizzard**, and what makes them frustrating most of all may just be the fact that they are **immune to all Status Ailments, and so you must vanquish them the old fashioned way**.

- **Icicle Extension** — Icicle arms extend to be several meters long lunging towards a single target dealing medium physical damage.
- **Spear Frost** — Several spears of ice rain down upon a single target dealing medium water element magical damage.
- **Fatal Blizzard** — A blizzard blows by dealing medium to massive water element magical damage towards all targets.

Do note that this creature is **immune to all Status ailments**.

**Encounter rate: Common**

## Drops

This creature drops the spell item **Fatal Blizzard with a rare probability of 8%**. This is a **powerful water spell item that can be bought for 20 gold in Kazas**.
