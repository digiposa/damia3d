Flying Rat is a Wind-based monster found in Evergreen Forest. It can drop an Angel's Prayer after battle. **It can cast a Water-based spell when it is at 25% or lower (red) health.** The Dark Elf or Forest Runner it is usually paired with are far more dangerous, with their Petrification Arrow and Wooing attacks.

---

## Battle

Main article: Battle

The Rat has 20% A-AV and may avoid attacks. **It has high Magic Defense, making Additions more efficient.**

- **Glide** — physical attack
- **Spear Frost** — Water single target
- **Escape**

### Battle pairing / formation

All battle formations in which you will encounter a Flying Rat:

- Flying Rat
- Flying Rat + Forest Runner
- Flying Rat x2 + Dark Elf
