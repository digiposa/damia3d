## Appearance

This monster is a **gelatinous being, composed entirely of mud while holding a humanoid form**. This is also the **third and strongest cousin in the slime family, above both Slime and Red Hot**. This monster is **slouched forward with its hands on the ground and its body dripping downwards**. They appear to be **frowning and are light brown all around**.

---

## Battle

Main article: Battle

Madman has **extremely high Attack**. It has the **highest health of all monsters in this location**. However, **both its physical and magical defences are a bit low, especially its magical defence**. Be **wary of its ability Mud Throwing which can inflict Arm Blocking upon hit with a 50% probability**. This will **prevent you from attacking for three turns**. This is the **only ability they do until critical health** so it is **recommended to attack relentlessly or guard often**.

Abilities :

- **Mud Throw** — **Launches a mud pile towards a single opponent dealing low physical damage with a given chance to inflict Arm Blocking upon hit**.
- **Body Slam** — **Slithers towards a single opponent and tackles into them dealing medium physical damage**.

## Drops

Upon defeat, this monster may **drop the item Body Purifier with a rare probability of 8%**. You can **purchase this item for 10 gold**. This item **may be useful here against Arm-Blocking, however, it still takes an average time of 25 minutes to obtain**. As always, this varies per person.
