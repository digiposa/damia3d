Magician Bogey is a creature haunting the Phantom Ship. It is a normal enemy in that it is vulnerable to Total Vanishing, the Magic Stone of Signet and similar effects, but the fights against it are unavoidable; i.E. it must be fought to advance the story and escaping from them is not possible.

Appearance
Its appearance is that of a purple legless ghost with a curved mustache, wearing a top hat, and wielding a black cane in its left hand. It makes an unsettling smile.

Battle
You battle this creature only twice in the game. The first encounter takes place when you open the chest in the second room down that same corridor, the second fight with it is when you open the chest to the far left in the final room at the head of said hallway. In both battles its stats and attacks remain the same, just with different creatures backing it up in battle, the first being accompanied by two skeletons and the second against three Magician Bogeys simultaneously.

The battles with the Magician Bogeys should not be taken lightly, as it can cause bewitchment to party members. Using the Magical Ego Bell is recommended. The fight can be made much easier by buying some Dancing Ray attack items in the item shop of the Queen Fury, which will deal massive damage to the opponents, particularly when used by a character with a high magical attack such as Shana or Meru. Also, it is possible to immediately destroy it with Total Vanishing or Rose using Demon's Gate; this is particularly recommended in the third and final battle between them since it destroys them all instantly. Using Repeat Items such as the Magic Stone of Signed is recommendable as well.

The Magician Bogey uses the following attacks:

Cane Stab - Magician Bogey floats forward to strike target with cane
Flower Gift - The Magician Bogey floats up to target and takes off top hat to have flowers spring out of the hat. This can cause the Bewitchment status
Black Bats - Takes off top hat and bats fly out, attacking target and causing the Stun status

Drops
Magician Bogey drops the Magical Hat with the very rare probability of 2%. This is wearable by any character, tied for highest bonus to MAT with Dragon Helm and Legend Casque, and tied for second highest MDF bonus with Phoenix Plume. You cannot retrieve this item again until Aglis on Disc 4.
