Baby Dragon (ベビードラゴン, _Bebīdoragon_, lit. "Baby Dragon") is a **thunder element creature** of which is located within the **Mountain of Mortal Dragon**.

## Appearance

They appear as **short green dragons that stand on two legs, with very short wings and a tail**. Their head is **spikey** and their **stomach appears yellow, and armored**.

## Battle

Baby Dragon has **low health and magical defence** so using spells, **preferably attack-all spells since they usually travel in groups of three** would suffice. Their **element is Thunder therefore they have no elemental weakness**. They begin using their **tail or fire element breath attacks** which each deal medium damage at best. As they get weaker, they begin to use the **Status Ailment attacks Fear or Dispirit upon single targets**. In general it should be pretty straight forward, and you should be capable of defeating them quick enough to not regret it, however, you might bring some Mind Purifiers just in case.

### Abilities (fandom canon names)

- **Dragon Tail** — Flies towards a single target and slashes them with its tail
- **Anger of Dragon** — Breathes upon a single target, dealing medium Fire element magical damage
- **Cry of Dragon** — Roars upon a single target, dealing medium magical damage and inflicting Fear upon hit
- **Sorrow of Dragon** — Roars upon a single target, inflicting Dispirit on hit

⚠️ **"This creature is capable of evading attacks with a given probability"** — fandom mentionne evade. Wiki tier 2 dit A-AV/M-AV 0% — divergence à investiguer.

**Encounter Rate**: Uncommon

## Drops

This creature can drop the item **Mind Purifier** with a rare probability of **8%**. The item on its own isn't special and can be **purchased for 20 gold anywhere**, however, if you did not bring any with you on your journey here, they may prove to be beneficial.
