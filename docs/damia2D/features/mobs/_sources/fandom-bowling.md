## Appearance

Bowling looks like some sort of **humanoid automaton with body made of light-brown wood and gray stone**. Its **head is indistinguishable from the torso aside from a red eye**. It attacks using its **three arms like flails**.

## Battle

These opponents deal **decent damage, enough to be a game changer if they aren't alone**. Their **speed is high** too so it makes them somewhat dangerous, although they **die very quick in contrast**. A hit or two if you use good additions or have good gear / levels. Its **All-out Attack does hurt but if you defend once it says Charging Spirit, you should be alright**. They **cannot be defeated by using status ailments** which is quite unfortunate for those of you who rely on those tactics.

⚠️ **Strategy canon ⭐** :

- **"Die very quick" canon** — Bowling fragile en HP malgré DF 160 (= low real survivability vs additions/levels)
- **"Defend once Charging Spirit"** = mitigation strategy canon — Guard reduce All-out Attack damage
- **"Cannot be defeated by status ailments"** confirme all 8 ✔ immune (cohérent wiki tier 2)
