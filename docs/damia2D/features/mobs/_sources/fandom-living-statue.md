**Living Statue** is an **Earth-based monster** that **look like Stone versions of a Norman Sergeant**. It can be **found in Shrine of Shirley**. It **uses its spear to attack an alley**. It can also use **Power Up**, and is **immune to all status effects**. It is a **high-defense and low-hitpoints monster**. It may **drop Detonate Rock upon being defeated**.

## Battle

> Main article: Battle

The **Statue has high physical Defense and medium-low Magic Defense**.

- **Stone Spear**
- **Power Up** — **Attack and Defense stats are multiplied by 1.5 until the end of the caster's third turn**

### Battle pairing / formation

All battle formations in which you will encounter a Living Statue:

- **Living Statue**
- **Living Statue + Gargoyle x2**
- **Living Statue x2 + Strong Man**
