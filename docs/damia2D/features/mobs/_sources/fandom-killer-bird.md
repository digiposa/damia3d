## Appearance

It appears as some sort of **small bird incapable of flight with a rather large pointed beak proportional to it's body and with very large talons**. This creature is a **much stronger cousin to the Vampire Kiwi**. The **color scheme of each has changed from yellow fur and blue talons to purple fur and red talons**. Also **Killer Bird can do Ultrasonic, an attack Vampire Kiwi cannot**.

---

## Battle

This creature has **low health and physical defence, so you should be able to take it out in a hit, possibly two at most**. They are **not damaging with any of their three attacks** and even if one **recovered slightly with Blood Sucking**, you might defeat it in the next turn. These are **by far the weakest creature in the area and pose no great threat even to under-leveled players**.

- **Quick Pick** — Runs towards a single target and picks at them with its beak dealing **low physical damage**.
- **Blood Sucking** — Runs towards a single target and sucks their blood which deals **low physical damage and heals it by the same amount**.
- **Ultrasonic** — Makes an ultrasonic frequency from its mouth towards a single target dealing **low magic damage inflicting either Stun or Confuse on hit**.

## Drops

Killer Birds **drop the Panic Bell with an 8% probability**. This is a **very good item for vanquishing unique creatures such as the Rainbow Bird**. It is of **the easiest ways to gain the massive experience, gold rewards and or rare item rewards from them without the use of a Sachet**. It is **not recommended, however, to farm these considering you can buy them midway into the fourth disc where Rainbow Birds are still accessible**. If you want one it should take **roughly 30 minutes at that rate, however, it varies from person to person and their luck**.
