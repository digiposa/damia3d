**Gangsters** are **Earth creatures in Home of Gigantos**. They drop the **superlative Bandit's Ring**, and **Berserkers in the area drop Energy Girdle, a very tough Vest for Haschel**, much earlier than he might otherwise obtain it. **Piggies here drop the useful Sachet**. And **Rock Fireflies are available to regenerate the characters' health and MP for nothing**. This area **rewards a lot of patience and slow "farming" with very powerful items**.

Gangsters are **faster than usual**, with **average attack that when they are Powered Up is 50% stronger**, so they can output some decent damage.

## Appearance

Gangster is a **human opponent who wears red pants, has a top knot, and fights with the Rouge style of martial arts**.

---

## Battle

Gangsters are all **students to Gehrich and his teachings in the Rouge School style of martial arts**. They are the **elite part of his gang of bandits**. You first fight Gangster as a **scripted boss fight in front of the great door with two Crafty Thieves**, but once you defeat them this enemy will appear with Piggies throughout the Home of Gigantos. Though the **least common of creatures to spawn in battle**, it is still somewhat common.

- **Rouge Kick** : The Gangster **runs up and spin kicks the target**.
- **Power Up** : The Gangster **uses their chi to strengthen themselves**, raising their **attack and defense to 1.5 times normal for the duration of three turns**.

## Drops

Gangsters can drop the **Bandit's Ring** with a **very rare probability of 2%**. These rings can take **30 minutes to an hour and thirty minutes on average**; however, as always it will vary from person to person. Some people find it easy, others have a very difficult time with retrieving this item and spend several hours to maybe even half a day in getting these.
