## Description verbatim

Carlo is **King Albert's father** as well as the **brother of Emperor Doel**. Not much is known about him except that he was the **last king of the unified Serdio** until his **murder at the hands of his brother Doel**, that Albert believed he was a **good king loved by his people**, and that **Doel believed him to be weak and incompetent**.

His death marked the beginning of a new chapter for Serdio as it would be **split in half in a civil war that would last two full decades**.
