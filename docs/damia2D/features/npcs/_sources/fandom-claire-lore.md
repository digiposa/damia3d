## Identity canon

**Claire** was the **sole daughter of Haschel** and the **designated heir to the Rouge School of Martial Arts**. As the daughter of the head of the Rouge School of Martial Arts, she **underwent vigorous martial arts training by her father from a young age**.

⚠️ **Identity canon ⭐** :

- **Haschel's sole daughter** canon
- **Designated heir Rouge School of Martial Arts** canon
- **Vigorous martial arts training young age** canon
- Pattern thematic "successor heir burden" + "father strict teaching" canon

---

## Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow (Queen Fury flashback canon)

On board of the **Queen Fury**, Haschel remembers the events that led to **Claire running away from Rouge 25 years before the event of the game** ⭐.

In his memories, Haschel is shown to have been an **extremely strict teacher**, to the point of :

- **Refusing to be called "father" by her during training**
- **Insisting on being called "master" instead**
- **Yelling at her in dissatisfaction about her skills and attitude**

During a sparring match with a fellow martial artist from the village, **Lotta**, Claire **seriously injured or possibly killed her training partner** after being repeatedly criticized and yelled at by Haschel.

⚠️ **Lotta incident canon ⭐ MAJEUR** :

- **Lotta** = fellow martial artist Rouge village
- Sparring match → Claire seriously injured/killed Lotta canon
- Trigger : repeated Haschel criticism/yelling
- Pattern thematic "stress-induced violence" canon

An **outraged Haschel chased her away** from the scene, and Claire **left the village subsequently**. Her fate is **never fully resolved in the game**.

⚠️ **Claire's fate "never fully resolved" canon** — game doesn't directly state Claire = Claire Feld (Dart's mother).

### Quote canon Haschel Queen Fury ⭐

> "I shouted out of anger. I was just running away from the pain that we have to overcome together, as a Master and as a Father. It's been 20 years since I left the village pursuing Claire, but what I have found was only my immaturity."
> — Haschel

⚠️ **Haschel pursuit timeline canon ⭐** :

- **20 years** Haschel pursuing Claire post-village departure
- 25 years pre-game Claire ran away → **5 years post-departure Haschel started pursuit** (à confirmer interpretation)
- OR : Claire ran 25y pre-game + Haschel pursued 20y at game time (5y gap difficile à expliquer)
- Pattern Haschel "immaturity" self-realization canon = arc redemption Disc 1-4

## Chapter 3: Fate & Soul (Furni lullaby canon)

In **Furni**, it is shown in Haschel's memories from **27 years before the events of the game** that Claire also had a **sweet and motherly side**, as she **composed and sang a lullaby in her teenage years she planned to devote to her future child** ⭐.

⚠️ **Lullaby composition canon ⭐ MAJEUR** :

- **27 years pre-game** : Claire teenage years (cohérent 25y pre-game running away age = teen)
- Composed + sang lullaby herself
- **"Devote to future child"** canon = Claire's maternal intention canon
- À cross-référer **Shana's Theme music canon** (= Claire's lullaby per identity connection)

During the events in Furni, this lullaby would **play a crucial role in Haschel finding clues about the fate of his daughter and her connection to Dart**.

⚠️ **Furni Disc 3 reveal canon ⭐** :

- Shana sings lullaby to mayor's child Fa
- Haschel recognizes Claire's lullaby
- Dart knew lullaby (childhood)
- → **Trigger Haschel awareness Dart connection** canon

⚠️ **Chapter 4 Moon trial canon ⭐** :

- **Haschel solo trial** Disc 4 Moon (cohérent Hero Competition individual trials pattern)

# Claire Feld — fandom lore (verbatim)

> **Source** : [The Legend of Dragoon Wiki (fandom) — Claire Feld](https://legendofdragoon.fandom.com/wiki/Claire_Feld)

## Identity canon

**Claire Feld** is the **late wife of the former Red-Eye Dragoon Zieg Feld** and the **mother of the protagonist Dart Feld**.

| Role                       | Hometown |
| -------------------------- | -------- |
| Dart's mother, Zieg's wife | Neet     |

| Age     | Species | Gender |
| ------- | ------- | ------ |
| Unknown | Human   | Female |

⚠️ **Claire Feld identity canon ⭐** :

- Wife of **Zieg Feld** (former Red-Eye Dragoon canon)
- Mother of **Dart Feld** (protagonist)
- Hometown **Neet**
- Species Human + Female + Age Unknown
- Status canon : **Late** (deceased)

---

## Chapter 1: Serdian War (Neet destruction backstory)

When Dart, Lavitz and Shana rest in the **Prairie**, Dart tells the story of the destruction of his hometown. **Claire Feld presumably died during Black Monster's raid on Neet 18 years before the events of the game** ⭐, **returning to try to help the villagers alongside her husband**.

The **deaths of his parents fuel Dart's quest for vengeance**, took him across the world and **separated him from Shana** before the events of the game.

⚠️ **Neet destruction canon ⭐** :

- **18 years pre-game** Black Monster raid Neet
- Claire Feld + Zieg returned to help villagers
- Both presumed dead canon (motivation Dart's quest vengeance)
- Dart separated from Shana pre-game post-tragedy

---

## Chapter 3: Fate & Soul (Mountain of Mortal Dragon flashback)

In the **Mountain of Mortal Dragon**, Dart remembers the scene of him and his parents fleeing Neet one more time. **Claire Feld followed her husband into the village with the intention to fight and defend her hometown**.

### Quote canon Claire Feld ⭐ MAJEUR

> "Your father is a strong person. You have that strength in you. So.... You can wait here by yourself, okay. I am going back to the village to fight. I will protect your hometown and your father. Live strong."
> — Claire Feld to Dart

⚠️ **Claire Feld final words to Dart canon ⭐** — confirme :

- Dart's father = strong canon (Zieg Red-Eye Dragoon)
- Dart inherited strength canon (foreshadowing Red-Eye Dragoon Spirit inheritance)
- Claire Feld voluntarily returns to fight canon (NOT just trapped)
- Pattern thematic "protective mother farewell" canon

---

WARNING: Major spoilers follow from here.

Though it is never revealed within the game what exactly happened in Neet, it is **revealed in Vellweb** that during the attack of the Black Monster, when Zieg Feld and Claire went back into the town to help other villagers, **Zieg used his Dragoon Spirit, likely with the intention of defending the village**.

Upon doing so, **Melbu Frahma, who transferred his soul into the Dragoon Spirit 11,000 years ago when Zieg destroyed his body at the end of the Dragon Campaign, was set free and took over Zieg's body** ⭐.

⚠️ **Melbu Frahma possession Zieg canon NEW MAJEUR ⭐⭐ MAJEUR** :

- 11,000 years ago : Zieg destroyed Melbu's body (end of Dragon Campaign canon)
- Melbu transferred soul into Dragoon Spirit (Red-Eye Dragoon canon)
- Sealed soul awaited reactivation
- **Neet 18y pre-game** : Zieg used Dragoon Spirit → Melbu set free → took over Zieg's body ⭐
- Pattern canon "Zieg Feld = Melbu Frahma possession vessel" post-Neet
- → Zieg final antagonist Disc 4 = Melbu possessed Zieg canon

Afterwards, **Zieg, under the control of Melbu Frahma, fled the scene, leaving Dart, a five year old orphan, wandering through the ruins of Neet**. As the only trace of his parents, **Dart finds the Red-Eye Dragoon Spirit from his father in the ruins of the town**.

⚠️ **Dart age 5 Neet canon ⭐** :

- Dart was **5 years old** at Neet tragedy (cohérent timeline 23-18=5)
- Wandered ruins alone
- Found **Red-Eye Dragoon Spirit** from Zieg in ruins → inheritance canon

⚠️ **Claire's exact fate** is **unknown and never revealed**, although there is little doubt that she died that day. It is **unknown how she died** :

- In the fire ?
- At the hands of the **Black Monster** ?
- Or even perhaps at the hands of **Zieg, who by trying to use his Dragoon Spirit was unwittingly taken over by Melbu Frahma** ⭐

⚠️ **3 possible canon causes Claire Feld death** :

1. **Fire** (Neet burning)
2. **Black Monster attack** directly
3. **Zieg under Melbu's control** ⭐ — tragic implication canon

Pattern thematic dark ambiguity canon : potential filicide via possessed husband.

## Chapter 4: Moon & Fate (Mayfil soul canon)

Later in the game, there is a possibility that **Dart meets Claire's soul in the Death City Mayfil**. He is approached by a **bright light** and when he reaches out for it, he realizes it's a soul, saying it feels both **familiar and warm**. After saying, **"We will stop dad"** ⭐, the soul then **heals the entire party**. It remains in the center of the room and becomes a **healing point**.

Though **not directly confirmed**, it seems to be hinting that the soul Dart speaks might be that of his late mother.

### Quote canon Dart Mayfil ⭐

> "This soul, I think I know it. This warmth...it really brings up some memories. We will stop dad. Wait for me."
> — Dart in Mayfil

⚠️ **Mayfil Claire's soul canon ⭐ MAJEUR** :

- Dart possible meeting mother's soul
- Bright light → soul (familiar + warm)
- Quote "We will stop dad" ⭐ — Dart aware of Zieg Melbu connection canon
- Soul becomes healing point (gameplay mechanic + narrative)
- Pattern thematic "mother's soul aids son" canon Disc 4
