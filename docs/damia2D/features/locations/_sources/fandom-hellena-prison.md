**Hellena Prison** (**ヘルライナ監獄, Heruraina Kangoku**) is a prison on the **southern coast of Serdio**. A **hellish place, feared throughout Serdio**, it is known for **cruel treatment of all prisoners and that those who come in, never come out**.

During the events of Disc 1, Hellena Prison is **visited by Dart and his companions twice**.

---

## First Visit

### Story

When Dart arrives at Hellena Prison, a riding merchant makes his way towards the prison gate. Dart quickly hides behind a tree, and waits for him to pass. As he gets closer, he notices that the cart is stopped and about to be inspected by a prison guard. Dart hops into the cart and is able to sneak in.

The merchant, however, finds him and immediately calls for a **Hellena Warden**. After the guard is defeated, Dart explains he intends no harm. The merchant then offers to keep quiet and sell Dart some of his supplies.

Once he is inside and out of the sight of any guards, he begins his search for Shana. Making his way through the prison, he comes across another warrior, **Lavitz Slambert, the head of the First Knighthood of the Kingdom of Basil**, trying to escape from the prison. After a quick clash, they find out that they are on the same side, fighting off further Hellena Wardens. Afterwards, Dart explains his situation to Lavitz, who decides to go with Dart, declaring that it will be hard for one man to survive Hellena alone.

Dart and Lavitz eventually come to a large room strongly guarded. They find the **key to the Prison Tower on the second floor**, in the room's upper section. While investigating the area near Shana's cell, they are caught by a large group of guards and a battle ensues. After the fight, Shana calls out, with Dart hearing her voice. Dart enters the cell as Lavitz stands guard and he and Shana have a intimate moment of reunion. As they exit the cell, more guards arrive and another fight begins.

**Shana, insisting of being an adult now, picks up a bow and insists on fighting alongside Dart and Lavitz**. As they seek to exit the place, they get in a fight with **the head warden of Hellena Prison, Fruegel**. **Fruegel explains that the orders to capture Shana were issued by the Emperor Doel of South Serdio, but he added the order to destroy Seles**. After a harsh fight, they emerge victorious but are unable to kill Fruegel. They quickly mount a few horses and ride off towards the Prairie, and Fruegel orders his men to give chase.

At this moment, the **Man in Hood appears and expresses his disappointment about Fruegel's failure**. Fruegel vows to bring Shana back, while the **Man in Hood tells to himself that despite of this, everything is going according to his plans**.

## Second Visit

### Story

> WARNING: Major spoilers follow from here.

After finding out in **Lohan after the Hero Competition that King Albert was taken prisoner in Hellena**, the group charges into the prison to free the king. They work their way upwards to a heavily guarded room, where the **Wardens set up a trap to drop them into a cave where they are supposed to become food to the monster Jiango**. After the battle, Shana finds an opening to a passage back up and the group heads toward the top of the Building to stop Fruegel from killing Albert. Once at the top, the group fight Fruegel again with his two pets, **Rodriguez and Guftas**.

> The Man in Hood steals the Moon Gem from Albert's body.

The group finally **kills Fruegel, with Lavitz delivering the finishing blow**. However, at this moment, the **Man in Hood steals the Moon Gem, a national treasure of Serdio, sealed inside the body of Albert**. **Lavitz transforms himself to a Dragoon to come to his king's help, but is struck by the Dragon Buster and falls to the ground**. **The Hooded Man reveals his identity to be Lloyd and flees**. Dart rushes to Lavitz, who entrusts everything to Dart, **as he dies in Dart's arms**. **The Dragoon Spirit of the Jade Dragoon is then passed on to Albert**, who thanks his best friend.

Together with Albert, the group makes a return to Seles, where they uncover a shelter near the center of the village to have an undisturbed conversation about the events that happened. Albert expresses his deep gratitude to Dart and his group, and reveals the story of the **Moon Gem, the national treasure of Serdio, being sealed inside the body of the royal heir**. They assume that **Lloyd, who was employed by Albert and worked a double agent for Sandora, stole it for Emperor Doel to become the legitimate king of Serdio**.

Although Albert, the legitimate heir to the throne, is still alive, he has no residence nor troops anymore, but as the group of legendary Dragoons, they decide to **head straight to Emperor Doel in the Black Castle in Kazas to end the war directly**. Dart and his group find new resolve and vow that **Lavitz' death will not be in vain**, and Albert is determined to personally take up his lance to end the war.

Lastly, a cutscene at the Black Castle shows a conversation between Emperor Doel and Lloyd, where it appears that **Lloyd has a deeper agenda and no intentions to hand over the Moon Gem to Doel**, and **warns the emperor to not be caught in a "red fire" as he goes off**.
