The **Death Frontier** is a **desert full of sand pits and tunnels**. It is **formerly a part of Gloriano**, and is located **southwest of Mille Seseau and west of Tiberoa**.

## Services

### Save Points

- In the very first area that contains the entrance to the first cave system.
- At the first oasis.
- At the second oasis.

### Rest Areas

| Type       | Price    | Effect                                 |
| ---------- | -------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Cave Water | **Free** | Fully restores HP and MP for the party |
| Oasis      | **Free** | Fully restores HP and MP for the party |

> **Cave Water** is only accessible by **falling down the sandpit** just before the transition to the second half of the desert.
> There are **two Oases**, both of which are in the **second half of the desert** and possess a nearby Save Point.
