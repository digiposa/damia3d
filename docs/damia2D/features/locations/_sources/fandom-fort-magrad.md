Fort Magrad is a location near the **Snowfield of Gloriano** which can be **visited as an optional sidequest on CD 3**. **Rose's memories of the past are invoked here, telling more of the story of the Dragon Campaign.**

## Appearance

Once a **glorious fortress for the Humans during the time of the Dragon Campaign**, the fort is now nothing but **standing ruins and eternally frozen over**. Now **uninhabited by people**, wild animals and creatures have taken to the area as a home. There are still **malevolent spirits that reside deep within this location**.

## Story

### Before the events of the game

**11,000 years ago, the leader of the Dragon Campaign, Emperor Diaz of Gloriano, declared war upon the Winglies with a large army and the seven Dragoons at this very location.**

### Chapter 3: Fate and Soul

In the Snowfield, Dart and his group find **a stone with glowing writing in the languages of Humans, Winglies and Gigantos**. **Meru, Dart and Kongol each recognize the script of their respective races**, and Dart asks Rose for help reading it.

> "The cursed land where the decline of the Winglies began. Nobody is allowed to touch the door. **The judge Nomos of the court of Zenebatos.**" — The sign before the entry to Fort Magrad

As the group progresses through Fort Magrad, **Meru playfully throws a snowball at Dart**, whereas **Rose is in deep thought and remembers scenes from the past**. At the point where you can see the arena from the hill top, **Rose has a memory of Emperor Diaz rallying the tens of thousands of troops for the Dragon Campaign**.

- **Diaz**: "What we desire is freedom!! Or give us death!!"
- **Liberation Army of Humans**: "God bless Diaz!! God bless Gloriano!!"
- **Diaz**: "I am to bring light to the Humans' future! I am to pass judgment on the past of the Winglies! I am Diaz!!"
- **Liberation Army of Humans**: "God Bless Diaz!! God Bless Gloriano!!"

Upon entering the arena, **Rose walks towards a stone and kneels before it**, remembering Emperor Diaz speaking with **Zieg about her engagement to be married to Zieg after the war of liberation**.

- **Diaz**: "There is no guarantee of coming back alive."
- **Zieg**: "Even if one of us dies, our bonds of affection are forever."
- **Diaz**: "Your eyes are already looking at our future. I will realize the future for you."

> _The Polter Armor awakes._

As Rose stays kneeling there, Dart's questions about Rose pile up behind her odd behavior at the Fort, and he is moved to remark: "**Sometimes... I wonder if you are the same Rose I know.**" Rose replies "**I am just me**", as ancient forces left over from the Dragon Campaign manifest: **A sword that had been pierced into a stone levitates hovers above the ground in front of them**. Unsurprised, Rose says **the spirits of those who died long ago have awakened**, and the fight with the **Polter Armor** begins.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

- Fort Magrad is an **optional location** that can be accessed after sliding down the slopes at the Snowfield by entering through the **gate in the rear area**.
- The area itself is **completely linear** and leads to a large square on a lower area. Upon entering the square, a cutscene will play and the **boss battle against the Polter Armor** will begin. **Saving before going onward is highly recommended**; just past the steps, a little closer to the center of the square, triggers a cutscene sequence that ends in an **optional yet difficult boss fight with Polter Armor**.
- The **Soul Eater** and **Repeat Item Smoke Ball** are rewarded for the battle; an **Armor of Yore and Panic Bell are found in treasures behind the Polter Armor**.
