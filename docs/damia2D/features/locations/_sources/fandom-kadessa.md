**Kadessa, or the Forbidden Land as it is now known**, was the **capital city of the Winglies in the ancient times** and their **political and military center before the Dragon Campaign**. It is **revisited by Dart and his group in their search for the Dragon Block Staff**.

The **broken shell of a city and the glory that was once a Wingly metropolis can still be glimpsed here and there**. A **mere shadow of its former glory**, the city is now nothing but **ruins covered with scars from the final battle of the Dragon Campaign 11,000 years ago**.

## Story

### Before the events of the game

**Kadessa was the political and military center of the Winglies during their oppressive reign over the world**. In the **Dragon Campaign, it was the last major Wingly city to be taken by Humans**, and **its fall marked both Human victory and an end to the Dragon Campaign**.

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

> **Legend of Dragoon — Cutscene 6 — Rose's Memory**
> _Rose remembering the final battle of the Dragon Campaign, which took place in the ancient Kadessa._

In a **flashback by Rose**, when she remembers Zieg **after being washed ashore near Lidiera together with Dart**, she **recapitulates the final battle of the Dragon Campaign in her memories, which took place at Kadessa**. In the battle, **a Super Virage fought against the Dragoons**, and her **fellow Dragoons fell one by one in the battle, most notably Kanzas, Shirley and Belzac**, as well as **Zieg, who slayed the Wingly dictator Melbu Frahma in a duel, but was simultaneously hit with a petrification spell by the Wingly and plunged into the depths**.

### Chapter 3: Fate & Soul

During the events of Chapter 3, the **group visits the Forbidden Land at direction of the Wingly Ancestor Blano from the Forest of Winglies to retrieve the Dragon Block Staff** in order to **fight the Divine Dragon that has awakened and released from its seal**. He **teleports them to the location from the top of the Forest of Winglies**.

> _In this arena, the Species dominated by the Winglies fought each other to the death for the Winglies' amusement._

Dart and his group make their way through the desolate shell of the ancient royal capital. Despite mere ruins over 11,000 years old, they find several **traces from the Dragon Campaign and the age of the Winglies**, such as a **colosseum, where oppressed species such as Dragons, Humans and Gigantos fought each other for the amusement of spectators**, much to the **distress and sadness of Meru**.

> "**I heard it from the Ancestor. Once upon a time, it was a coliseum. But it wasn't for regular fights that you won or lost. It was for matches to the death. Winglies would just enjoy the view from above...or fighters picked from among the dominated Humans, Dragons, and some Gigantos. I am the offspring of that kind of Wingly.**"
>
> — **Meru**

> _A room with symbolic drawings of species from the Divine Tree, where Dart and his group must enter in the order of their creation._

To this, **Dart and Albert encourage Meru and tell her that the sins of the past are merely a warning to not repeat them anymore**. The group proceeds and also finds a **room with symbols of different species born from the Divine Tree, in which the group enters the teleporters in their order of creation in order to progress**. After Dart and his group solve the riddle, they progress to an area where they find the **body of a Super Virage that itself fought in the final battle of the Dragon Campaign**. Despite being **massively mutilated, it awakes and attacks the Dragoons, who fight and destroy it for good**.

> _The Grand Jewel appears in the room with the Dragon Block Staff._

Eventually, Dart and his group arrive at the location where the **Dragon Block Staff** is. **Miranda comments about it looking unspectacular, but Rose emphasizes that its strength cannot be judged by its appearance and that it sealed the Divine Dragon for over 10,000 years**. As **Meru flies up to get it, suddenly its guardian, the Grand Jewel, emerges and forces the group to a fight**.

After the fight, the **Dragoons finally receive the Dragon Block Staff**. As they make their way back down, they suddenly notice a **massive shadow over the location, with the Divine Dragon flying over it and going on a vengeful rampage to Deningrad**. The group rushes back to the Forest of Winglies, where **Ancestor Blano immediately tries to teleport them there, however, he fails in doing so, as his magical power alone is insufficient**. However, **with the support of Guaraha and Meru's parents, who come to the aid, he succeeds in sending them to Deningrad**.

> "**We believe in Meru!! And her friends who Meru believes in too!!**"
>
> — **Meru's father**

Later during Chapter 3, **Lloyd reveals that it was in the ruins of Kadessa, where Emperor Diaz met with Lloyd to discuss the decline of magic and his plan to regenerate the world by releasing into it the 108th species from the Divine Tree**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

The **Forbidden Land is arguably one of the more complex and long dungeons** the player has to work through during the game.

- The player first has to **go through an outer area with geyser-like smoke coming out of the walls and a chest with a Mind Purifier** and will land at a teleporter that brings them up to the outer walls of the large ancient city. Following the path and teleporters there, the player will be transported into an inner area.
- To **acquire the Dancer's Shoes**, the player should use the teleporter on the left side and follow through, leading to the chest.
- To progress, the player should go right, walk under a pipe and then go outside again. Following the teleporter chain will lead back inside on the left side and to a door with a teleporter to the right.
- Using the teleporter will lead to the **coliseum area**. After the cutscene, the player must progress going through the coliseum and using the teleporters on the ramps. This will lead to an area with a **Healing Spot (a purple shining interactible platform) and the Saving Spot**. From there, the player can take **two routes**. **It is not possible to change the route after a certain point**.

#### Route A (Divine Tree Puzzle + Scarred Super Virage)

This route offers **more EXP because of the boss fight against the Scarred Super Virage**, but the player will **miss out on the Power Up Repeat Item when continuing through**.

- To pick this route, the player has to **head north from the Saving Spot**. This will lead to a **large room with six symbols on the wall representing species from the Divine Tree**. To progress, the player must **enter in the order of their creation**. **Counting from the left side, the order is 6 (an unknown species on which Rose comments it doesn't look intelligent), 1 (Gigantos), 3 (Minintos), 4 (Dragons), 5 (Humans), 2 (Winglies)**. Solving the riddle makes a teleporter appear in the middle.
- Using the teleporter leads to the **depths of the Forbidden Land**, where the group encounters the **Scarred Super Virage**.
- Using the teleporter behind where the Scarred Super Virage lied will lead to a **spiral-shaped room with a teleporter at the center that will lead to the room with the Dragon Block Staff and the Grand Jewel**. However, at this point it is **still possible to backtrack and go to Route B to obtain the Power Up item**.

#### Route B (Spinninghead Collision Encounter + Power Up)

This route can be accessed by using the teleporter right to the east of the Saving Spot. The player will land in a room where **five spheres on the floor spawn Spinningheads that go after the player and will force a battle with the Collision Encounter mechanic**. To progress, the player has to go to all **five spheres** and interact with them, to **deactivate the energy barriers around the teleporter in the middle of the room**.

- After using said teleporter, the player will land in a **location with floating platforms similar to the Valley of Corrupted Gravity**. After jumping onto the first platform, it is **recommended to not immediately jump off at the first stop, but to wait for it to float one floor higher, where the chest with the Power Up repeat item is found**.
- After obtaining the Power Up, the player can now go back down to the "first floor" and take the next floating platform towards the right side. The player then has to use two further floating platforms on the right side to drop down more. This will lead to a spot where a **chest with the Mind Crush is on the left and two floating platforms between**. To jump over, the player essentially has to **mash X quickly, as the platforms quickly drop down after jumping on them**.
- To progress, the player can fall choose to fall down at the spot and use the floating platform which is the furthest to the right, leading to **another teleporter and to the spiral-shaped room with a teleporter that leads to the room with the Dragon Block Staff and the Grand Jewel**.

## Trivia

- The **exact location of the Forbidden Land is not revealed**. However, it can be **assumed that it lies somewhere in Mille Seseau**, as after retrieving the Dragon Block Staff, Dart and his group **spot the Divine Dragon, as he flies above the Forbidden Land towards Deningrad whereas his large shadow becomes visible on the ground**. **Its location may be somewhere between Deningrad and the Mountain of the Mortal Dragon**.
- The location is **mostly referred to as "Forbidden Land" by characters in the game and the naming of the location for a save file**. Its **ancient name "Kadessa" is used at four occasions**: When **Ancestor Blano tells the story of it**, in the **flashback when Lloyd tells the story of him meeting with Emperor Diaz there**, when the **spirit of Belzac in Vellweb tells about the coming subjugation of Kadessa, unaware that these events happened 11,000 years ago**, and from **Lapto in Zenebatos warning of human intruders**.
- It is **assumed that the Forbidden Land is where Lloyd acquired the Dragon Buster**, as **Guaraha revealed to Meru in the Forest of Winglies that the Dragon Buster was sealed in the same location together with Dragon Block Staff but was lost**. In **Rose's flashback, it was shown that the Dragon Buster was the weapon Melbu Frahma himself wielded in his fight against Zieg**.
- The **Forbidden Land can be visited again after clearing it by going back to the top spot of the Forest of Winglies and using the teleport device**. It can be **left again by going downwards on the initial path the player arrives at**. **If the player tries to go there during the first visit, a dialogue will prevent them from doing so**.
