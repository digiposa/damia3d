Hoax is a **military fortress near the western border of Basil**. The **Eighth Knighthood, commanded by Kaiser**, is currently garrisoned there. **Civilians live there in addition to the soldiers**. It is on the **front line of the war, just north of Lohan, and adjacent to the trade route Marsa Road**. Dart and Lavitz are sent to Hoax in order to **respond to an impending attack, likely bolstered by a Dragon, by Imperial Sandora**.

## Story

Upon arriving at the "town" of Hoax, **Shana remarks that the town more closely resembles a fortress**. Dart speculates that the war changed it, and Lavitz confirms that its transformation was an **unwanted necessity**. He recommends proceeding immediately to headquarters to report to **Kaiser, the commander of Basil's Eighth Knighthood**.

Kaiser is waiting for a group inside of a **pub that has been converted to a war room**. He expresses his **condolences to Lavitz for the loss of the Seventh Knighthood**. Lavitz pledges that he will **honor the deaths of his troops by ending the war**. Kaiser agrees. Lavitz introduces Dart and Shana. Word has traveled fast as **Kaiser already recognizes Dart's name for his action at Hellena Prison**.

## Trivia

- Hoax is one of **three locations whose scenery can be seen during both day and night**, the others being **Fletz and Ulara**.
- Similarly, Hoax is one of **three locations that is seen in both a damaged and undamaged state**, the others being **Deningrad and various rooms within Vellweb**.
- The **game files contain an unused battleground scene in Hoax**. It depicts the town in an undamaged state during daytime.
