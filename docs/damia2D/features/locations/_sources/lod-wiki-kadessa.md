**Commonly referred to as the Forbidden Land**, Kadessa is a **ruin of a time forgotten by the world**. During the **time of the Dragon Campaign**, Kadessa was the **royal capital of Winglies; a palace where eternal prosperity was promised**. Home to monsters today, it is said that **an ancient relic lies within which could limit the powers of the recently-awakened Divine Dragon**.

## Trivia

- Several of the **Minor Enemies in this area seem to be inspired by the play "A Midsummer Night's Dream,"** as indicated by their aesthetic appearance. In addition, **one foe's name is Puck**.
- The **treasure chest containing the Dancer's Shoes can be accessed from across the gap** while standing on the platform south of it, despite the distance appearing too far.
- **All of the submaps in this location can be grouped by ID to possess identical encounter formations and chances** with others in their group. These grouping are as follows: **(393, 398, and 403,) (394, 399, 404,) (395, 400, 405,) (396, 401,) and (397, 402.)**
