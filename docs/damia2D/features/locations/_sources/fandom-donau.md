**Donau** (ドナウ), also known as the **Flower City**, is a **pleasant floral town in the north of Tiberoa**. At the beginning of Chapter 2, it is, however, **terrorized by the Gehrich Gang**, with both **people and flowers suffering from the infestation of Bandits**.

---

## Story

### Chapter 2: Platinum Shadow

#### First Visit

As Dart and his group arrive at Donau, a scene from before is shown where **Wink, a Sacred Sister of Mille Seseau, was cornered by bandits before she is rescued by Lloyd**. She thanks him and introduces herself, but **he shows no further interest in her and disappears after giving her his name**.

> _Dart meets Kate — Kate explaining her trouble to the party_

Dart and his companions are greeted by a **timid girl who mistakes them for bandits for their dirty clothing**. The witness the otherwise pleasant town infested by bandits, and upon entering **Kate's house** to the right of the entry to the town, Dart and his group hear that **her fiancé, Lynn, had went alone just a few days before their wedding day to convince the Gehrich Gang to leave the town**. He left her a letter and has not returned. She begs Dart and his group to save Lynn. The group agrees to help Kate and **shows Lynn's letter to his father, the mayor**, who tells the group that the **Gehrichs come from an area beyond the Valley of Corrupted Gravity**.

> _Meru The Dancer — The group meets Meru._

After talking to the mayor, the group heads down from the mayor's house and meet **Meru, a platinum-haired girl who chases away a bunch of bandits**. Meru is excited to meet Dart and his group and **wants to join their quest to rescue Lynn**, but the heroes are sceptical at first. **Dart ultimately accepts Meru as he recognizes her being serious about it**, as she tells the group about requiring the **permission from King Zior to enter the Valley of Corrupted Gravity**. Together with Meru, the group makes its way to **Fletz** to ask for said permission.

#### Second Visit (optional)

> WARNING: Minor spoilers follow from here.

Later, after Dart and his group defeated **Gehrich at the Home of Gigantos**, they **free Lynn and escort him back to the Barrens near Donau**. If the player chooses to follow Lynn to Donau, the group witnesses the **couple's wedding ceremony**.

#### Third Visit

After fighting **Lenus at the Twin Castle**, the group visits Donau once more to **board the Queen Fury** and pursue **Lenus to the Prison Island**. They meet **Commodore Puler, the ship's captain, and his assistant Kayla at the docks of Donau**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

#### First Visit

- After the scenes upon entering the town, the player should **first enter the house of Lynn and Kate** immediately to the right to hear Kate's story and receive **Lynn's letter**.
- The player must then visit the **mayor's house**, which is located in the **western part of the town on the upper floor** (the stairs up are on the left side of the screen).
- After speaking to the mayor and descending the stairs, the group will meet **Meru**. **Regardless on how Dart answers Meru's question, she will join the group**. The player can then head back to **Fletz** to get the king's permission to enter the Valley of Corrupted Gravity.

#### Second Visit (optional)

The **second visit to the town is entirely optional**. After the group rescues Lynn and escorts him to the Barrens, he makes his way to Donau. If the player follows him, the group will witness the wedding scene between Lynn and Kate. At the scene where Kate throws her flower bouquet, the player can **press X to make Shana catch the bouquet**. However, if the player is too slow, **Meru or Haschel will catch it instead**.

The **trigger for this appears to be Dart's dialogue choice in the bedroom after the first banquet at the Twin Castle**. If Dart **tells Meru that he does think she will look pretty in a wedding dress**, then she will catch it. If he says otherwise, **Haschel will agree that Meru "lacks that mature charm."** This latter option results in **Haschel later intercepting the bouquet** if the player skips the prompt to have Shana catch it, which **irks Meru greatly**.

If the player goes to the **bar in Donau after Kongol joins the party**, the **Mininto Mr. Pelpee** will be there. He will give the player a clue where to find the **Dragoon Spirit for Kongol** (it is found in **Lohan**), although this clue is not required to obtain it.

#### Third Visit

After the events in the third visit to Fletz, the player has to make their way to the **docks of Donau**, which are found in the **area behind the mayor's house**. Upon speaking to **Kayla**, they can enter the ship.
