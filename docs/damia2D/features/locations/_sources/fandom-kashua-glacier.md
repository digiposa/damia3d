## Story

### Chapter 3: Fate & Soul

#### Heading to the Tower of Flanvel

After **finding out about the abduction of Queen Theresa, Dart and his group give pursuit to Lloyd**, after **Miranda reveals that the Moon Mirror, the Divine Moon Object and national treasure of Mille Seseau, is stored in the Tower of Flanvel in the Kashua Glacier**.

Upon arriving, **Meru and Haschel complain about the bitter cold**, but **Miranda attempts to console them**. She goes on to **explain how, as a child, she used to go to the Glacier for solace** and reveals that **Queen Theresa is not her biological mother and how her actual mother abandoned her, and how Queen Theresa kindly took her in as if she was one of her own children**. After a pause, they then **proceed deeper into the glacier to chase after Lloyd**. The heroes **descend deep into the glacier by climbing down between ice walls and hopping from one pillar shaped chunk of ice to another**. Once they reach the bottom, the **Tower of Flanvel is ahead**, however, the group is **confronted by the monster Windigo**.

> "**Kongol feels. Monster angry. About we entered here. [...] Monster, don't know coldness. Monster only knows to kill. It wants kill us.**"
>
> — **Kongol**

#### Heading to the Gloriano Snowfield

After the events within the Tower of Flanvel, the **group continues on their journey to Vellweb and heads west towards the ancient lands of Gloriano**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

#### Heading to the Tower of Flanvel

- The **Kashua Glacier is a relatively linear area**.
- In the **second screen**, to go to the Tower of Flanvel, the player has to take the route that **leads downwards and "deeper" into the glacier**. Should the player go west over the small **"ice bridge"** on the left side and go north, **Miranda will stop the player and direct them back**, as the Tower of Flanvel is in a different location.
- In the **third screen**, the player meet the **two peddler brothers Segundo and Tercero** and can buy some Equipment or Items.
- On the **fourth screen**, the player must **descend along some ice pillars** and pick up some treasure. At the bottom of the area is the **Saving Spot**.
- The fourth screen leads to the Tower of Flanvel but **triggers the battle with Windigo**. After the battle, the player can simply progress to the Tower of Flanvel.

#### Heading to the Gloriano Snowfield

- After the events at the Tower of Flanvel, the player has to **return to the second screen** of the Kashua Glacier and **go west, by going over a small "ice bridge" to where a wooden sign is, then head north**.
- This will take the player **immediately to the world map towards the Gloriano Snowfield**.
