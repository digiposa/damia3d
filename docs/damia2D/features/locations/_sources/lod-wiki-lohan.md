**Lohan is a commercial city located in the southwest corner of Serdio**. **Hosting the Hero Competition in a central arena**, it appears to be the **largest city in Serdio after a civil war split the country into its northern and southern parts**. Described as a **place where everybody is welcome, even during wartime**, Lohan's **position as a trade hub keeps its shops stocked with rare and valuable items**. Below the smithy's shop the **Merchant Guild of Lohan offers a venue for those of all trades to discuss business while keeping, "the guiding principles of neutrality and fairness."**

## Story

Lohan is a **major hub for the story in the latter half of Disc 1**. **Dart, Rose, and Lavitz rush to Lohan after their fight with Feyrbrand**. **Exposure to the Dragon's Nest having made Shana ill**, the **party has to make their way to Lohan to get her medical attention**. **Shana remains in Lohan while the party searches for a cure**. After the **resolution of this plot, Dart joins the hero competition where he meets his old mentor Haschel, as well as the mysterious Lloyd**.

## Minigames

Minigames are **only accessible after the conclusion of the Hero Competition and each cost 1 ticket to play**. Although **most minigames award tickets when beating them, the initial cost of one ticket is not refunded, so winning "3" tickets only increases the number held by 2**.

| Name                             | Explanation                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    | Reward                                                                          |
| -------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Monster Conquest**             | Conquer as many monsters as possible before being hit by a ball and ending the game. Big monster takes 6 hits + small ones 1 hit + 1 environment props 4-8 random hits + monsters can lift props as cover + small monsters at top of tree prop knocked down with prop + 1 big monster + 3 small max + small=1pt + big=10pt + difficulty scales with score + big throws 2 balls at higher score | **None.**                                                                       |
| **Who is the Man**               | Correctly pick which of the three identical minintos has the pink bird hidden under his hat. Man shown at start + 3 quickly exchange places running around to obfuscate.                                                                                                                                                                                                                       | **3 tickets.**                                                                  |
| **What's Wrong With the Scene?** | Correctly pick the three objects in the room which are changed after the curtain falls. 10 possible objects always begin the same.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             | **3 tickets.**                                                                  |
| **Obstacle Course**              | Run to the ending platform without being hit by any of the obstacles before the score reaches 400. Dart can stop moving to duck underneath some obstacles, linear course can be completed without ducking at all.                                                                                                                                                                              | **3 tickets (score ≥201) / 5 tickets (score 101-200) / 8 tickets (score ≤100)** |
