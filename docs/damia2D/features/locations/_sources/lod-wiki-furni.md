Furni is a **southern coastal port allowing the import and export of goods between Mille Seseau and Illisa Bay**. Like many coastal towns fishing is a popular past time, but Furni is **unique in that the residents use boats to traverse waterways as opposed to having only streets**. Their homes and businesses appear to be **carved into massive fossilized corals that extend below the sea**, which may have been done to **distance themselves from the dangerous beasts that dwell in Evergreen Forest**.
