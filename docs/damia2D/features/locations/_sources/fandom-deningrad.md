**Deningrad** (デニングラード, Denningurādo) is the **capital of Mille Seseau** and the location of the **Crystal Palace**, from which **Queen Theresa** rules over the country. Deningrad is also home to the **Mille Seseau National Library**, where large amounts of knowledge on any topic can be found, including legendary tales such as Dragons, Winglies, Dragoons, and the **Black Monster**.

## Story

### Chapter 3: Fate & Soul

#### First Visit, First Scene

> _Mslibrary — Dart and his group enter the Mille Seseau National Library._

In their first visit to Deningrad, Dart and his group meet **Bishop Dille** in the church and hear about the story of the creator **Soa** and the **Divine Tree**. They speak to **Librarian Ute**, who invites them to the **Mille Seseau National Library**, where the group scatters to collect some knowledge about things such as Dragoons, Dragons, Winglies, Virages and the Dragon Campaign.

> _Legend of Dragoon - Cutscene 8 - History of the Dragoon Campaign_
> Librarian Ute sharing his knowledge about the Dragon Campaign.

> **There were 7 Dragoon warriors in the legend of the Dragon Campaign, dragoons reputed to be the incarnations of Dragons. With Dragons at their side, the dragoons fought boldly. However, in the age when the legend was reality, the Winglies dominated all, even the gods. The gods answered to dictator Melbu Frahma's prayers and granted him ultimate power; but, the power was abused. The earth of Saint Imperial Gloriano was scorched by the gods' fire and became ashen. The anger and sadness of the people was the fuel for the dragoons' cause. Then at last, they freed the gods. Without the gods' power, the Winglies perished and our age, the Human Age, began. Thereafter, the gods floated in the sky. For 11,000 years, they have been looking over the world as the Moon That Never Sets. That is the story.**
> — Librarian Ute

Upon revealing his past to Ute as a survivor from the tragic village of **Neet**, Ute tells a story about the **Black Monster** and recommends Dart to meet with **Luanna**, the **Second Sacred Sister of Mille Seseau**, who was also a survivor.

> **Why did the gods create him? The Black Monster... he appears in various legends, as a demon who rebels against and devours the gods, an evil plague that rejects peace in the world, the god that is the Moon That Never Sets. The Moon That Never Sets glows red every 108 years. It is a sign that a herald will be sent to earth, a herald is known to us as the Moon Child. The legend says the Moon Child revives the gods on earth and purifies the world. The Black Monster abhors, hates, and despises it, and kills the Moon Child. This sad karma is endless. This god stays forever in the sky, and the world is in never-ending chaos.**
> — Librarian Ute

#### First Visit, Second Scene

> WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

Outside of the Mille Seseau National Library, Dart and his companions have a last short conversation with Librarian Ute.

> **What are you going to do? Are you going to pursue that man, Lloyd? When you get time, go visit Neet. You cannot change the past, however, you can face it. By facing it, maybe you can find out where you are from and where you want to go.**
> — Librarian Ute

After Ute disappears back into the building, the group decides to wait for Rose and Meru. At this moment, however, Dart suddenly feels a shiver, leans over and starts gasping for air. **Albert confirms that he also feels something is wrong.**

Dart and his group meet again with Rose, who went off a while before, and who accompanied **Setie**, the **Fourth Sacred Sister**, and Second Sacred Sister **Luanna** from Neet back to Deningrad. Upon meeting and exchanging a few words in the hotel, however, it is at this very moment Dart nearly loses his sanity to his **Dragoon Spirit**, as the **Divine Dragon breaks free** from his millennials old seal created by the Winglies.

> **War is not in human nature. Humans fight by making themselves enter insanity. It is...the same for revenge. This is a tool to amplify the insanity. And this insanity is the source of the power of Dragoons, the Dragon Knight. Someday, something will happen, and if you cannot chasten your insanity, I wonder if you will be able to endure it, given the meagerness of your spirit?**
> — A voice inside Dart, repeating the words of Rose.

> _Signet Sphere Crystal Palace — The ominous "sealing chamber" where Shana is drawn to._
> _Shana in the Crystal Palace — The group giving some encouragement to Shana before leaving._

The Divine Dragon breaks out and **flies over Deningrad**, but only causes some minor damage. After these scenes, **Meru**, who went off as well to the **Forest of Winglies**, meets Dart and the others in front of the Crystal Palace.

Dart and his group are introduced by Luanna and Setie to **Queen Theresa** as well as the **First and Third Sacred Sisters** of Mille Seseau, **Miranda** and **Wink**. As Queen Theresa requests the help of the Dragoons to fight the Divine Dragon, Rose explains the need for an ancient Wingly weapon used to lower the power of Dragons, the **Dragon Block Staff**, and suggests that **Meru** might know something about the location of it. Meru reveals that it is in the **Forest of Winglies** in the north of the Evergreen Forest, and that Winglies actually were **not extinct**.

As the group sets out, Shana is **mysteriously drawn to a mysterious room with a spherical object next to the throne room** of the Crystal Palace, and **loses her power over the Dragoon Spirit of the White-Silver Dragon**, which subsequently **chooses Miranda as its new wielder**.

**Miranda reveals that the Crystal Palace used to be an ancient fort of the Winglies before the Dragon Campaign**, and the room is said to have been used in **a ceremony to seal something**. Without Shana, the group sets off to retrieve the Dragon Block Staff. Before leaving, the group gives some encouragement to Shana.

#### Second Visit

> WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

> _1792997-divinedragon11 — The Divine Dragon attacks and destroys Deningrad._

Although Dart and his group are successful in finding the Dragon Block Staff and are teleported back to Deningrad, they arrive too late, as the Divine Dragon goes on a vengeful rampage before they are able to face it off in a battle. It **destroys the majority of the town with its Divine Dragon Ball attack**, and then **destroys the Crystal Palace with its Divine Dragon Cannon**.

During the collapse of the tower's top parts, **a bright spherical light flashes and ascends towards the Moon**, as the **"sealing chamber" from before has been destroyed**. However, **Shana, Queen Theresa and the Sacred Sisters are miraculously unharmed**, as Shana filled the throne room in a mysterious bright light.

The city is now in ruins, however, a significant amount of the population appears to have survived the Divine Dragon's raid unharmed, although building such as the church, or the Mille Seseau National Library suffered major damage. Dart and his companions rush to the Crystal Palace and find **Setie and Luanna unconscious but unharmed**, and **Wink unharmed** as well further above. Dart is in distress as he sees that the chamber where Shana rested has collapsed, but Wink assures him that Shana is in the throne room with Queen Theresa.

> _Shana protects Queen Theresa in the Crystal Palace — Shana protects Queen Theresa with a mysterious, bright light._

In the throne room, Dart and his companions find the room engulfed in a **bright, mysterious light emitting from Shana**, and both Shana and Queen Theresa miraculously unharmed. Miranda is so relieved to see Queen Theresa well that she starts to cry, whereas Shana is in worry and unrest about her mysterious power, despite her bringing a miracle. In her distress, **Shana faints, and is brought to the hotel of Deningrad, where she rests**. Dart and his group set out to the **Mountain of Mortal Dragon** to confront the Divine Dragon.

#### Third Visit

> WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

> _Lloyd abducts Queen Theresa in the Crystal Palace — Wink bringing Lloyd to the Crystal Palace, unaware of his intentions._

After the events on the Mountain of Mortal Dragon, **Lloyd abducts Queen Theresa** on his quest to retrieve the **Moon Mirror**, Mille Seseau's **national treasure**, by using **Wink's trust** after saving her from the **Younger Bardel** to get into the Crystal Palace. Wink reveals that while she was on a patrol together with some guards to confirm the defeat of the Divine Dragon, she and her troops were attacked by the vengeful Wingly but protected by Lloyd. To treat Lloyd's injuries, Wink offered him to accompany her to the Crystal Palace, where, upon being demasked by Luanna, abducted Queen Theresa.

Miranda reveals that **the Moon Mirror is sealed inside the Tower of Flanvel in the Kashua Glacier**, with **Queen Theresa herself being the key of opening the chamber to access it**. Dart and his group venture towards the Kashua Glacier to give pursuit to Lloyd.

Later, after the events at the Tower of Flanvel, **Wink reveals that Shana, who rested in the hotel, is abducted to Vellweb by Emperor Diaz**, setting the final destination for the group during the events of Chapter 3.

### Chapter 4: Moon & Fate

> WARNING: Section contains spoilers.

In **Ulara**, **Charle Frahma reveals the actual nature of the Moon That Never Sets and the Signet Spheres**, solving the mystery surrounding the "sealing chamber" next to the throne room of the Crystal Palace and the bright flashing light upon the collapse of the upper part of the Crystal Palace. **The spherical object was actually a Signet Sphere from the ancient days binding the Moon That Never Sets. Setting free enormous amounts of magical power, the Divine Dragon destroyed the Signet Sphere in his attack on Deningrad.**

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

#### First Visit, First Scene

- Upon arriving in Deningrad, the player should head to the **church**, which is the large building on the right side over the bridge. After speaking to Bishop Dille, the player should speak to the NPC on the right side in the church, who is **Librarian Ute**. After speaking to him, the player can proceed to the **Mille Seseau National Library**.
- The Mille Seseau National Library is the large building on the left side in the area with the entrance to the Crystal Palace. Dart now has to **talk to all of his companions**, which will reveal several historical views on Divine Moon Objects, Dragons, Virages and Winglies. Eventually, Librarian Ute will tell a folklore about the **Dragon Campaign**.
- After the FMV, Dart will talk to Librarian Ute about being a survivor of Neet and will be taken to the **attic**. Librarian Ute will then tell the folklore about the **Black Monster**.
- After the scene, the game will shift to when Rose visits the village of Neet.

#### First Visit, Second Scene

- After the events with Rose at Neet and Meru in the Forest of Winglies, the group will meet Rose alongside Sacred Sisters Luanna and Setie at the entry to the city. They will go to the hotel together, when the **Divine Dragon finally breaks free**.
- Outside, the group will re-unite with Meru and will be taken to the Crystal Palace. The player can explore the Crystal Palace to find some treasure, or head straight to the **throne room**, where they will meet **Queen Theresa and Miranda**.
- After the conversations, **Miranda joins the group**, but Shana goes missing in a chamber next to the throne room. Upon finding her, she will **lose the power over the Dragoon Spirit of the White-Silver Dragon, which passes over to Miranda**. From there on, **Shana is replaced with Miranda**.

#### Second Visit

- After the events at the Forest of Winglies and the Forbidden Land, the player will immediately be transported back to Deningrad. The player must make their way to the Crystal Palace and go upwards towards the throne room. On the way the player will meet the three Sacred Sisters Setie, Luanna and Wink.
- In the throne room, there will be a scene with Shana and Queen Theresa. Afterwards, the player will be back in the hotel which is still operational. The player can then collect the Stardust in the city and in the remains of the Crystal Palace, and head to the **Mountain of Mortal Dragon**.

#### Third Visit

- After the events on the Mountain of Mortal Dragon, the player returns one more time to Deningrad. In the Crystal Palace, Setie, Luanna and Wink will tell the story about the **abduction of Queen Theresa**.
- The player can now go to the Kashua Glacier by exiting Deningrad towards the Evergreen Forest, but there will be a **new path available to the west towards the glacier**.
