Fletz is the capital city of Tiberoa, where King Zior and his daughters Princess Emille and Princess Lisa reside in the Twin Castle.

Fletz is a large city with a somewhat "oriental" appearance. Star drawings and symbols can be seen all over the place, as the people of Tiberoa, and particularly the ones residing in Fletz, have a fondness for astronomy and astrology.

## Story

### Chapter 2: The Platinum Shadow

#### First Visit

After ending the Serdian War by defeating Emperor Doel, Dart, Rose, Shana, Albert, and Haschel leave Serdio in pursuit of Lloyd. They arive in the Kingdom of Tiberoa at the capital city Fletz and discover that many of its residents have a fondness for astrology, astronomy and the stars. While the inhabitants seem friendly, they keep their doors locked in fear of the neighboring bandits in the Barrens. At the town's entrance, the party comes across a bridge named "Claire Bridge", discovering that his is both Haschel's missing daughter's name and Dart's mother's name. However, the group quickly dismisses the possibility of the two being blood related, despite Haschel joking about the possibility of some "secret connection" between him and Dart.

> _Princess Emille being disrespectful to the people of Fletz._

While exploring the downtown near the Twin Castle, the group witnesses Princess Emille in the streets, who, despite being rumored to be beautiful and graceful, mistreats the citizen and particularly a pregnant woman asking her to name her unborn child. This shocks Albert, who appears to have been excited to meet her.

> "Is, is this person Princess Emille? I heard that she was very sweet. Oh no... if this is a dream, please wake me up!" — Albert

At the town bar, they meet the town gossip Kaffi, who knows nothing about Lloyd, but suggests the should visit the astronomer Fester at the next house. After talking to Kaffi, in the evening, they visit Fester, who tells the group the folklore about the Moon that Never Sets and the Black Monster. Fester soon leaves to see Princess Lisa, his student and Princess Emille's younger sister. Curiously, in the evening, Dart and his group witness Bandits roaming the city. They meet Nello, a gardener whose goal is to fill the Barrens with green life. They stay for the night, with Albert showing particular interest in Nello's ideas. The morning after, they head north to the Barrens to continue searching for Lloyd.

> _Legend of Dragoon - Cutscene 4 - The Moon That Never Sets_
> _Fester telling the folklore about the Moon That Never Sets._

> "In astronomy, a moon that never sets canot exist. Stars travel the sky by catching the winds of time. But this moon is not affected by time. It never moves, day or night. That's why it's called the Moon That Never Sets. After counting 108 years, when the Moon That Never Sets glows red, a moon child descends upon the earth to fill the world with holy bliss. A poem of war, telling the fate given by the Moon. However, what we really get is the spawning of destruction and fear that is the Black Monster." — Fester

#### Second Visit

> _Dart and his group thinking about a way to enter the castle._

The group returns from Donau together with Meru to get King Zior's permission to enter the Valley of Corrupted Gravity and rescue Lynn and retrieve the stolen Dragoon Spirit. The group tries to convince the giant guard blocking the castle entrance, but are denied entry to the Twin Castle, especially after Meru attempts to force her way in. The group incidentally meets Fester in the Accessory Shop, and one more time in the downtown before the castle entry at the market square, and after talking to him at his home and telling their story, accept his offer to go to the castle together with him, as he has free access to it as the Governor and tutor of Princess Lisa.

> _Fester, Dart and Albert kneel before King Zior._

In the evening, the group follows Fester to the castle. They meet King Zior who grants them the permission to enter the Valley of Corrupted Gravity, though the speech from the king at first indicates that he cannot trust his own senses as he sees three Festers as Dart and Albert kneel before him alongside Fester, and must be reassured that both his eyes and his ears are operating correctly. When Dart speaks to the servants around the castle, they confirm that King Zior has also been behaving strangely.

> _Princess Lisa telling Dart and his group about the recent happenings._

When Dart and his group leave the castle, Princess Lisa's maid Libria stops them, adresses Albert as "your majesty" and brings them to meet with Princess Lisa, who used astrology to find out about Albert's real identity. Princess Lisa reveals that six months ago, she and Princess Emille went for a horse ride, where her sister fell from her horse. Princess Emille underwent a major personality change after that, although Emille was once deeply loved by her people. Princess Lisa also tells the group that the stars hint at a connection between Princess Emille's change of behavior and the Gehrich Gang that started to roam all over Tiberoa since about the same time. Lastly, she gives a warning from the stars to "never hand over the Moon", possibly referring to the passing of the Moon Dagger at her sister's coming of age ceremony which will soon be held.

After the conversation, Princess Lisa invites the group to stay for dinner and overnight in the castle. During the meal, Princess Emille behaves badly, speaking impolitely to the guests and Fester, but King Zior still praises her of her vividity in front everyone else. After dinner, the group begins to suspect that something is wrong with Princess Emille.

> "Haa Haa Haa! Isn't she a great eater! It seems when girls become 20 years old they develop quite an appetite! Oh, Fester? Would you introduce our guests to Emille?" — King Zior

After staying overnight in the castle, Dart and his group make their way to the Valley of Corrupted Gravity.

#### Third Visit

WARNING: Spoilers follow from here.

After the group hears from Gehrich that the Princess Emille currently acting in the Twin Castle is an imposter, and after freeing Lynn and retrieving the Dragoon Spirit of the Red-Eye Dragon, they head back towards Fletz, traversing the Valley of Corrupted Gravity and the Barrens again. Lynn says his goodbyes, and the group has the option to follow him to Donau, where they witness his wedding ceremony with Kate.

When Dart and his companions arrive at Fletz, they hear fireworks, indicating that the coming of age ceremony has already begun. They hurry to the castle and are warned by the giant gatekeeping soldier that a lot of unfamiliar new knights have been installed by Princess Emille.

> "[...] just asking doesn't mean you have an invitation. It's so packed you couldn't fit a penny in. Look. She suddenly got a lot of hanger-ons. There are so many new faces that I have never seen before. and according to rumor, they used to be bandits. See, bad attitude, right? They call themselves knights, but nobody can say anything because it's Princess Emille. Oh!! Don't tell anybody about this! And I'll let you in." — The Giant Gate Keeper

> _Princess Lisa tries to use astrology to locate her sister, but doesn't receive any information._

Upon speaking to the "Bandit Knights", the group decides they need to find the real Princess Emille as proof. The group sneaks through the fake guards and finds Princess Lisa in her tower and tell her the truth about the imposter. Princess Lisa tries to locate her sister using astrology but doesn't receive any information and decides to go with the group, but even with the young princess accompanying them, they are still unable to gain admittance to the ceremony.

> _The group enters Princess Emille's room and found it ransacked._

Together with Princess Lisa, the group sneaks up to the room of Princess Emille, which they find in disorder and signs of looking. The group splits up to search, leaving Meru, Haschel and Kongol outside. After touching the portrait of Princess Emille, Dart, Albert, Shana, Rose and Princess Lisa are teleported to a magical parallel dimension. Across an eerie void of twinkling lights they saw Princess Emille unconscious on a seat; a glowing bridge is summoned and they go over. Princess Lisa is unsuccessful in awaking her, but Albert tries again, telling her about the urgency and Tiberoa being at grave danger, to which the princess awakes. After being told about the happenings of the past six months, Princess Emille is determined to reveal the truth.

> _Albert and Princess Lisa waking up the real Princess Emille._

> "It seems I've troubled you a lot due to my absence. Let us go to the Chamber of the Sun. We have to let people know the truth." — Princess Emille

> _The fake Princess Emille reveals herself as Lenus._

The group and Princess Lisa take Princess Emille to the Chamber of the Sun, but they arrive just in the moment where King Zior is about to hand the Moon Dagger to the fake Emille. While the King is shocked that there are two Emilles, the imposter snatches the Moon Dagger from his hands and reveals she is Lenus. Dart and his group fight her and corner her, but she reveals herself as a Wingly, escaping through the window towards the open sea.

After the fight, Albert reveals his true identity as the king of Serdio to King Zior, who pledges his finest ship, the Queen Fury, under the command of Commodore Puler, to sail from Donau to Illisa Bay in pursuit of Lenus.

#### Fourth Visit

Although Dart and his group overcome Lenus and the Sea Dragon Regole on Prison Island, they eventually are unsuccessful in retrieving the Moon Dagger. After crossing the sea back from Fueno to the mainland of Tiberoa with the Queen Fury, they arrive in Fletz again, witnessing a scene where Princess Emille visits the people of the city. As the graceful, kind and elegant Princess she was known for, the townspeople immediately notice that their real Princess returned. Emille deeply apologizes for the deeds of her imposter and vows to the people to make amendments, but the people insist that she is not at fault. Then, the pregnant woman that was mistreated by the false Princess Emille during the group's first visit to Fletz approaches the real Princess, asking her again to name her unborn children, with Princess Emille happily doing so.

> "I have returned after a half year of sleep. My father, King Zior, wouldn't say it, but I heard that the evildoings of the sinner who feigned to be me has harmed our people in Tiberoa. I can say it is my fault. Because people were wounded by trusting someone they thought was me. I am determined to make atonements for this, even if it takes forever. [...] I owe you a debt of gratitude. I love this country. I love you people. Let us make this country continue to be a wonderful country!" — Princess Emille to the citizen of Fletz

As Dart and his companions see this scene, Albert is awestruck at the Princess' beauty and grace, and cannot wait to see her again. Back in the castle, they are greeted by King Zior as heroes and the special guests of Tiberoa for saving his country from the Dragon, the bandits, and making everyone happy again. He remarks that the Moon Dagger is a mere ceremonial tradition, and that instead of it, the royal will family will now pass down the story of the seven heroes who saved his country.

> "It will be passed down through the ages from person to person: 'The seven stars arose from the East to save the country of the king who lives in the Chamber of the Sun.' ..." — Princess Lisa

The king prepares a large feast to celebrate the heroic acts of Dart and his companions. The group splits apart and takes some time off, with Dart and Shana going to the bridge between the two towers of the Twin Castle. There, Shana confesses her true feelings to Dart.

> _Dart and Shana sharing an intimate moment at the Twin Castle._

> "I love you, Dart. Since I was little... It never changed, even when you went on your journey, Dart. Actually, I started to love you even more... Because while you were gone, I was thinking about you every single day... So, I don't mind being your baby sister. Because I can be with you everyday... That's just how I think." — Shana

Just as Dart comes close to Shana, the two are suddently interrupted by Fester, who enthusiastically tells them that they are famous in the whole country and looks forward to see them again at the banquet, then leaving hastly. As Shana and Dart get intimate again, suddently Lady's maid Libria interrupts them again, asking to gather everyone. Without any further conversation, Shana goes off to get everyone.

Dart looks around the castle to find his companions. He finds Kongol near the dressing rooms, doing some practice with his axe, while talking to himself of having found friends of different species.

> "Kongol. Has. Friends. Friends of different species. Not bad." — Kongol

In the bedroom of the castle, Rose is amused at herself being named a hero, and takes off a choker she wears and remarks that she did not laugh anymore since she put it on.

Near the dining room, Haschel and Meru tried to get some food beforehand, but are kicked out by the staff.

In the Moon Tower, where Princess Emille resides, she and Albert are talking to each other, with Emille being very enchanted by the conversations with the king.

After Dart gathers everyone, the women of the group gather in the dressing room on the direction of Maid Libria, who says that Princess Emille and Princess Lisa insist on them wearing formal dresses for the banquet. However, Meru declines the offer, saying that she prefers her regular clothes to dance, and Rose also declines saying that she would feel naked without her sword, leaving Shana as the only one to accept.

At the celebration, Dart looks for Shana to continue their previous conversation. He talks to all of his companions that are present, with Kongol not seeing her in the room, Haschel helping him to get out of a conversation with two curious ladies about the heroes' story, witnessing the developing romance between Albert and Princess Emille, and dancing with Meru. Maid Libria then approaches him, having been called by Rose, who is outside on the balcony. Rose scolds Dart for leaving Shana waiting for so long, and leaves the two alone.

> _Dart and Shana share their first kiss._

Dart finds Shana dressed up in her dress and talks to her in the moonlight before they spot a shooting star. They both make a wish upon falling star; Shana wishes for her dream to come true, and Dart says it will because he wished the same thing. The chapter ends with the two sharing their first kiss.

### Chapter 4: Moon & Fate

The group returns to Fletz one more time coming from the Death Frontier back to Tiberoa through an underground passage to the Home of Gigantos. They make their way to Fletz to board the Queen Fury one more time towards the southern ocean to Rouge. King Zior, Princess Lisa and Princess Emille bid farewell to them before they depart with the Queen Fury.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

#### First Visit

- After the arrival, the player should first head towards the Twin Castle, where a cutscene with Princess Emille plays.
- Afterwards, the player should go to the bar (upstairs in a relatively large building on the right side of the bridge in the front section of the city) and speak to Kaffi. After Kaffi asks whether Dart and his group are interested in the stars, the player should answer with "yes", to which Kaffi will recommend the group to visit Fester. This scene will cause the time of the day changing to the night, where the city cannot be left.
- Fester is met to the house right to the left side of the bar, where a large telescope is mounted on the roof. After hearing his story, the player can, if desired, take a glance at the Moon That Never Sets through the telescope.
- Afterwards, the player should go to Nello's house, the house with a garden next to it, to the left side of the bridge. Upon speaking to Nello, the group will stay overnight and can leave for the Barrens the following day.

#### Second Visit

- After the events of Donau and returning to Fletz together with Meru, the player can head to the castle, where entry is denied at first. The player should then head to the Accessory Shop, where Fester is out for some shopping. He will return to his home and suggest they should head to the castle together.
- The player can now follow Fester to the castle, where some cutscenes play. As the player tries to exit the castle, they will be stopped by Lady's maid Libria who directs them to Princess Lisa. After the scenes in the bedrooms, Dart has to speak to Shana, Haschel and Meru to progress.
- After these events, the player can now leave the castle and from then on is free to re-enter the castle at any time and head to the Barrens and to the Valley of Corrupted Gravity.

#### Third Visit

- After the events at the Home of Gigantos, the player can either follow Lynn to Donau for an optional scene of his wedding, or directly head back to Fletz. The player should first head to the Twin Castle.
- In the Twin Castle, the player will meet several unwelcoming "Bandit Knights". The player should head to the outside on the right, where they have to hide from the views of the Bandit Knights guarding the area. The player should make their way up on the tower on the right side, where Princess Lisa's chamber is. When spotted by a Bandit Knight, the player will be brought back to the exit to the outside and has to start over.
- After speaking to Princess Lisa, she will accompany the group. The player should now head to the left tower and evade the Bandit Knights again. In Princess Emille's chamber on the top, the player should examine the large picture, where they will be transported to a parallel dimension. Checking the "star-like" lights on the upper side will create a bridge. After the group wakes up Emille, they can now head down to the throne room. Being spotted by Bandit Knights will now cause Princess Emille to come out and the Bandit Knights to flee.
- After entering the throne room and the cutscenes, the player has to fight Lenus.
- After the battle with Lenus, the player should head back to Donau to board the Queen Fury.

#### Fourth Visit

- After the final battle against Lenus and Regole, the player should head back to the Twin Castle and the throne room again, where they will be welcomed by King Zior.
- After the cutscene between Dart and Shana, the player has to get the other group members that have spread all over the castle. Kongol will be found in the central area on the first floor near the dressing room, Haschel and Meru when approaching the door on the left side of the second floor in the entry hall of the castle where the dining room is. Albert is together with Princess Emille in her chamber on top of the left tower, and Rose is in the bedrooms accessible through the door on the right side of the second floor of the entry hall. Another scene will play where Lady's maid Libria directs the women of the group to the dressing rooms.
- At the banquet, the player has to speak to all group members present in the throne room. This will cause Lady's maid Libria to direct Dart to the outside, where the final scenes of Disc 2 play.

#### Fifth Visit (Chapter 4: Moon & Fate)

- After returning to Fletz through the Home of Gigantos, the Valley of Corrupted Gravity and the Barrens, the player should head to the Twin Castle.
- Upon talking to King Zior in the throne room, the player can now enter the two doors on the left and right side on the ground floor of the entry hall. This will lead them to a underground dock where the Queen Fury can be boarded by speaking to Kayla.

### Treasure

Both treasure chests are located next to each other in the bottom left corner of the training room inside the Twin Castle where two men are jogging up and down. A Moon Serenade and a Sun Rhapsody can be found in the chests.

## Trivia

- Fletz is the town with the most required visits during the game, with a total of five.
- Fletz is one of the only two locations in the game where there is a playable day and night setting (the other being Ulara in Disc 4). Only certain cutscenes change the time of the day, and after the respective events, the city will always be visited and played in the daytime.
- In the story of the Third Visit, there is a geographical contradiction when comparing the events with the official map of Endiness. Fletz, located at the south coast of Tiberoa, is on the border to a large southern ocean with the Aquaria Coral Reef, as it can also be found out from game dialogue when checking the large telescope on the left side before the entrance to the Twin Castle and when talking to Kaffi during the events of Disc 4. However, after Lenus escapes from the Chamber of the Sun in the castle to the ocean, King Zior somehow concludes that she fled to Illisa Bay, which however is located exactly in the opposite direction to the north of Tiberoa, with Donau located on its coast. From the map of Endiness, it is more or less impossible to reach Illisa Bay from the southern ocean unless sailing around the entire continent on the western side.
