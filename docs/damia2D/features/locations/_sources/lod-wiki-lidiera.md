**Lidiera** is a **coastal fishing village on an island between Tiberoa and Mille Seseau**. The buildings are **fashioned out of a ship wreck**, as they possess only a **single room** and have structures like **ship masts and a crows nest atop their roofs**. Additionally, they are all comprised entirely of **wood and tarps that were once sails**. While **lacking any businesses such as hotels, clinics, or shops**, the town does still possess a **Mayor**. Additionally, they have a **gated cave nearby that holds the town treasure** and a **mechanism that allows partial control of the Undersea Cavern's water level**.
