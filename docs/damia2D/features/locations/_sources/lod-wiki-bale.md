## Aperçu

**Bale** est la **capital de Basil**. Située au nord de Serdio. Architecture : brick & clay, style **European renaissance**. Streets bustling avec people, plein de services. **Indels Castle** dans la partie ouest, ses spires émergent au-dessus des waterways. Indels Castle = siège du gouvernement Basil dirigé par **King Albert**.

## Story

Dart, Lavitz, and Shana arrive in Bale following their escape from **Hellena Prison** with urgency. Lavitz even eschews visiting his mother, prioritizing his responsibility to report to King Albert. The trio makes its way through the city to **Indels Castle**. Upon presenting themselves in the throne room, Albert expresses his relief for Lavitz's return. **Minister Noish** points out that Albert was so overcome with worry that it was distracting him from his political duties. Lavitz reports on the destruction of his knighthood and recounts his experience in Hellena Prison. Albert offers Dart a favor in exchange for helping Lavitz, which Dart immediately capitalizes on by **asking Albert to lodge Shana in Indels Castle for protection against Sandora**. Shana protests, saying she wants to continue journeying with Dart. Albert informs Dart he cannot fulfill the request as **not even the castle is safe from Sandora due to their use of a Dragon in battle**. Dart warns Shana that their journey may become more dangerous, but Shana is content to stay with him.

## Collectibles

After bumping into **Martel** near the entrance to the town, Dart can find her in the house next to Lavitz's home and turn in Stardust to her. If at least **10 Stardust** have been given, she will reward Dart with the **one-of-a-kind Physical Ring**.

## Trivia

- A merchant comments that it is **impossible to travel to or trade with the City of Lohan because Marsa Road is closed**.
- Lavitz's mother will have different dialogue if the party leaves without spending the night. Normally she mentions having another meal again, but instead will say that although she knows he doesn't wish to worry her, she wishes Lavitz would visit more often.
