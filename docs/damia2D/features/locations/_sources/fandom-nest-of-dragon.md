**Nest of Dragon** (竜の巣, Ryū no Su, **"Dragon's Nest" or "Dragon's Lair"**) is the place where the heroes encounter **Greham and the Dragon, Feyrbrand**. **The forest has been poisoned as a result of the Dragon's presence.**

---

## Story

### Chapter 1: The Serdian War

After passing through **Volcano Villude** and entering the Nest of Dragon, **Rose senses that the atmosphere is infected due the Dragon's poison**, and the **trees around the area have mutated as well**. **Shana is sickened by the environment around her and requires a break, however she is soon ready to continue with their quest**.

The group proceeds to the **giant web but breaks through it**, falling into an **underground habitat** and discovering a **source of uncontaminated pure water**. Dart and his companions work their way up, **touching strange plants that retreat and set the path free**, and make their way to a larger hall.

> _Inspecting the Dragon's poison — The Dragon's poison contaminating the forest._

**Lavitz is shocked to see Greham there**, the **former head of the Second Knighthood of the Kingdom of Basil**, who **turned treacherous many years ago and killed Lavitz' father Servi**. Vowing to **revenge his father**, Lavitz confronts Greham, who then **transforms into the Jade Dragoon** and challenges the group, **summoning Feyrbrand to his side**.

> _Greham the Knight of Betrayal — Dart and his group meet Greham in the Nest of the Dragon._

> **It shouldn't be a surprise. Only the Dragoons can manipulate Dragons. Behind the Dragon, there should be...**
> — Rose to Greham, right before Feyrbrand appears.

Before the fight begins, **Rose asks Greham where he has the Dragoon Spirit from**, to which he reveals that **Emperor Doel received his power and intelligence from Emperor Diaz**. This leaves Rose in **entire disbelief**, as **Diaz was a historical figure that died over 11,000 years ago**. Greham, however, refuses to elaborate, and **challenges the group to a fight**.

Eventually, **Lavitz and the others defeat the Dragon and Greham**. **Greham, in his final moments, praises Lavitz for having the strength of his father, and shows regret about his own betrayal**. After he dies, the **Dragoon Spirit of the Jade Dragoon chooses Lavitz as his new master**.

It is then when **Shana collapses as the result of being exposed to the poison for too long**. The group **rushes to Lohan to find a cure for Shana**.

Later on, the group **returns to the Nest of Dragon one more time to collect pure water from the nest and purify the mutated plant obstructing the road to Shrine of Shirley**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

Upon entering the area, **Shana will feel sick from the Dragon's poison**. After the cutscene, the player can continue towards the Dragon's nest. In the section where there is a giant web on the ground, it is recommended to **first go near the stepping stone in the river and jump over the river to proceed to a chest containing a Chain Mail** for either **Lavitz or Dart**.

The player then has to **walk on the giant web, which will collapse and bring them down into the Dragon's nest**, where a **Healing Spot is found with pure water**. The Dragon's nest is a **two-floor dungeon** where the player can **climb plants to go up and down**. In order to proceed, the player has to **touch small umbrella-like plants on the upper floor**, which will **lead to other plants forming a "wall" to disappear**. In order to proceed to the **Saving Spot**, the player has to touch the **small plant on the upper left** (before the hole leading down to the Bravery Amulet) and on the **middle left of the upper floor**.

The player will then encounter **Greham and Feyrbrand**. After the battle, when **Shana feels sick again**, the player has to **climb up the large web and exit the area towards the north to proceed to Lohan**.

After the events in Lohan, the player will **return to the Dragon's Nest one more time to fill the pure water into a bottle bought in Lohan and use it to get rid of the mutated plant blocking the road towards Shirley's Shrine**, where Shana rested.

### Treasure

- **Bravery Amulet** : After taking a sip of the pure water to replenish your party, head straight up the column and turn to the right, climbing down another one. Once down, head down to the first opening on the right. Follow the passageway until you reach the room with the Save Point. Head over to the moving plant-like creature and touch it. Continue to follow the passaged way till you reach the Bravery Amulet.
- **Spirit Potion** : Now we must take the long trek back to the first entry way. Go ahead and climb up the column just to the right of the first entry way. Shortly after climbing out of the hole, you'll find another plant. Touch it and retrieve your wondrous Spirit Potion just to the left.
- **Body Purifier** : Continue the path, touching the plants as you go along and you'll reach your Body Purifier within 20 seconds of your last treasure.
- **Mind Purifier** : This trek won't take as long as the last, but, yet again, we must backtrack to move forward. Before leaving the way you came, head towards the left and move through the entry way to your last treasure, Mind Purifier.
- **Chain Mail** : Before the spiderweb that you fall through, go up to the rock in the middle of the stream and jump across and run back along the path till you reach the chest. **Not to be confused with the superior Plate Mail that is a rare drop from Greham**. **Reports vary, but Plate may also drop from the Man Eating Bud.**
