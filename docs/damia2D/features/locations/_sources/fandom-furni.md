Furni (**ファーニ, Fāni, "Farni"**), also called the **Water City**, is the **first location visited by Dart and his group in Mille Seseau**. It is a **peaceful town on the south coast of Mille Seseau**, bordering to **Illisa Bay**, with **water canals running through the city with bridges, streets and even houses built over them**.

## Story

### Chapter 3: Fate and Soul

> Dart and his companions approach Furni on the Queen Fury.

The **third chapter of The Legend of Dragoon, Fate and Soul**, opens on the prow of the Queen Fury with Dart and his companions watching as the **town of Furni draws nearer across the waters**.

> The mercenaries being briefed about Kamuy.

The Queen Fury docks at the **harbor of Furni, the gateway to Mille Seseau**. As the group explores the city in their pursuit of Lloyd, they stumble upon a **gathering of mercenaries**, as **Resident Knight Harris** informs them of the objectives:

1. **Hunting down a white wolf named Kamuy that has been rampaging in the Evergreen Forest**
2. **Finding a boy named Teo who went missing**

After hearing that **Dart's party restored safe shipping around Tiberoa by slaying the Sea Dragon**, the **man in the rent-a-boat freely gives them a licence to use the boat in the city**.

> "From Tiberoa? With that huge boat? [...] Well, wait a minute. One, two, three... seven!! Could you be the famous Dart and companions who **conquered the Sea Dragon in Illisa Bay**? [...] Hon! Let the Mayor know about this!! He'll be happy to hear about this! Here it is! **The License for the boat! Take it!** [...] I have to thank you! Thanks to you, we can go fishing and go to Tiberoa! If you need anything, just drop by! We have accommodations too! I'll give you a good price!" — Man at the rent-a-boat

As Dart and his group make their way around Furni, they visit the **mayor of Furni** who introduces them to **Fa, his daughter**. Fa, he says, **did not speak since she was allegedly attacked by Kamuy**, and on that same night, **Teo, who had kept Kamuy as a pet, disappeared**. Teo **ran away from the village**, says the mayor, as he is assumed to **feel guilty for hurting his best friend Fa**. Dart and company **agree to stay in the mayor's house for a night** before leaving to seek out Teo and Kamuy.

> "The wolf is becoming more savage every day. Now, he has been joined by monsters from the Evergreen Forest, and they attack people! [...] But it is more complicated than that. **The wolf called Kamuy that attacked Fa was kept by a boy of this village, until recently**. And the boy who kept Kamuy named **Teo has disappeared**! On the night Fa was injured, Teo disappeared from his home. Probably he feels responsible and ran away. **Teo has been Fa's friend since they were little. They are best friends**." — Fa's father, the mayor of Furni

> Dart and Shana looking at the Moon That Never Sets.

On that night, after dinner, **Shana is upstairs singing a lullaby to Fa**. **Haschel, on the stairs, overhears it**, and **remembers his daughter Claire singing the same tune** and explaining that she made the song for her future children. Shana explains that **the lullaby was sung to Dart as a child by his mother**. **Haschel is shocked at this and believes it was not a coincidence for him to be with the group, and decides to go out to get some air**, apparently **becoming well aware of his possible connection to Dart**, having found out in **Fletz before that his daughter had the same name as Dart's mother**.

Shana then joins Dart, who is **looking at the Moon That Never Sets on the top of the house**. They **reminisce about their childhood at Seles**. Suddenly, under the Moonlight, **Shana resonates with the Moon and suffers an acute headache, collapses and is brought back into the building by Dart**.

The next morning, Shana feels well again and the group leaves Furni. Crossing the canal and leaving the town, they **begin their venture into the Evergreen Forest to search for Teo and Kamuy**.

## Gameplay

### Walkthrough

1. At first, the player should head into the town where the **mercenary gathering around Resident Knight Harris** takes place. After the scene, the player should enter the building beforehand, which is **both the hotel and the rent-a-boat**.
2. Upon asked whether they belong to the warriors, the player must answer with **"No, we are not"** to receive the boat licence.
3. The player can now use the boat to explore the city and use the shops, and should proceed to the house of the mayor, which can be found when going **"To the upper right"** from the rent-a-boat and then using **"To the Mayor's house"** in the next screen.
4. When the Mayor tells his story, **it doesn't actually matter what answer Dart gives** on his request to help him out. After the scenes at the Mayor's house, the player can now leave towards the Evergreen Forest or **preferrably collect the remaining Stardust first**.
5. It should be noted that **finding Kamuy and Teo is an optional (but recommendable) sidequest**. When returning to Furni afterwards and talking to Resident Knight Harris, the player will **receive 500G as reward**. Harris can be found in his house right next to the hotel and rent-a-boat.

> ⚠️ **Note**: **Bulgus** from the mercenary horde is **blocking the path to the Evergreen Forest and to Teo's house if Dart has not met the mayor yet**.

### Shops

Both of the shops are located at the **canal and require the boat licence to use them**. The Weapon shop is found when using going **"To the left"** from the rent-a-boat, where it is possible to talk to the man at the left side who is the shopkeeper of the Weapon shop. The Item shop can be accessed when using **"To the front"** and then **"To the item shop"**. The building itself is **accessible by land as well, but no items can be bought** and only a short conversation with the shopkeeper can be held when entering by foot.

## Trivia

- Upon arriving in Furni, **Rose remarks that "there is the smell of blood around this place". It is never revealed what she actually refers to**, as no fighting takes place in Furni during the entirety of the game.
- The **warriors gathering around Resident Knight Harris use the same models as the competitors from the Hero Competition (Gorgaga, Serfius, Danton, Atlow) as well Drake the Bandit and Greham**. Of these warriors, **only Bulgus (using the model of Gorgaga) is identified by his name and speaks**.
- In contrast to the **cold climate witnessed around other places of Mille Seseau**, Furni appears to have a **more mild and temperate climate**, despite the majority of citizen dressing in a more packed, "Mille Seseau-ish" type of clothing.
