## Items overview

Items refers to **game elements useable with the "Items" command during Battle**, although several items are **usable outside of combat as well**.

---

## Attack Items

Attack Items are used **exclusively in Battles** to either inflict damage or Status Ailments to enemies.

### Magical Attack Items

Magical Attack Items deal **elemental damage to enemies**. Like Dragoon Magic, Magical Attack Items are **affected by the user's Magical Attack attribute**, but the **element of the character using the item has no effect** (e.g. a Fire element item like **Burn Out does not receive any bonus if Dart uses it, nor is it weakened if used by Meru**). Magical Attack Items **deal double damage when used against enemies of their opposing element and half damage when used against enemies of the same element**, with the exception of the **Non-Elemental Psychedelic Bomb, which deals double damage to enemies of all elements other than Non-Elemental, and is not resisted by itself**.

It should be noted that **"Powerful" items with no price or location cannot be bought anywhere, and sell for 20 Gold**. The **damage dealt by (Multi) items can be amplified by over 200% by rapidly pressing the X button during their animation**. Their **animation changes and intensifies up to three times if the player presses X fast enough**.

### Effect Items

Effect Items deal a Status Ailment to minor enemies. They are relatively rare and limited in their use, but can have good use against Unique Monsters that otherwise cannot be damaged, such as the **Rainbow Bird**.

> **Note**: It is disputable whether **Can't Combat, dealt to enemies by Total Vanishing**, is an actual Status Ailment, as the **Rainbow Earring, an Accessory preventing all Status Ailments, does not protect from Can't Combat**. However, for purposes of simplicity, Total Vanishing is listed as an Effect Item. Also, the Sachet does not inflict a Status Ailment, but is listed here as well.

| Item                | Price / Shop/Location   | Effect                       |
| ------------------- | ----------------------- | ---------------------------- |
| **Poison Needle**   | 20 — Ulara, Moon        | **Poisons minor enemies**    |
| **Stunning Hammer** | 20 — Ulara              | **Stuns minor enemies**      |
| **Midnight Terror** | 20 — Ulara, Hellena     | **Frightens minor enemies**  |
| **Panic Bell**      | 20 — Ulara, Rouge, Moon | **Confuses minor enemies**   |
| **Total Vanishing** | **400 — Arena (Lohan)** | **Destroys minor enemies¹**  |
| **Sachet**          | **sell only: 200**      | **Gives subtle good aroma²** |

> ¹**Total Vanishing has no effect against bosses or Unique Monsters**. Notably, **Total Vanishing can be used against Rodriguez and Guftas in the second fight against Fruegel, as these are not recognized as bosses by the game**.
> ²**The Sachet deals a fixed 10 damage to any enemy regardless of stats**. This makes it **very useful against Unique Monsters, as it destroys them in one turn**. It also has a **scripted effect against the boss Jiango, putting him asleep for several turns and making the fight easier**.

## Repeat Items

Repeat items are **special, unique items that are given back at the end of a battle and are one of a kind**. Many of them (such as the Magic Signet Stone or the Speed Down) are particularly useful against **Unique Monsters that tend to flee very quickly**.

| Name                   | Effect                                                       | Location                      |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------- |
| **Psychedelic Bomb X** | Non-elemental damage to all enemies (Multi)                  | **Aglis**                     |
| **Speed Up**           | **Increases ally's speed for 3 turns**                       | Valley of Corrupted Gravity   |
| **Speed Down**         | **Decreases enemy's speed by half for 3 turns**              | Mountain of Mortal Dragon     |
| **Power Up**           | **Ally physical and magical attacks increased for 3 turns**  | **Forbidden Land**            |
| **Power Down**         | **Decreases enemy's strength by half for 3 turns**           | Death Frontier                |
| **Magic Shield**       | **Nullifies magical attacks for 3 turns**                    | Snowfield                     |
| **Material Shield**    | **Nullifies physical attacks for 3 turns**                   | Black Castle                  |
| **Pandemonium**        | **Causes minor enemies to attack only one ally for 3 turns** | Hellena Prison (Second Visit) |
| **Smoke Ball**         | **100% chance of escape from minor enemies**                 | **Fort Magrad**               |
| **Magic Signet Stone** | **Blocks an enemy's movements for 3 consecutive turns¹**     | Marshlands                    |

> ¹This is essentially the **same effect as Stun, but it is not removed by attacking the enemy**.

## Trivia

- **Dabas's Shiny Bag**, and **Magic Shiny Bag are items with teleportation magic and they're exclusive to the Japanese version of the game**. In the **PocketStation game called "MoGuuRu Dabas"**, Dabas travels through tunnels in his basement and finds treasures. He then puts these treasures in his bag and teleports them to Dart's Shiny Bag (**This was the only way to make a decent amount of money in the Japanese version as it gives very little money from the battles**). These **"bags" are often mistaken as an inventory enhancement because of false information released in the strategy guide**.
- The **Letter from Lynn can be re-acquired from the Mayor of Donau's house after you leave it**. When you return, you will find it on his dining table where you can pick it up again.
